TESTIMONY

Testimony by Amanda Farias, Majority Leader of the New York City Council, on ULURP and term limits

0:05:54

·

7 min

Majority Leader Amanda Farias testifies on the importance of the City Council's role in the Uniform Land Use Review Procedure (ULURP).

She argues that ULURP empowers communities, particularly marginalized ones, and allows council members, who live in their districts, to mediate interests and secure community benefits, citing the Bronx Metro North plan as an example.

Farias contends that delays stem from pre-certification and financing issues, not council review, and opposes removing council oversight, suggesting instead improvements like community education and streamlining pre-ULURP processes.

She also advocates for extending council term limits to provide stability and continuity for long-term projects and effective governance.
