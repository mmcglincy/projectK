Testimony by Shane Moynihan on the need for a top-four open primary system

4:14:28

·

3 min

Shane Moynihan, cofounder of the New Leaf Democrats and a leader at the Forward Party, testifies in support of open primaries but strongly advocates for a top-four system over a top-two system.

He calls the arguments against open primaries "hollow," suggesting they are made by people who have gained power in the current system and don't want to give it up.

He argues that a top-two system does the reverse of what is needed, as it trims down options and makes it harder for independent and third-party candidates to break through.

  • Moynihan dismisses arguments that open primaries would destroy grassroots power.
  • He strongly advocates for a top-four system, arguing that a top-two system reduces options for voters.
  • He believes a top-four system would provide more competition and hold officials more accountable.
  • He states he would campaign "full force" against a top-two proposal.
