Testimony by Candis Tall from SEIU Local 32BJ on land use proposals

1:05:42

·

164 sec

Candis Tall, Executive Vice President and Political Director of SEIU Local 32BJ, testifies on behalf of the union's 90,000 members in New York City.

While supporting the goal of more affordable housing, she expresses opposition to the commission's current land use recommendations.

She argues that the proposals to fast-track housing construction fail to include important democratic checks and would remove the public's ability to weigh in on large projects.

  • Tall states that SEIU 32BJ does not support allowing affordable projects to be approved by unelected bodies like the Board of Standards and Appeals or the City Planning Commission.
  • She argues that community board hearings are not meaningful if the local, democratically elected council member does not have a say in the final approval.
  • The union opposes awarding developers a 30% density bonus without any check by the local council member.
