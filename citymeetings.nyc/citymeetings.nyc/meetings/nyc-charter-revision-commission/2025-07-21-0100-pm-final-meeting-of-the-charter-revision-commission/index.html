NYC Charter Revision Commission

Final Meeting of the Charter Revision Commission

Jul 21, 2025

·

01:00 PM

55 min

Summary

A vote.

The commission approves five ballot proposals for the November election:

  • Fast-tracking affordable housing
  • Simplifying review for modest projects
  • Establishing an affordable housing appeals board
  • Creating a digital citywide map
  • Moving local elections to presidential years

The commission also approves the report containing these proposals and recommendations for future charter revisions.

Features closing remarks from all the commissioners.
