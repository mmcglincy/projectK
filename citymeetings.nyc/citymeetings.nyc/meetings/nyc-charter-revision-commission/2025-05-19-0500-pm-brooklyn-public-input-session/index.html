NYC Charter Revision Commission

Brooklyn Public Input Session

May 19, 2025

·

05:00 PM

2 hr 29 min

Summary

A hearing on potential revisions to the New York City Charter, with a primary focus on opening primary elections to independent/unaffiliated voters and housing/land use reforms.

Discussions included:

  • Various models of open primaries
  • Streamlining processes like ULURP for housing production
  • Fair share requirements for housing and services across districts
  • Moving local elections to even years
  • Police accountability reforms
  • Homeowner protections
  • Comprehensive city planning processes

The commission is in an information-gathering phase, with more public hearings scheduled and expert consultations planned.

Features testimony from elected officials, including Assembly Member Robert Carroll, Council Members Pierina Sanchez and Lincoln Restler, and extensive public testimony.
