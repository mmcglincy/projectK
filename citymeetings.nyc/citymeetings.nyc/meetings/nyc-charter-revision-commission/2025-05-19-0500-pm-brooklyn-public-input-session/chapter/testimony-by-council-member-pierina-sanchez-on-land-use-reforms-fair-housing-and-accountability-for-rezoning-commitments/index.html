TESTIMONY

Testimony by Council Member Pierina Sanchez on land use reforms, fair housing, and accountability for rezoning commitments

1:28:27

·

8 min

Council Member Pierina Sanchez, Chair of the Council's Committee on Housing and Buildings, offers her perspective on land use reforms for the city charter. She urges the commission to consider reforms that build on the Speaker's fair housing framework by creating a charter mechanism with enforceability or expedited review for districts that fall short of meeting their fair share of housing.

Sanchez also advocates for accelerated review processes for 100% affordable projects targeting neighborhood incomes, projects below a certain density threshold, and projects aligned with adopted community or comprehensive plans. Furthermore, she calls for charter reforms to strengthen accountability for city commitments (points of agreement) made during large-scale rezonings, citing delays and unfulfilled promises in her own district after the Jerome Avenue rezoning.
