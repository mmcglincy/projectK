PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Garfield Green, Member of the Public

3:23:11

·

4 min

Garfield Green, a member of the public, shares his deeply personal and emotional experience with immigration detention and its impact on his family. He describes the hardships he faced, including health issues and separation from his children, and expresses his fear and uncertainty about his upcoming immigration appointment.

  • Green highlights the inhumane conditions he experienced in immigration custody, including being denied necessary medication and proper accommodations.
  • He emphasizes the emotional toll of potential separation from his family, stating that he fears this more than cancer.
  • Green criticizes the immigration system, describing it as unjust and contrary to American values, and calls for change.
