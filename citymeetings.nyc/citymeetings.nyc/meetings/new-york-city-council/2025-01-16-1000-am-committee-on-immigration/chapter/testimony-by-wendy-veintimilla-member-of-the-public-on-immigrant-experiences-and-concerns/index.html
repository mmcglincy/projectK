PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Wendy Veintimilla, Member of the Public, on Immigrant Experiences and Concerns

4:27:18

·

4 min

Wendy Veintimilla, an immigrant from Ecuador, shared her personal story of fleeing her country due to torture and threats, and her experiences as an undocumented immigrant in the United States. She highlighted the challenges faced by immigrants and expressed fears about the potential impact of the new presidential administration on immigrant communities.

  • Veintimilla described being exploited by employers and having to work in difficult conditions to avoid being a burden on the country.
  • She emphasized the need for support from the City Council, including assistance in obtaining legal status and protection from deportation.
  • Veintimilla called for the opportunity to contribute to society through paying taxes and living freely without fear.
