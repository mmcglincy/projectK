New York City Council

Committee on Immigration

Jan 16, 2025

·

10:00 AM

6 hr 27 min

Summary

A hearing on New York City's protections and resources for immigrant communities in light of potential changes to federal immigration policies under the incoming Trump administration.

Discussions focused on:

  • Strengthening sanctuary city policies
  • Expanding legal services
  • Improving agency compliance with existing laws
  • Addressing concerns about increased ICE enforcement and family separations

The council introduced resolutions calling for state-level protections and a bill to create accountability for violations of city sanctuary laws.

Features testimony from the Mayor's Office of Immigrant Affairs (MOIA), Office of Asylum Seeker Operations (OASO), and the public.
