Testimony by Howard Teich, Founding Chair of Manhattan Jewish Historical Initiative

0:17:52

·

3 min

Howard Teich, Founding Chair of the Manhattan Jewish Historical Initiative, testifies in support of Resolution 438, emphasizing the significance of Jewish history in New York City and the importance of recognizing "Landing Day". He highlights the contributions of the Jewish community to various aspects of New York life and the value of cultural recognition.

  • Teich describes New York as one of the five greatest Jewish civilizations in 5000 years of history
  • He mentions the creation of a Jewish heritage and history map of the city, which is now being used in school curricula
  • Teich suggests the potential for a cultural tourism program focused on the Jewish community in New York
