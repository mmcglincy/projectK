Catch basin modernization program and flood mitigation strategies

2:48:11

·

112 sec

Anastasios Georgelis details DEP's efforts to modernize and improve the city's catch basin system to mitigate flooding issues.

  • 150,000 catch basins maintained across the city
  • Implementation of slotted manhole covers to combat matting over conditions
  • Goal to install 10 slotted covers per month, with 100 installed this fiscal year
  • New catch basin design with narrower openings and relief slots to prevent clogging
  • Retrofit program adding grill plates to existing catch basins, with 700 installed this fiscal year
