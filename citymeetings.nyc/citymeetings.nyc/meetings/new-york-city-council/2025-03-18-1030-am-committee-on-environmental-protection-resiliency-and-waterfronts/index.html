New York City Council

Committee on Environmental Protection, Resiliency and Waterfronts

Mar 18, 2025

·

10:30 AM

4 hr 30 min

Summary

A hearing on the Department of Environmental Protection (DEP)'s fiscal year 2026 preliminary budget, focusing on the agency's $1.6 billion budget allocation and staffing of 6,307 positions.

Discussions covered:

  • Water infrastructure projects
  • Stormwater management
  • Lead service line replacements
  • Climate resilience efforts
  • Concerns about federal funding risks

Key issues included DEP's 13% staff vacancy rate, the controversial $289 million rental payment from the water board, and implementation of Local Law 97 on building emissions.

Features testimony from DEP Commissioner Rohit Aggarwala and the public.
