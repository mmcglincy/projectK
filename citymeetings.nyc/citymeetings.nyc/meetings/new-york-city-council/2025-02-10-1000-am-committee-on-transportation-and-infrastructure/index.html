New York City Council

Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure

Feb 10, 2025

·

10:00 AM

2 hr 42 min

Summary

A hearing on the status of the yellow cab industry and proposed legislation affecting taxis and for-hire vehicles in New York City.

Discussions focused on:

  • Vehicle accessibility requirements
  • The impact of congestion pricing
  • Driver compensation
  • Insurance coverage

Key bills addressed included:

  • Requiring warning decals for cyclists
  • Extending vehicle retirement dates
  • Studying electric vehicle adoption
  • Potentially lowering insurance requirements

Features testimony from the Taxi and Limousine Commission (TLC), industry stakeholders, disability advocates, and the public.
