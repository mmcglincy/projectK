Council Member Brooks-Powers asks for an update on the e-hail program for yellow and green taxis. Commissioner Do provides information on the program's progress and impact on driver revenue.

  • Driver revenue has increased up to 40% since the meter rate increase in 2022
  • The program provides flexibility for drivers to choose between street hails and e-hails
