Overview and impact of the Taxi Medallion Owner Relief Program

0:31:56

·

7 min

Council Member Brooks-Powers inquires about the Taxi Medallion Owner Relief Program (MRP and MRP Plus), and Commissioner Do provides details on its impact, funding, and current status.

  • The program has provided over $471 million in debt relief to 2,034 medallion owners
  • $65.7 million in city grant funding was provided to incentivize participation
  • 419 medallions are in default status, with 45 defaulted medallions sold
  • The program sunset in April 2024, with most applicants joining around September 2022
