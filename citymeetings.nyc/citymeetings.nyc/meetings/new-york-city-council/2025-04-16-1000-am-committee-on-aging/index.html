New York City Council

Committee on Aging

Apr 16, 2025

·

10:00 AM

2 hr 11 min

Summary

A hearing on Adult Protective Services (APS) referrals, examining operations, challenges, and potential improvements in serving vulnerable adults, particularly older New Yorkers.

Discussions focused on:

  • Increased referrals but decreased eligibility
  • Staffing shortages
  • Housing assistance challenges
  • Federal funding concerns
  • Interagency collaboration
  • Assessment processes
  • Elder abuse response
  • Preparation for a growing aging population

Features testimony from the Human Resources Administration (HRA), Department of Social Services (DSS), NYC Department for the Aging, and the public.
