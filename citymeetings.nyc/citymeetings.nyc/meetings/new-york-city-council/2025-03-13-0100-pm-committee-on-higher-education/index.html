New York City Council

Committee on Higher Education

Mar 13, 2025

·

01:00 PM

2 hr 38 min

Summary

A hearing on CUNY's fiscal year 2026 preliminary budget.

Discussions focused on the $85 million decrease in CUNY's budget compared to the previous year, the impact on key programs like CUNY Reconnect, ASAP, and ACE, and the need for capital improvements across campuses.

Features testimony from CUNY officials, including the Interim Executive Vice Chancellor and University Provost, Executive Vice Chancellor and Chief Operating Officer, and Senior Vice Chancellor for Budget and Finance, as well as student representatives and the public.
