New York City Council

Committee on Finance

Jan 14, 2025

·

10:00 AM

1 hr 7 min

Summary

A hearing on several bills related to property tax administration and preventing deed theft.

Discussions focused on:

  • Improving outreach about tax exemptions
  • Recording tax liens in ACRIS
  • Notifying council members of property recordings for long-held properties
  • Alerting interested parties of property recordings

Features testimony from the Department of Finance (DOF) and the public.
