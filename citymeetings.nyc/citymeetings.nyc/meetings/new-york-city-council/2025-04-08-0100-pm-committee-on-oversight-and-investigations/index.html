New York City Council

Committee on Oversight and Investigations

Apr 08, 2025

·

01:00 PM

2 hr 6 min

Summary

A hearing on New York City's food infrastructure and its impact on produce quality and cost.

Discussions focused on:

  • Modernizing the Hunts Point Market
  • Addressing cold storage capacity challenges
  • Improving regional food distribution
  • Increasing access to healthy and affordable food
  • Rising food prices
  • Potential transportation improvements for food distribution.

The hearing also covered the need for diverse representation among food distributors and balancing emergency food support with sustainable community-centered solutions.

Features testimony from the Mayor's Office of Food Policy, the Economic Development Corporation (EDC), Hunts Point Market operators, food policy experts, and the public.
