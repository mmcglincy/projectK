Representative from the Chinese American Planning Council on Challenges Faced by Seniors and the Need for Increased Support Amid COVID-19

3:02:08

·

8 min

A representative from the Chinese American Planning Council highlights the isolation and safety challenges faced by seniors amid COVID-19 and anti-East Asian sentiment and calls for increased support for meal delivery and facility maintenance.

  • The COVID-19 pandemic and heightened anti-East Asian sentiment have significantly impacted the mobility and safety of the senior population, leading to increased isolation.
  • The meal delivery service faces logistical challenges with a reduction to just one driver, affecting the council's ability to distribute meals effectively.
  • There has been a decrease in financial support for essential services such as facility maintenance, contributing to service delivery challenges.
  • The representative urgently requests additional support, including funding for more drivers and facility repairs, to better support the senior community.
