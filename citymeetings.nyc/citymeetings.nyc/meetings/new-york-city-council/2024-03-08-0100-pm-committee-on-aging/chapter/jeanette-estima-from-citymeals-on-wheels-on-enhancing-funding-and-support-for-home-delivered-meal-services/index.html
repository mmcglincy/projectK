TESTIMONY

Jeanette Estima from Citymeals on Wheels on Enhancing Funding and Support for Home-Delivered Meal Services

2:23:54

·

138 sec

Jeanette Estima highlights the growing need for home meal services for older adults and the financial challenges facing providers due to escalating service demands and food costs.

  • Estima requests a renewal and increase in funding from the NYC Council to sustain and expand home-delivered meal services.
  • Citymeals on Wheels has provided over 34,000 emergency meals and pre-supplied over 160,000 meals for extreme weather situations in the fiscal year.
  • The organization served about 115 more older adults than the previous year, with a 33% increase in food costs during the pandemic.
  • Estima urges an increase in the reimbursement rate for meal deliveries and a $50 million capital fund for kitchen repairs and upgrades.
