PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Jodi Ziesemer, Co-Director of the Immigrant Protection Unit at New York Legal Assistance Group

6:38:00

·

157 sec

Jodi Ziesemer testified about three key programs: the Rapid Response Legal Collaborative, Key to the City program, and Immigrant and Health Initiatives. She emphasized the need for increased funding and support for these programs, particularly in light of rising deportation orders and enforcement.

  • The Rapid Response Legal Collaborative has an 88% success rate in challenging removal orders but faced budget cuts despite increased demand.
  • Key to the City program provides monthly community-based intake clinics and screenings throughout the city.
  • Ziesemer called for the restoration and expansion of the million-dollar budget for the Rapid Response Legal Collaborative.
