Internet network pilot program and state funding

1:46:24

·

174 sec

Council Member Jennifer Gutiérrez inquires about the Internet network pilot program mentioned in the digital equity roadmap, particularly its funding source and the city's involvement. Matthew C. Fraser provides clarity on the program's structure and funding.

  • The program is a New York State-funded initiative, with an RFP that a nonprofit provider (Flume) applied to.
  • The city provides the vision and execution for the program, while the state provides the funding.
  • The pilot program, run through HPD, aims to construct a wireless network providing free Internet access to households receiving Section 8 assistance in the Bronx and Upper Manhattan.
  • OTI works closely with HPD on the deployment of technology, setting the strategy and validating the provider selection process.
  • The $2.4 million in capital mentioned in the city's roadmap does not include the state funding for this particular program.
