New York City Council

Committee on Technology

Mar 10, 2025

·

03:00 PM

2 hr 9 min

Summary

A hearing on the Office of Technology and Innovation (OTI)'s preliminary budget for fiscal year 2026.

Discussions focused on major initiatives including the Big Apple Connect program, MyCity portal, AI implementation, and a new T-Mobile contract for city mobile services.

Council members raised questions about funding sustainability, contract transparency, digital equity plans, and improvements to the 311 system. The hearing also covered OTI's success in contracting with minority and women-owned businesses.

Features testimony from the Office of Technology and Innovation (OTI) and the public.
