Summary

A hearing on the Health + Hospitals (H+H) executive budget for FY 2026.

Discussions focused on potential impacts of proposed federal Medicaid cuts, current financial outlook, capital projects for flood mitigation, and various healthcare initiatives.

Features testimony from Health + Hospitals officials, including President and CEO Dr. Mitchell Katz, and the public.
