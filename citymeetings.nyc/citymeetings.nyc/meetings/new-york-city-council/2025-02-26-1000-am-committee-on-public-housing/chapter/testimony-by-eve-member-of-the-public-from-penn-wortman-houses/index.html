Eve, a resident of Penn-Wortman Houses, provides passionate testimony criticizing NYCHA and the RAD/PACT program for lack of transparency, coercion, and poor management. She describes personal experiences of harassment, legal troubles, and deteriorating living conditions since the conversion of her development.

  • Eve alleges that NYCHA and PACT partners are secretive, lie to residents, and treat them poorly
  • She reports ongoing issues such as broken lobby doors, ineffective security cameras, and frequent management changes
  • Eve expresses feeling stressed and afraid to speak out due to perceived retaliation and labeling by authorities
