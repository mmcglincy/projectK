Testimony by Rosemary Williams, Public Health Leader and NYCHA Resident

5:10:57

·

171 sec

Rosemary Williams, a NYCHA resident, provided testimony about her experiences living in public housing, highlighting issues of safety, health, and unresolved complaints. She shared personal stories of dangerous living conditions in both Eastchester Gardens and Ingersoll Houses, emphasizing the lack of response from various authorities.

  • Described being kidnapped and raised in Eastchester projects, with NYCHA failing to question discrepancies in her living situation
  • Reported ongoing issues with rats, mice, and dangerous living conditions in her current NYCHA apartment
  • Claimed to have made complaints to various officials and organizations, including NYCHA management, politicians, and federal agencies, without resolution
