Testimony by Dr. Keisha Crawford, Resident Management Corporation Chair of Stuyvesant Gardens

0:18:51

·

7 min

Dr. Keisha Crawford, speaking as the Resident Management Corporation Chair of Stuyvesant Gardens, detailed years of neglect, poor living conditions, and recent intimidation tactics used to force residents into conversion. Dr. Crawford announced a binding resolution rejecting RAD/PACT and threatened legal action if harassment continues.

  • Described chronic issues like mold, leaks, and pest infestations in Stuyvesant Gardens
  • Alleged violations of federal housing laws, ADA non-compliance, and tenant harassment
  • Demanded a freeze on RAD/PACT conversions and an independent oversight body
  • Gave NYCHA and involved parties 7 days to correct course before escalating legal and legislative actions
