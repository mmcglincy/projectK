Testimony by Danette Chavis, Tenant Association President of LaGuardia Houses

5:14:19

·

143 sec

Danette Chavis, the Tenant Association president of LaGuardia Houses, testifies about concerns regarding NYCHA's RAD/PACT program and issues affecting tenant associations. She criticizes the lack of time for residents to speak at hearings and expresses frustration with NYCHA's implementation of RAD/PACT without proper resident voting.

  • Chavis argues that NYCHA is constructively dismantling tenant association boards by withholding funding and providing inadequate training.
  • She criticizes NYCHA for forcing TA leaders to sign agreements they haven't had a chance to read or understand.
  • Chavis calls for action to address these ongoing complaints and issues affecting tenant associations.
