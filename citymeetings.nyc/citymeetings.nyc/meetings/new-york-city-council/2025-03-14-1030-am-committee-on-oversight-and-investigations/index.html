New York City Council

Committee on Oversight and Investigations

Mar 14, 2025

·

10:30 AM

1 hr 43 min

Summary

A hearing on the Department of Investigation (DOI)'s fiscal year 2026 preliminary budget.

Discussions focused on DOI's budget needs, staffing challenges, recent accomplishments, and proposals to strengthen the department's independence.

Key issues included the two-for-one hiring policy, low salaries affecting staff retention, reliance on forfeiture funds for operational needs, and the need for additional positions.

Features testimony from DOI Commissioner Jocelyn Strauber and the public.
