Testimony by Mayra Garcia, Director of iHOPE School, on School Bus Transportation Issues

4:31:13

·

139 sec

Mayra Garcia, Director of iHOPE School, testified about severe transportation issues affecting their medically fragile students. She highlighted how these problems are causing significant absences, missed therapies, and potential loss of tuition reimbursement for families.

  • 68 out of 130 students have collectively missed over 400 school days due to transportation issues
  • Students face problems such as missing routes, lack of travel nurses, and excessively long bus rides (2-3 hours)
  • Transportation delays are causing students to miss crucial medical interventions and therapeutic services
  • Families risk losing tuition reimbursement due to high absences caused by transportation problems
