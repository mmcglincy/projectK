PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Sarah Catalina Naro, Representative of Parents to Improve School Transportation (PIST)

2:49:59

·

3 min

Sarah Catalina Naro, representing Parents to Improve School Transportation (PIST), testified about various issues and recommendations regarding school bus transportation in NYC. She highlighted concerns about flawed routes, understaffing, and communication problems, while also addressing proposed legislation and suggesting improvements.

  • Advocated for safer and more sensible school bus routes, citing issues with unstaffed routes and missed classes
  • Opposed rebidding contracts without Employee Protection Provisions (EPP), suggesting a contract extension to evaluate vendors and expand services
  • Commented on proposed legislation, supporting Resolution 250 for stop arms on buses, but expressing reservations about Intro 515 without further accountability measures
