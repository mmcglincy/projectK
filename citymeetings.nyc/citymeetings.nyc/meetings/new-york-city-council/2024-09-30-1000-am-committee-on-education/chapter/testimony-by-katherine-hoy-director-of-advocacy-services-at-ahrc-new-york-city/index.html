PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Katherine Hoy, Director of Advocacy Services at AHRC New York City

3:26:30

·

3 min

Katherine Hoy, representing AHRC New York City and various advocacy coalitions, testified on the challenges in school bus transportation for students with disabilities. She highlighted issues such as long ride times, inadequate training for bus staff, and the impact of transportation problems on school attendance and after-school programs.

  • Emphasized the need for improved processes in implementing transportation accommodations for students with IEPs
  • Called for better training of bus drivers and matrons to handle the needs of students with intellectual and developmental disabilities
  • Stressed the negative impact of transportation issues on school attendance and the loss of after-school programs due to lack of transportation
