Transportation services for students in foster care

1:50:04

·

121 sec

Council Member Rita Joseph inquires about transportation services for students in foster care. Glenn Risbrook provides statistics and information about the request process and service provision.

  • 1,371 students in foster care submitted applications for busing in the 2023-2024 school year
  • 739 students received bus service; the difference is due to duplications and ineligible requests
  • If busing cannot be established within 10 days of receiving a request, rideshare is automatically provisioned
  • The department does not track data on requests withdrawn due to students changing schools or foster homes while waiting for bus service
