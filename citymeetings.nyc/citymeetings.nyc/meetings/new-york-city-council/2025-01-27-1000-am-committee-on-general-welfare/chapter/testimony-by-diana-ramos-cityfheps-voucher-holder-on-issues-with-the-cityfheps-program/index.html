Testimony by Diana Ramos, CityFHEPS Voucher Holder, on Issues with the CityFHEPS Program

1:18:35

·

4 min

Diana Ramos, a CityFHEPS voucher holder, testifies about the numerous challenges she faces with the program, including lack of information, poor living conditions, and difficulties in transferring to a new apartment. She highlights issues such as maintenance problems, pest infestations, and the absence of clear communication from program administrators.

  • Ramos describes specific problems in her apartment, including a non-functioning toilet, mice infestation, and faulty electrical outlets.
  • She expresses frustration about the lack of information on how to transfer apartments or access utility assistance.
  • Ramos mentions that her neighbors are facing similar issues, including potential evictions and long waits for shopping letters.
