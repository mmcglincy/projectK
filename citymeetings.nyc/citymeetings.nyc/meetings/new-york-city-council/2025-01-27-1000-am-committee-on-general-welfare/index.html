New York City Council

Committee on General Welfare

Jan 27, 2025

·

10:00 AM

2 hr 27 min

Summary

A hearing on the administration of the CityFHEPS voucher program, exploring its effectiveness in addressing homelessness and housing instability in New York City.

Discussions focused on:

  • Application processes
  • Delays in processing
  • Landlord engagement
  • Recommendations for improvement

Features testimony from the Department of Social Services (DSS), Human Resources Administration (HRA), and the public.
