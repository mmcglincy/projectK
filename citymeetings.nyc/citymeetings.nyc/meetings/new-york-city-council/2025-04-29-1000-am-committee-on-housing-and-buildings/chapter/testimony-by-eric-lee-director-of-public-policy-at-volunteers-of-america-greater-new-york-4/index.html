PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Eric Lee, Director of Public Policy at Volunteers of America Greater New York

3:01:32

·

3 min

Eric Lee from Volunteers of America Greater New York (VOAGNY) testified about improving access to affordable housing and the Housing Connect system. He highlighted VOAGNY's recent affordable housing projects and emphasized the need to maximize the use of every affordable housing unit in the city.

  • Supported HPD's one-year waiver for re-rentals and called for more frequent training and faster approvals for marketing agents
  • Endorsed Intro 1265 for email/text notifications and Intro 1266 for in-person assistance with Housing Connect applications
  • Stressed the importance of making the affordable housing process more equitable while ensuring Housing Connect can effectively achieve this goal
