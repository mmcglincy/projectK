New York City Council

Committee on Housing and Buildings

Apr 29, 2025

·

10:00 AM

3 hr 21 min

Summary

A hearing on the Housing Connect system and affordable housing in New York City.

Discussions focused on:

  • Inefficiencies in the application process
  • Accessibility challenges for seniors and non-English speakers
  • Mismatch between income requirements and applicants' actual incomes
  • Proposed legislation to improve the system

The hearing explored issues such as long processing times, re-rental vacancies, and the need for a comprehensive overhaul of Housing Connect.

Features testimony from the Department of Housing Preservation and Development (HPD), housing advocates, developers, and members of the public.
