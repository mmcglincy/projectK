NYPD's community engagement initiatives like 'Coffee with a Crime Prevention Officer'

0:54:39

·

157 sec

Captain Papavlasopoulos describes NYPD's community engagement initiatives, particularly the 'Coffee with a crime prevention officer' program, aimed at educating older adults about scams and crime prevention.

  • NYPD partners with local businesses like McDonald's and Starbucks to offer free coffee to older adults.
  • Crime prevention officers meet with older adults one-on-one in these settings.
  • Officers provide informational materials about the latest scams and protection strategies.
  • These initiatives are a response to crimes targeting older adults and have received positive feedback from communities.
  • Large retail stores have reached out to be part of these initiatives, showing community support.
  • Commissioner Cortés-Vázquez emphasizes the importance of the partnership between NYPD and the dedicated older adult liaison in making these initiatives possible and effective.
