Testimony by Jacqueline Tellez, Representative of New York Association of Wholesalers And Distributors

2:56:02

·

3 min

Jacqueline Tellez, representing the New York Association of Wholesalers And Distributors, testified about the illegal sale of untaxed cigarettes and flavored e-cigarettes in unlicensed stores throughout New York City. She highlighted the significant financial loss to the city and state, as well as the broader implications for public safety and the legal market.

  • Illegal cigarette trade is costing New York City and State over $2.5 billion annually, totaling $37.5 billion over the past 15 years.
  • The association's sting operation and first alert program identified thousands of locations suspected of selling illegal products.
  • Tellez urged the council to take decisive action, enforce existing laws, and employ stringent penalties on violators to reclaim the billions lost to the illegal marketplace.
