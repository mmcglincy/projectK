How are incidents of alleged jaywalking involving interactions with the public reviewed?

1:29:56

·

115 sec

The deputy chief explains that body camera footage is reviewed to ensure interactions are within guidelines.

  • Officers' supervisors and integrity control officers regularly review body camera footage from officers
  • They conduct spot checks to look for any violations of guidelines
  • For specific incidents like alleged jaywalking stops, they can review the body camera footage and conduct investigations if needed
  • This allows oversight over enforcement like jaywalking summonses to ensure they follow proper procedures
