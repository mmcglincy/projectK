2:08:20

Council Member Rita C. Joseph engages in a Q&A session with Jacques Jiha, Director of NYC Mayor's Office of Management and Budget, regarding funding for various educational programs. The discussion covers Head Start, special education services, LGBTQ+ inclusive curriculum, and services for autistic learners.

  • Joseph expresses concern about the uncertainty of Head Start funding and the potential impact on current providers.
  • Questions are raised about the provision of mandated services for students with IEPs and the funding for preschool special education.
  • The council member inquires about the commitment to fully fund the LGBTQ+ inclusive curriculum and increase seats for autistic learners.
