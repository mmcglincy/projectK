New York City Council

Committee on Fire and Emergency Management

Feb 25, 2025

·

10:00 AM

40 min

Summary

A hearing on the process and inspections of New York City's temporary certificates of occupancy (TCOs).

Discussions focused on:

  • The Department of Buildings' practices for issuing and monitoring TCOs
  • Concerns about expired TCOs
  • The Fire Department's role in approving fire protection plans
  • Potential gaps in enforcement and monitoring.

The hearing also addressed the need for increased staffing and resources in light of expected construction increases due to recent zoning changes.

Features testimony from the Department of Buildings (DOB), Fire Department (FDNY), and the public.
