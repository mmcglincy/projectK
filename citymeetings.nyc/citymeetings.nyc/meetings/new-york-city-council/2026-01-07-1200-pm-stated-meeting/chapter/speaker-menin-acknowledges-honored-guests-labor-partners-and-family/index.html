1:22:36

Speaker Julie Menin recognizes the diverse group of leaders and supporters present for her election, including former Speakers, city and state officials, and labor representatives.

  • She recognizes high-ranking officials including Attorney General Tish James, Public Advocate Jumaane Williams, and Comptroller Mark Levine
  • She thanks prominent labor leaders from HTC, 32BJ, DC 37, UFT, AFT, and RWDSU
