New York City Council

Stated Meeting

Jan 07, 2026

·

12:00 PM

1 hr 55 min

Summary

The Stated Meeting happens every 2 weeks (4 weeks in summer), when the council votes on and introduces new legislation. Notably at this meeting, Julie Menin is elected Speaker of the NYC City Council and David Carr is elected Minority Leader.
