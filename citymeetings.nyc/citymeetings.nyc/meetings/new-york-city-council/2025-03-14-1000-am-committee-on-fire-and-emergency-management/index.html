New York City Council

Committee on Fire and Emergency Management

Mar 14, 2025

·

10:00 AM

4 hr 23 min

Summary

A hearing on the fiscal year 2026 preliminary budget for the Fire Department of New York (FDNY) and New York City Emergency Management (NYCEM).

Discussions focused on FDNY's $2.6 billion budget, including initiatives to reduce fire fatalities, address EMS staffing challenges, and implement technology upgrades.

NYCEM's $79 million budget was examined, with emphasis on emergency response activities, community preparedness programs, and potential impacts of federal funding cuts.

Council members raised concerns about EMS response times, pay parity, vehicle replacements, and firehouse repairs.

Features testimony from FDNY Commissioner Robert Tucker, NYCEM Commissioner Zachary Iscol, and the public.
