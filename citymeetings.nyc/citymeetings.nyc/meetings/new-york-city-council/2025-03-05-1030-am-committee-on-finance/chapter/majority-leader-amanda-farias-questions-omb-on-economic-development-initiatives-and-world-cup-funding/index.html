1:37:58

·

3 min

Majority Leader Amanda Farias questioned OMB officials about various economic development initiatives, including tax breaks for the office sector, funding for the 2026 World Cup, and their economic impacts on New York City. OMB officials provided some answers and promised to follow up with more detailed information on several points.

  • Farias inquired about the economic impact of EDC's MCORE tax breaks and requested estimates of their benefits.
  • Questions were raised about the $20 million allocation for the 2026 World Cup, its intended use, and potential economic benefits for the city.
  • OMB officials agreed to provide more detailed analysis on the cost-benefit ratios and economic impacts of these initiatives.
