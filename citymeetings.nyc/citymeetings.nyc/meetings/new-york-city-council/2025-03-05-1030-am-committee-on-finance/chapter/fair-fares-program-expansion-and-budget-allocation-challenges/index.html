Fair Fares program expansion and budget allocation challenges

1:29:14

·

3 min

Council Member Ayala discusses the recent expansion of the Fair Fares program and inquires about its budget allocation, particularly the lack of baseline funding for the expansion in future years. Director Jiha addresses the current funding status and future adjustments.

  • The Fair Fares eligibility was expanded to include those making up to 145% of the federal poverty level
  • Additional funding of $20.8 million for the expanded program has not been baselined
  • Director Jiha states that the program is well-funded for FY25 and adjustments will be made based on utilization
  • $2 million is budgeted for outreach to increase program usage
  • Council Member Ayala expresses concern about the stress of having to repeatedly adjust funding for fluid programs
  • Director Jiha acknowledges that many city programs face similar circumstances of not being fully funded based on expenses
