New York City Council

Committee on Small Business

Jan 30, 2025

·

01:30 PM

2 hr 57 min

Summary

A hearing on legal and regulatory challenges affecting small businesses in New York City.

Discussions focused on three proposed bills:

  • Intro 1132 (requiring SBS to report on violations issued to businesses and conduct education/training)
  • Intro 449 (establishing a commercial landlord watch list focused on tenant harassment)
  • Intro 1082 (requiring a study on costs/fees related to opening and maintaining a small business)

Key issues addressed included:

  • Navigating complex regulations
  • High operational costs
  • Commercial tenant harassment
  • Access to legal assistance
  • Burdensome fines and violations

The hearing also explored various SBS support programs such as the Commercial Lease Assistance program and NYC Business Express Service Team (NYC BEST).

Features testimony from the Department of Small Business Services (SBS) and the public.
