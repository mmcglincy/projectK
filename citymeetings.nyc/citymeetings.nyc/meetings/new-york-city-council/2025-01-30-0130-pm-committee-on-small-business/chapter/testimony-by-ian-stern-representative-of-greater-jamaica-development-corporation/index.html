PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Ian Stern, Representative of Greater Jamaica Development Corporation

2:09:56

·

156 sec

Ian Stern, representing the Greater Jamaica Development Corporation (GJDC), expresses strong support for Intro 1132, which aims to enhance fine transparency for small businesses in New York City. The legislation addresses critical concerns shared by the business community and aligns with GJDC's mission to foster economic growth in southeast Queens.

  • GJDC has witnessed regulatory challenges, especially fines, as a major source of stress and financial strain for business owners over its 50 years of service.
  • The proposed annual review of fines levied on businesses citywide is seen as a crucial step towards providing valuable insights and enabling targeted interventions.
  • GJDC believes that increased transparency is essential to building trust between small businesses and the city, empowering them with knowledge and resources to navigate regulations confidently.
