Discussion on small business awareness of available support programs

2:43:04

·

5 min

Council Member Oswald Feliz inquires about small businesses' awareness of available support programs, including commercial lease assistance and other legal aid. The panel members provide insights on program awareness and effectiveness:

  • Many businesses, especially in certain areas, are unaware of available support programs
  • Outreach efforts, including monthly meetings and networking events, are making a difference in raising awareness
  • SBS Best program is well-received by those who have used it
  • Commercial Lease Assistance (CLA) program faces challenges, including privacy concerns and limitations on court representation
  • Panel members stress the importance of accessible information and resources for small businesses
  • There's a need for more one-on-one advice and support, as educational workshops alone are not sufficient
  • Cultural factors and trust issues can impact program participation, especially in immigrant communities
  • The discussion highlights the value of connecting potential business owners with resources before they invest in starting a business
