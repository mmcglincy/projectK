PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Andrea Tan, Director of Microenterprise Project at Volunteers of Legal Service (VOLS)

2:38:07

·

136 sec

Andrea Tan, representing Volunteers of Legal Service (VOLS), testified in support of proposed bills addressing challenges faced by small businesses in New York City. She emphasized the importance of analyzing costs for starting and maintaining businesses, introducing a commercial landlord watch list, and providing tailored regulatory compliance assistance.

  • VOLS provides transactional legal services to small businesses and sees firsthand the regulatory and financial barriers entrepreneurs face.
  • The proposed commercial landlord watch list would help address the power imbalance between commercial landlords and tenants, as commercial tenants lack many legal protections.
  • Tan stressed the need for accessible resources and thoughtful policies to create a more equitable and thriving small business ecosystem.
