Council Member Mercedes Narcisse discusses small business challenges and introduces Intro 1082

0:09:49

·

3 min

Council Member Mercedes Narcisse, drawing from her experience as a former small business owner, discusses the challenges faced by small businesses in New York City. She introduces Intro 1082, which calls for a comprehensive study on the costs and regulatory burdens affecting small businesses, with a focus on MWBEs and businesses in transit-poor areas.

  • Narcisse emphasizes the need for more city support to make starting and sustaining small businesses easier and more affordable.
  • She highlights the importance of small businesses to communities and the need to help them thrive.
  • Narcisse urges her colleagues to support Intro 1082 as a step towards empowering the small business community.
