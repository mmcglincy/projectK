New York City Council

Committee on Civil Service and Labor

Feb 28, 2025

·

10:00 AM

1 hr 40 min

Summary

A hearing on pending layoffs at the Brooklyn Museum focusing on the institution's plan to lay off 47 staff members, including union workers from DC37 and UAW Local 2110.

Discussions centered on:

  • The museum's financial challenges
  • The impact of layoffs on museum operations and community services
  • Alternatives to job cuts
  • The role of city funding in supporting cultural institutions.

Features testimony from the Department of Cultural Affairs (DCLA), union representatives from DC37 and UAW, affected museum employees, and the public.
