Council Member Susan Zhuang introduces bills on speed camera signage and rat complaint response

1:04:48

·

110 sec

Council Member Susan Zhuang introduces two new bills aimed at improving street safety and cleanliness in New York City. The first bill, Intro 1218, requires warning signs near speed cameras, while the second, Intro 1217, mandates a 10-day response time for rat and pest complaints to the Department of Health.

  • Intro 1218 aims to reduce traffic fatalities by alerting drivers to slow down before reaching speed cameras.
  • Intro 1217 addresses the city's rat problem by requiring faster responses to 311 complaints and better accountability for city agencies.
  • Zhuang emphasizes the need for transparency and urges her colleagues to support both bills.
