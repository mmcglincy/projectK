New York City Council

Stated Meeting

Mar 12, 2025

·

01:30 PM

1 hr 16 min

Summary

The Stated Meeting happens every 2 weeks (4 weeks in summer), when the council votes on and introduces new legislation.

At this meeting, the council passed several bills related to improving foster care, enhancing jail visitation processes, requiring more reporting on probation programs, archiving government social media posts, and improving access to IDNYC municipal ID cards.

The council also voted on land use items, including a proposal for a potential casino development near Citi Field in Queens, and passed resolutions urging state action on immigrant legal representation and food benefits programs.
