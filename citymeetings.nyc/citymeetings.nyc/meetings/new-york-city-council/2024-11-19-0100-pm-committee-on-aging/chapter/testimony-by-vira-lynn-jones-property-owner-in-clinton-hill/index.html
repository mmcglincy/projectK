PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Vira Lynn Jones, Property Owner in Clinton Hill

1:54:53

·

7 min

Vira Lynn Jones, a property owner in Clinton Hill, Brooklyn, shares her harrowing experience with deed theft and predatory lending. She describes how her home was fraudulently sold without her knowledge through a deceptive foreclosure process, and the subsequent psychological and physical harassment she has endured while trying to reclaim her property.

  • Jones highlights the involvement of specific individuals and entities in what she calls a "well-organized criminal enterprise" targeting seniors and homeowners.
  • She details the difficulties in getting help from agencies and the court system, which often dismiss her case due to the foreclosure label.
  • The testimony underscores the severe emotional, physical, and financial toll that deed theft and related harassment can have on victims, particularly senior citizens.
