New York City Council

Committee on Cultural Affairs, Libraries and International Intergroup Relations

Jan 27, 2025

·

10:00 AM

2 hr 59 min

Summary

A hearing on expanding pathways into the arts and cultural workforce for New Yorkers.

Discussions focused on:

  • Internship and apprenticeship programs
  • Workforce development initiatives
  • Strategies to increase diversity in the cultural sector

Key topics included:

  • Funding challenges
  • The importance of paid opportunities
  • Efforts to improve data tracking on program outcomes
  • Proposals for a citywide creative job fair

The hearing also addressed issues such as affordable workspace, living wages, and portable benefits for arts workers.

Features testimony from the Department of Cultural Affairs (DCLA), representatives from various cultural institutions, and the public.
