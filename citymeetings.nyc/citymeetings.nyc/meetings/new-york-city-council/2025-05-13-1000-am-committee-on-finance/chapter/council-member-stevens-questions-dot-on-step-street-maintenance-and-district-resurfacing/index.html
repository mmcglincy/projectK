Council Member Stevens questions DOT on step street maintenance and district resurfacing

1:58:30

·

3 min

Council Member Althea V. Stevens questions DOT officials about the budget and maintenance of step streets in her district, expressing concerns about their poor condition and lack of regular upkeep. She also inquires about the status of previously allocated funds for district resurfacing.

  • Stevens highlights the importance of step streets as a form of transportation in her district, particularly in areas with limited access to other transportation options.
  • She requests detailed information on the maintenance schedule, contracts, and coordination with the Department of Sanitation for step street upkeep.
  • The council member also follows up on the allocation of funds from the previous budget season for district resurfacing, seeking clarity on the project's status and implementation.
