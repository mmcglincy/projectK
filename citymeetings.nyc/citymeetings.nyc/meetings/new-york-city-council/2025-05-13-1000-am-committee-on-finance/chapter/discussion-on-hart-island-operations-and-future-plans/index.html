Discussion on Hart Island operations and future plans

4:21:06

·

4 min

Council Member Shekar Krishnan and Commissioner Sue Donoghue engage in a detailed discussion about Hart Island, covering its current operations, staffing, budget, and future plans. The Parks Department has taken over management of Hart Island and is working on improving access and facilities.

  • The Parks Department facilitates visits for loved ones and offers public tours through their Urban Park Rangers.
  • There is capital funding for projects such as slope stabilization and chapel preservation.
  • The Parks Department is developing a conceptual plan for enhancing public access while respecting the site's nature as a public burial ground.
  • The department anticipates continuing to manage Hart Island unless directed otherwise.
