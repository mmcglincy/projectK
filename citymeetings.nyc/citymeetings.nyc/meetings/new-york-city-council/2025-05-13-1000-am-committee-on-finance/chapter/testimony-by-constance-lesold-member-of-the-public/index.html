PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Constance Lesold, Member of the Public

5:36:12

·

3 min

Constance Lesold, an 87-year-old senior citizen, testified in support of additional funding for the Parks Department and proposed rebuilding the Eastern Parkway Coalition to increase public involvement in parks. She emphasized the need for more public engagement and highlighted past successful initiatives.

  • Advocated for restoring a trolley program in Prospect Park to improve accessibility for seniors
  • Suggested combining administration of Eastern Parkway and Ocean Parkway to increase attention and involvement
  • Strongly opposed the creation of a skateboard park in Mount Prospect Park
