PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Susan Wallace, Veteran

1:31:52

·

4 min

Susan Wallace, a veteran and retired JAG officer, testifies about the difficulties and discrimination she has faced as a female veteran in New York City. She highlights issues with recognition, professional licensing, and community involvement, emphasizing the need for better understanding and support for all veterans, including reservists.

  • Describes challenges in obtaining a bar license transfer and facing inappropriate questions about her military service
  • Highlights the invisibility and isolation experienced by female veterans
  • Addresses the issue of veteran definition, particularly for reservists, and offers to help improve legislation
