New York City Council

Committee on Fire and Emergency Management

Apr 03, 2025

·

10:00 AM

2 hr 34 min

Summary

A hearing on residential displacement after emergencies, focusing on challenges faced by displaced residents and proposed legislation to improve support and protections.

Discussions covered agency coordination, tenant rights, emergency housing assistance, and accountability for negligent landlords

Features testimony from the Fire Department, Department of Buildings, Housing Preservation & Development, and the public, including displaced residents and legal advocates.
