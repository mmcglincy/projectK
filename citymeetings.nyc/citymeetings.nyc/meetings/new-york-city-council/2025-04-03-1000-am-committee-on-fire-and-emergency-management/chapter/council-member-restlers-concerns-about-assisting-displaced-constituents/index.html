Council member Restler's concerns about assisting displaced constituents

1:46:48

·

3 min

Council Member Lincoln Restler expresses concerns about the challenges in contacting and supporting constituents displaced by fires. He emphasizes the need for better mechanisms to facilitate communication between council offices and affected residents.

  • Restler highlights the difficulty in providing timely assistance, especially for fires occurring at odd hours
  • He suggests the need for a standard protocol to share contact information of displaced residents with council offices
  • Restler emphasizes the role of council members in navigating city agencies and bureaucracy on behalf of affected constituents
