New York City Council

Committee on Hospitals

Jan 10, 2025

·

01:00 PM

4 hr 11 min

Summary

A hearing on challenges faced by NYC Health + Hospitals (H+H) physicians, focusing on competitive wages, working conditions, staffing shortages, and their impact on patient care.

Discussions covered issues such as:

  • Reduced appointment times
  • Difficulty in recruiting and retaining doctors
  • Burnout among medical staff
  • The potential for a doctors' strike

The hearing also explored H+H's strategies to address these challenges and the need for increased funding to improve doctor compensation and working conditions.

Features testimony from H+H CEO Dr. Mitch Katz, representatives from the Doctors Council union, H+H physicians, and the public.
