PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Damian Bazadona, Founder of Situation Project, on Arts Access and Untapped Cultural Resources

3:00:28

·

3 min

Damian Bazadona, founder of Situation Project, testified about the untapped potential in New York City's arts and cultural sector, highlighting millions of empty seats in venues annually while many students lack access to arts education. He described his organization's efforts to connect students with these opportunities and emphasized the need for public-private partnerships to utilize this untapped equity.

  • Situation Project has reached over 100,000 students since its founding in 2011, providing access to live shows, career opportunities, and educational tours.
  • Bazadona estimates 3-5 million empty seats annually in NYC's cultural venues, representing hundreds of millions of dollars in untapped equity.
  • He advocates for discussions on public-private partnerships to leverage these resources for expanding arts access and education.
