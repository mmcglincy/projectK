New York City Council

Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure

Apr 23, 2025

·

10:00 AM

3 hr 56 min

Summary

A hearing on the Dining Out NYC permanent outdoor dining program, which replaced the temporary pandemic-era program.

Discussions focused on challenges in the program's rollout and implementation, including:

  • Complex application processes
  • Approval delays
  • High costs for restaurants
  • Restrictions on year-round roadway dining
  • Clearance requirements

Features testimony from the Department of Transportation (DOT) and the public.
