Council Member Hudson introduces new bills and acknowledges a constituent's death

1:08:12

·

112 sec

Council Member Crystal Hudson apologizes for speaking out of turn earlier and acknowledges the passing of Denzel Chan, a constituent killed on Labor Day.

She then introduces three of six new bills she's proposing, focusing on automatic enrollment in city benefits, improved record-keeping for jail visits, and gender-affirming items for transgender individuals in city jails.

  • Intro 1028: Requires DSS to automatically enroll eligible individuals in city benefits programs using existing records.
  • Intro 1026: Mandates more detailed record-keeping for jail visits to address concerns about canceled video visits.
  • Intro 1027: Aims to reduce dehumanization of transgender and non-conforming people in city jails by providing access to gender-affirming items.
