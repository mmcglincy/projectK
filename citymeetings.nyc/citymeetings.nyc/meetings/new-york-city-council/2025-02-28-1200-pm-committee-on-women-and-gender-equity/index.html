New York City Council

Committee on Women and Gender Equity

Feb 28, 2025

·

12:00 PM

2 hr 43 min

Summary

A hearing on ensuring access to supports for transgender, gender non-conforming, and non-binary (TGNCNB) people in New York City.

Discussions focused on eight proposed bills and resolutions covering topics such as support for TGNCNB migrants, healthcare access, public outreach, and protection of health information.

Key issues included increasing the Trans Equity Fund to $10 million, protecting access to gender-affirming care, and addressing recent federal actions threatening TGNCNB rights.

Features testimony from the NYC Unity Project and the public.
