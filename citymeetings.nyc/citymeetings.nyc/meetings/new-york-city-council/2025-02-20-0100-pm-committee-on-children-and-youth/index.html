New York City Council

Committee on Children and Youth

Feb 20, 2025

·

01:00 PM

2 hr 56 min

Summary

A hearing on evaluating New York City's foster care system.

Discussions focused on:

  • Placement stability
  • Kinship care
  • Residential foster care
  • Outcomes for youth aging out of the system
  • Challenges faced by providers

Key topics included efforts to reduce the number of children in care, improve reunification rates, enhance support for LGBTQ youth and foster parents, address housing needs for those aging out, and increase transparency and accountability in the system.

Features testimony from the Administration for Children's Services (ACS), foster care providers, advocates, and the public.
