What is delaying the $100,000,000 in federal funding and what are the challenges in accessing these resources?

1:39:09

·

151 sec

Jacques Jiha explains that the delay in receiving $100,000,000 from the federal government is due to stringent requirements for information not previously collected by the City, complicating the application process.

  • The NYC Mayor's Office of Management and Budget (OMB) faces challenges in collecting new types of information, such as alien numbers, required by the federal government.
  • Other municipalities are encountering similar problems, and complaints have been lodged against these stringent requirements.
  • Despite the hurdles, OMB is actively gathering the necessary information with the aim of submitting an application to access the funds.
  • The timeline for receiving the funds is uncertain, as it will depend on the federal review process after submission.
