Christopher Leon Johnson on Allegations of Corruption and Extortion in the Proposed North Brooklyn Business Improvement District (BID)

1:10:04

·

3 min

Christopher Leon Johnson testifies against the proposed establishment of the North Brooklyn Business Improvement District (BID), expressing concerns over alleged corruption and extortion.

  • Johnson accuses leadership, specifically Kenny Katie Dennis Horowitz, of planning to exploit the BID for personal gain.
  • He claims these leaders have connections to controversial figures and have admitted to extorting business owners.
  • Johnson warns that the establishment of this BID could negatively affect other BIDs and the overall business community in New York City.
  • He stresses the importance of the city council and small business chair to decline funding for the North Brooklyn BID due to these concerns.
