New York City Council

Committee on Finance

May 19, 2025

·

10:00 AM

6 hr 8 min

Summary

A hearing on the FY 2026 executive budget for the Administration for Children's Services (ACS) and Department of Youth and Community Development (DYCD).

Discussions focused on:

  • Funding for child care vouchers
  • Foster care
  • After-school programs
  • Runaway and homeless youth services
  • Youth employment programs.

Key issues included:

  • Potential shortfalls in child care voucher funding
  • New investments in after-school programs
  • Expansions to youth services
  • Concerns about rising costs for providers

Features testimony from ACS, DYCD, and the public.
