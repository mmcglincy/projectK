Council Member Althea Stevens questions DYCD on CMS contracts and Saturday Night Lights program

3:20:33

·

3 min

Council Member Althea Stevens engages in a Q&A session with DYCD officials, focusing on Crisis Management System (CMS) contracts and the Saturday Night Lights program. She inquires about contract renewals, provider numbers, and attendance rates, while emphasizing the need for flexibility in catchment areas due to changing crime patterns.

  • Stevens presses for specific attendance data for the Saturday Night Lights program, which DYCD officials promise to provide at a future hearing.
  • Discussion reveals there are currently 19 CMS providers citywide, with potential for changes based on crime data.
  • DYCD reports over 6,000 young people enrolled in Saturday Night Lights, with some sites exceeding the 30-person attendance target.
