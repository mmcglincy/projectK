New York City Council

Stated Meeting

Feb 27, 2025

·

01:30 PM

1 hr

Summary

The Stated Meeting happens every 2 weeks (4 weeks in summer), when the council votes on and introduces new legislation.

The council passed several bills including Intro 1061-A on improving reporting of sexual abuse in correctional facilities, Intro 565-A on protecting voter confidentiality for domestic violence survivors, and Intro 794 on requiring youth representation on the city youth board.

The council also approved a rezoning project in Brooklyn and introduced new legislation on protecting gender-affirming care facilities and tracking squatter data.
