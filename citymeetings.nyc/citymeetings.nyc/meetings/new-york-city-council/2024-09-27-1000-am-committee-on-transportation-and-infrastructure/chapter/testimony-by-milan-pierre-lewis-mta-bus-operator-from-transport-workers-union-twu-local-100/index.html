Testimony by Milan Pierre Lewis, MTA Bus Operator from Transport Workers Union (TWU) Local 100

3:14:24

·

123 sec

Milan Pierre Lewis, an MTA bus operator with 21 years of experience, testified about safety concerns related to commuter van operators in Jamaica, Queens. He highlighted the unsafe practices of van drivers and how they impact bus operations and passenger safety.

  • Van drivers often make sudden stops and pickups, creating hazards for bus operators and pedestrians
  • Presence of vans at Jamaica Center contributes to congestion and blocks bus lanes, affecting bus schedules
  • Unsafe driving practices of van operators, such as running red lights and picking up passengers in unsafe locations, pose risks to bus passengers and operators
