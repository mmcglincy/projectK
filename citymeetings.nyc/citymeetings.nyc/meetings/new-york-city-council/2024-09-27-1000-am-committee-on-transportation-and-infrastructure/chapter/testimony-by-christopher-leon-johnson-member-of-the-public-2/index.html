Testimony by Christopher Leon Johnson, Member of the Public

4:52:46

·

131 sec

Christopher Leon Johnson testifies in support of both the Independent Drivers Guild (IDG) and the Taxi Workers Alliance (TWA), urging them to stop conflicts and unite against unfair deactivation practices by ride-hailing companies like Uber and Lyft. He also touches on congestion pricing and political issues related to state legislation.

  • Criticizes Uber and Lyft for deactivating drivers without justification
  • Highlights potential conflicts of interest involving State Senator Jessica Ramos
  • Emphasizes the need for rank-and-file members of different driver groups to work together
