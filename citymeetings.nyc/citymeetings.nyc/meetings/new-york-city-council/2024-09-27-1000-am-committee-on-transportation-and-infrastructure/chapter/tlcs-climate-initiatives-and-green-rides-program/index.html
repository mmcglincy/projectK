AGENCY TESTIMONY

TLC's climate initiatives and Green Rides program

1:18:02

·

84 sec

Commissioner Do discusses TLC's efforts to support the city's climate goals, focusing on the Green Rides program and the electrification of the taxi/for-hire fleet. He highlights the progress made and the impact on charging infrastructure.

  • Released 'Electrification Emotion' report analyzing data from over 10,000 EVs performing trips
  • Green Rides program requires high-volume companies to dispatch exclusively to zero-emission or wheelchair accessible vehicles by 2030
  • Exceeding 2025 benchmarks with almost 20% of high-volume trips dispatched to EVs or wheelchair accessible vehicles in August
  • Over 90% of EV-only FHV licenses issued to individual drivers
  • Significant increase in charging infrastructure, including new fast charger stalls and planned expansion of curbside level 2 network
