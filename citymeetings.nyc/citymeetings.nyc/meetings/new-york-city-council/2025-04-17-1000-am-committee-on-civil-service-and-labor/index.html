New York City Council

Committee on Civil Service and Labor

Apr 17, 2025

·

10:00 AM

1 hr 52 min

Summary

A hearing on equitable representation in New York City's municipal workforce, focusing on pay equity, diversity, and inclusion initiatives.

Discussions covered gender pay disparities, occupational segregation, retention challenges, and efforts to improve diversity in high-paying positions.

The hearing also addressed proposed legislation on gender pay equity, unionization rights, telework expansion, and fair pay for musicians from streaming services.

Features testimony from the Department of Citywide Administrative Services (DCAS), Office of Labor Relations (OLR), Commission on Gender Equity (CGE), and the public.
