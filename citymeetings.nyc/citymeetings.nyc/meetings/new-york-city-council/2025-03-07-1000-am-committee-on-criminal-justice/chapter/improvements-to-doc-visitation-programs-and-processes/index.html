Improvements to DOC visitation programs and processes

2:48:33

·

3 min

Council Member Gale Brewer inquires about funding and improvements to visitation programs at DOC facilities. Multiple DOC officials, including First Deputy Commissioner Francis Torres and Deputy Commissioner James Boyd, provide updates on various initiatives.

  • The Children's Museum of Manhattan (CMOM) program is enhancing visitation areas on Rikers Island
  • DOC has hired visitor coordinators to engage family members and ensure structured activities
  • A wholesale assessment of the visit process is underway to streamline and improve efficiency
  • Plans include implementing a visitor scheduling system and encouraging use of free shuttle buses
  • The department recognizes that the current visit process takes too long and is working to address various factors causing delays
