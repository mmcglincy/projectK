New York City Council

Committee on Public Housing

Apr 15, 2025

·

10:00 AM

2 hr 37 min

Summary

A hearing on NYCHA's security guards, fire guards, and oversight of contractors.

Discussions focused on the Department of Investigation's findings of widespread neglect by contracted guards, NYCHA's failure to implement proper oversight measures, and plans for improvement.

Key issues included guard absenteeism, falsified time sheets, inadequate inspections, and lack of resident engagement in security decisions.

NYCHA officials acknowledged past failures and outlined steps to implement DOI recommendations, including geofencing technology and increased inspections. Council members pressed for better resident feedback mechanisms and improved communication about security measures.

Features testimony from the Department of Investigation (DOI), New York City Housing Authority (NYCHA), and the public.
