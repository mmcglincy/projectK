New York City Council

Committee on Immigration

Apr 15, 2025

·

10:00 AM

2 hr 52 min

Summary

A hearing on combating immigration services fraud in New York City, focusing on the city's efforts to prevent fraud and support victims.

Discussions included enforcement strategies, outreach efforts, and two proposed bills: Intro 205 to expand education about fraud, and Intro 980 to increase penalties for violations by immigration service providers.

Challenges such as 'fly-by-night' operations and underreporting due to fear and distrust were highlighted.

Features testimony from the Department of Consumer and Worker Protection (DCWP), various immigrant advocacy organizations, legal service providers, and the public.
