Public Advocate Jumaane Williams delivers remarks on the current state of immigration in New York City, criticizing Mayor Adams' policies and addressing the issue of immigration services fraud. He expresses concern over the mayor's alignment with ICE and the resulting environment of fear and disinformation that allows scammers to take advantage of vulnerable immigrants.

  • Williams accuses Mayor Adams of engaging in a quid pro quo arrangement and aligning with Trump's MAGA agenda
  • He highlights examples of immigration fraud, including impersonation of federal agents and solicitation of bribes
  • Williams calls for education about these scams, prosecution of offenders, and support for legal service providers
