PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Marc Valinoti, Assistant Director of Immigrant and DV Services at Northern Manhattan Improvement Corporation

2:35:51

·

150 sec

Marc Valinoti from the Northern Manhattan Improvement Corporation testified about common practices of immigration services fraud, highlighting the prevalence of simplistic schemes that promise employment authorization and paths to green cards. He emphasized the need for increased legal funding and education to combat these fraudulent practices.

  • Described fraudulent schemes involving false VAWA and asylum applications
  • Explained how temporary work cards issued during application processing contribute to the propagation of fraud
  • Stressed the importance of one-on-one conversations with clients to establish trust and prevent them from falling victim to fraudulent services
