New York City Council

Committee on Civil Service and Labor

Jan 27, 2025

·

01:00 PM

1 hr 47 min

Summary

A hearing on municipal workforce recruitment and retention efforts in New York City.

Discussions focused on:

  • Civil service examinations
  • Outreach strategies
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Collective bargaining initiatives
  • Challenges in various agencies including high vacancy rates
  • Low pay for certain positions
  • Alleged discriminatory practices.

The committee also discussed legislation to waive civil service exam fees for first-time applicants and high school students.

Features testimony from the Department of Citywide Administrative Services (DCAS), Office of Labor Relations (OLR), and the public, including union representatives and city workers.
