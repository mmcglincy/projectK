New York City Council

Subcommittee on Zoning and Franchises

Apr 23, 2025

·

10:00 AM

1 hr 50 min

Summary

A hearing on four zoning proposals in Brooklyn:

  • A mixed-use development at 2201-2227 Neptune Avenue in Coney Island
  • A 13-story residential building at 73-99 Empire Boulevard in Crown Heights
  • A 15-unit building at 19 Maspeth Avenue in East Williamsburg
  • A revised proposal for 166 Kings Highway in Bensonhurst

Features testimony from the applicants and the public.
