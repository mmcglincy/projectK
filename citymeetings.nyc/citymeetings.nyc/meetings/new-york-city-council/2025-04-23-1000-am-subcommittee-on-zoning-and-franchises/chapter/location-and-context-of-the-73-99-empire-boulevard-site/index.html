Location and context of the 73-99 Empire Boulevard site

0:30:35

·

157 sec

The development site is located at the corner of Empire Boulevard and McKeever Place in Crown Heights, Brooklyn. It is situated in a mixed-use area with good access to public transportation and nearby amenities.

  • Located at the corner of Empire Boulevard and McKeever Place
  • In Council Member Hudson's district, bordering Council Member Rita Joseph's district
  • Near Ebbets Field Apartments (25 stories tall)
  • One block from Jackie Robinson Playground and two blocks from Prospect Park
  • Well-served by public transit, including nearby subway stations and multiple bus routes
