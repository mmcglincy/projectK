Support and food assistance for new shelter residents

1:35:45

·

3 min

Council Member Ayala and Jill Berry from DSS discuss the process of supporting new shelter residents and connecting them to food assistance and benefits. Berry explains the efforts to improve this process and the resources available to shelter residents.

  • DSS is working with shelter providers to connect eligible residents to benefits
  • Shelters are provided with information about Access HRA and the provider portal
  • Emergency food pantries are expected to be maintained in family shelters with kitchens
  • Information about nearby community kitchens and food pantries should be provided to residents
