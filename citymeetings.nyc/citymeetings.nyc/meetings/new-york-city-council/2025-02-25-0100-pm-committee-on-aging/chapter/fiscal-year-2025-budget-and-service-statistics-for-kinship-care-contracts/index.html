Fiscal year 2025 budget and service statistics for kinship care contracts

0:46:29

·

131 sec

Council Member Hudson inquires about the fiscal year 2025 budget for kinship care contracts and the number of older adult caregivers served by contracted providers. Commissioner Cortés-Vázquez provides available data but needs to follow up on some information.

  • The commissioner does not have immediate access to the FY 2025 budget information for kinship care contracts
  • Data on the number of older adult caregivers served is provided for recent fiscal years:
  • FY 2024: 5,485 individuals
  • FY 2023: 5,015 individuals
  • FY 2022: 5,349 individuals
  • These numbers represent unduplicated clients, not the number of services provided
  • There is currently no wait list for older adult kinship caregiving services
