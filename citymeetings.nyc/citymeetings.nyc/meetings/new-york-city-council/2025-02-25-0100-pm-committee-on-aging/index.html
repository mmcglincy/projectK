New York City Council

Committee on Aging

Feb 25, 2025

·

01:00 PM

1 hr 31 min

Summary

A hearing on older adult kinship caregiving resources and Introduction 1184, which would establish a program to support older adults providing kinship care.

Discussions focused on:

  • The closure of the Grandparent Resource Center (GRC)
  • Integration of kinship care services into existing caregiving programs
  • Challenges faced by older kinship caregivers
  • The potential impact of Intro 1184

Features testimony from the Department for the Aging (NYC Aging) and the public.
