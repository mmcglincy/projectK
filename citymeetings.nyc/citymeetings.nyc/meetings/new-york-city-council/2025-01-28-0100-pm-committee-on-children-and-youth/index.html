New York City Council

Committee on Children and Youth

Jan 28, 2025

·

01:00 PM

2 hr 38 min

Summary

A hearing on supporting young victims of human trafficking, examining the city's progress in identifying and supporting trafficking survivors and strengthening protections for at-risk youth.

Discussions focused on:

  • Agency efforts to combat youth trafficking
  • Challenges in victim identification, providing trauma-informed care
  • Addressing trafficking hotspots
  • The need for better data collection and interagency coordination

The hearing also covered Intro 831, which would require NYPD to report annually on missing persons cases, including data on trafficking victims.

Features testimony from the Administration for Children's Services (ACS), Department of Youth and Community Development (DYCD), New York Police Department (NYPD), Mayor's Office to End Domestic and Gender-Based Violence (ENDGBV), and the public.
