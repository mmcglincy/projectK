Ground floor layout and supermarket plans

0:19:21

·

4 min

The ground floor of the proposed building is designed to accommodate a supermarket and address the challenges posed by the adjacent highway. The layout prioritizes accessibility and safety for pedestrians.

  • The building is shielded from the highway, which is about 50 feet high adjacent to the site
  • The supermarket has entry and exit points on the corner and on Mill Street
  • An extra-wide sidewalk (about 20 feet) enhances safety and accommodates high foot traffic
  • A significant freight area is included to service both the supermarket and second-floor uses
