PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Reem Ramadan, Director of Community Organizing and Public Advocacy at New York City Anti-Violence Project

4:38:56

·

164 sec

Reem Ramadan from the New York City Anti-Violence Project (AVP) testifies about the importance of supporting LGBTQ individuals and addressing hate violence. She emphasizes AVP's role as the only LGBTQ-specific victim services agency in the city and calls for increased funding and support from the City Council.

  • AVP provides services including a 24/7 bilingual hotline, free counseling, legal services, and community organizing for LGBTQ survivors of violence.
  • Ramadan highlights the disproportionate rate of hate violence against LGBTQ people, especially those with intersecting marginalized identities.
  • She requests increased funding for the Path Forward Initiative and the Hate Violence Prevention Initiative, as well as reallocation of funds from law enforcement to community-based organizations.
