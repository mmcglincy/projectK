New York City Council

Subcommittee on Zoning and Franchises

Apr 17, 2025

·

11:00 AM

20 min

Summary

A hearing on a proposed rezoning for 102-51 Queens Boulevard in Forest Hills, Queens.

The proposal seeks to rezone the site from R7-1 to R8X C2-4 to allow development of a 17-story mixed-use building with 216 apartments, including 54 affordable units.

Discussions focused on the project's potential to activate an underdeveloped stretch of Queens Boulevard, affordable housing creation, labor commitments, and design considerations.

Features testimony from the applicant team (QBM Properties LLC) and SEIU Local 32BJ.
