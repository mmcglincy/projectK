Council Member Restler advocates for accelerated timeline and increased partnerships

1:51:38

·

3 min

Council Member Restler emphasizes the need for new funding strategies to accelerate the 30-year plan and inquires about partnerships for green infrastructure projects, particularly in schools and parks within his district.

  • Restler stresses the urgency of addressing flooding issues and suggests aiming for a 10-12 year plan instead of 30 years.
  • He acknowledges the challenges, including lack of federal cooperation, but pushes for better organization and strategy.
  • Restler asks for advice on advocating for more projects in flood zones within his district.
  • The discussion touches on existing partnerships with the Trust for Public Land and the Department of Education for school-based projects.
