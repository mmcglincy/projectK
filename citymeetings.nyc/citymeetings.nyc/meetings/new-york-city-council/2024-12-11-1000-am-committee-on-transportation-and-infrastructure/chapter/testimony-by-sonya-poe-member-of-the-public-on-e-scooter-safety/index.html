Testimony by Sonya Poe, Member of the Public, on E-Scooter Safety

4:21:42

·

102 sec

Sonya Poe shares her personal experience of being hit by an e-scooter, resulting in severe injuries to her and her husband. She advocates for stricter rules and consequences for e-vehicle users to improve pedestrian safety in New York City.

  • Poe's husband suffered a traumatic brain injury, spending 10 days in intensive care
  • Poe herself sustained injuries including a broken nose, shoulder, and foot
  • She emphasizes the need for responsibility and consequences for e-vehicle users who cause harm
