PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Florence Friedman, Member of the Public, on E-Bike Safety and Regulation

6:13:13

·

124 sec

Florence Friedman recounts her personal experience of being hit by an e-bike on the sidewalk, resulting in a broken wrist. She advocates for stronger accountability measures for e-bike riders, comparing the situation to car regulations and emphasizing the need for license plates on e-bikes.

  • Friedman argues that education alone is insufficient for ensuring compliance with traffic laws
  • She criticizes the current state of Manhattan's streets and sidewalks, calling them the "wild west"
  • Friedman expresses support for "Priscilla's law" and questions why it hasn't been passed yet
