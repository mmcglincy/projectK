PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Christopher Leon Johnson, Member of the Public, on E-bike Regulation

3:18:23

·

128 sec

Christopher Leon Johnson testifies in support of Intro 606, which proposes e-bike regulation. He strongly criticizes organizations opposing the bill, particularly Transportation Alternatives and Open Plans, accusing them of fearmongering and racism. Johnson emphasizes that the bill is not racist and argues for the necessity of e-bike regulation in New York City.

  • Argues against claims that the bill will bring back "stop and frisk" policies
  • Expresses support for NYC EBSA (E-bike Safety Alliance) and their efforts to regulate e-bikes
  • Criticizes Worker Justice Project for alleged discrimination against African Americans
