New York City Council

Subcommittee on Landmarks, Public Sitings and Dispositions

Mar 11, 2025

·

11:30 AM

37 min

Summary

A vote and hearing.

The committee passes several land use items including the Beacon project in East Harlem, a UDAAP project on Jerome Avenue in the Bronx, and a project on Davidson Avenue in the Bronx.

The hearing portion focused on a proposal for a new 547-seat primary school in the Bronx and the West 128th-129th Street Cluster project in Harlem, which aims to rehabilitate four buildings for affordable homeownership.

Discussions included school overcrowding, resident relocation plans, and the timeline for project completion.

Features testimony from the School Construction Authority (SCA), Department of Housing Preservation and Development (HPD), West Harlem Group Assistance Inc., and the public.
