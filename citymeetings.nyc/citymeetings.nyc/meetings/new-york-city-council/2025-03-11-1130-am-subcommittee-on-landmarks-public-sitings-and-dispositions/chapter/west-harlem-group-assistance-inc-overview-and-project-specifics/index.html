PRESENTATION

West Harlem Group Assistance Inc. overview and project specifics

0:28:25

·

3 min

Donald Notice, Executive Director of West Harlem Group Assistance Inc., provides an overview of the organization and additional details about the West 128-129th Street Cluster project.

  • West Harlem Group Assistance is a 54-year-old not-for-profit community development corporation.
  • The organization has extensive experience with HPD projects and affordable housing initiatives in Harlem.
  • Current projects include two NYCHA conversions totaling over 2,800 units.
  • The West 128-129th Street Cluster project includes 96 units, with 38 for existing residents and 58 for first-time homebuyers.
  • The project involves a gut renovation and is scheduled to close in summer 2025.
  • Webster Bank will be the construction lender for the project.
