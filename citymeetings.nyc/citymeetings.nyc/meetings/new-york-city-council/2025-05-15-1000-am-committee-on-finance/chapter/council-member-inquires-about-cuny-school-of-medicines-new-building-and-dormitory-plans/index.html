1:50:40

·

3 min

Council Member Eric Dinowitz inquires about the status of acquiring a new building and dormitory for the CUNY School of Medicine. Chancellor Félix Matos Rodríguez provides updates on the preliminary studies conducted and the current focus on the school's transition to independence.

  • The CUNY team has done a preliminary study on space needs for an independent school building.
  • There's currently no specific location identified for the new building.
  • The administration is hoping to attract philanthropic dollars for the project.
  • $4 million has been allocated in the state budget for the medical school, though it's acknowledged as insufficient.
