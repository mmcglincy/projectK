0:21:41

Council Member Yusef Salaam inquires about the potential impact of the City of Yes zoning for housing opportunity on the Malcolm Shabazz Harlem Plaza project. Peter Procida from Procida Construction and Development Group explains the possible changes and timeline considerations.

  • If City of Yes passes, the project could increase from 109 to 123 units
  • The AMI mix and unit type percentages would remain proportionally the same
  • The approval of City of Yes is not expected to significantly impact the project's timeline or financial planning
