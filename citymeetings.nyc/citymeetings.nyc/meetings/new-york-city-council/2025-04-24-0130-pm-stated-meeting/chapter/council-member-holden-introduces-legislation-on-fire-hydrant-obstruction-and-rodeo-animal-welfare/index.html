Council Member Holden introduces legislation on fire hydrant obstruction and rodeo animal welfare

1:28:17

·

81 sec

Council Member Robert F. Holden introduces two pieces of legislation: Intro 1255 and Intro 1256. The first bill aims to strengthen enforcement against obstructing fire hydrants, while the second seeks to ban inhumane practices in rodeos within New York City.

  • Intro 1256 addresses the dangerous practice of obstructing fire hydrants, including covering them with garbage bins or other objects.
  • Intro 1255 takes a stand against animal abuse in rodeos, banning practices like calf roping, electric prods, and flank straps.
  • Holden emphasizes these as common-sense quality of life bills that reflect a commitment to public safety and compassion.
