Sharon Brown, a member of the public, provides testimony on various education-related topics, focusing primarily on the challenges faced by transfer students and the need for better information about scholarships and funding. She also touches on controversial topics such as prayer in schools and the Israel-Palestine situation.

  • Discusses personal experiences as a transfer student at multiple institutions, including Marymount Manhattan College
  • Emphasizes the need for better guidance on scholarship and funding options for students
  • Advocates for the return of prayer and religious education in schools, specifically mentioning Judaism and Christianity
