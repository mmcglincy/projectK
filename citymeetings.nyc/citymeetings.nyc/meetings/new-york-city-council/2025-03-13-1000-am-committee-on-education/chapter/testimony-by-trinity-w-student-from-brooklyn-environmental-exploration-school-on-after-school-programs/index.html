Testimony by Trinity W., Student from Brooklyn Environmental Exploration School, on After School Programs

8:58:37

·

3 min

Trinity W., an eighth-grade student, passionately advocates for the importance of after-school programs, emphasizing their role in providing a safe space for students to grow, learn, and socialize beyond regular school hours. She shares her personal experiences with various after-school activities, including STEM, cosmetology, sports, leadership, art, and STEP, highlighting how each has contributed to her personal growth and development.

  • Trinity describes how after-school programs offer opportunities for students to engage with friends and learn new skills in a structured environment.
  • She recounts her proud moment of being nominated to play in a girls' football league at MetLife Stadium, which boosted her confidence and challenged gender stereotypes.
  • The testimony underscores the value of after-school programs in providing diverse experiences, including field trips, that open students' eyes to new opportunities and potential career paths.
