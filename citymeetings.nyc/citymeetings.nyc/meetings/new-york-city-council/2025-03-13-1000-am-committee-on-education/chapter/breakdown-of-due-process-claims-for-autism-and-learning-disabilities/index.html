Breakdown of due process claims for autism and learning disabilities

4:22:36

·

133 sec

Liz Vladek provides a detailed breakdown of due process claims for students with autism and learning disabilities (including dyslexia) over the past three school years.

  • Dyslexia is not a separate disability classification on IEPs or IESPs, but falls under learning disabilities
  • School year 2021-2022: 1,827 claims for autism, 1,286 for learning disabilities
  • School year 2022-2023: 2,090 claims for autism, 1,271 for learning disabilities
  • School year 2023-2024: 2,067 claims for autism, 1,450 for learning disabilities
  • These numbers include some public school cases, not only tuition issues
