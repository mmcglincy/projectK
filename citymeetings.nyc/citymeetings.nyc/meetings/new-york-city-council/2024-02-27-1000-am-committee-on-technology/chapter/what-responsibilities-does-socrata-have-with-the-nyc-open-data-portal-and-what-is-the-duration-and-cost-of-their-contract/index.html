QUESTION

What responsibilities does Socrata have with the NYC Open Data portal, and what is the duration and cost of their contract?

0:24:13

·

115 sec

Socrata is annually contracted for hosting and maintaining the NYC Open Data portal, with a recent annual cost close to $400,000.

  • Socrata has a longstanding annual contract with NYC since 2012 for services related to the Open Data portal.
  • The company hosts public data on their cloud platform, ensuring data accessibility via the internet.
  • Socrata provides user experience tools and maintains the portal, including the documentation of the API utilized for data interaction.
  • The cost of Socrata's services for the recent year is approximately $400,000.
  • While Socrata is tasked with the portal's technical maintenance, the NYC Office of Technology and Innovation (OTI) is responsible for content management and presentation on the portal.
