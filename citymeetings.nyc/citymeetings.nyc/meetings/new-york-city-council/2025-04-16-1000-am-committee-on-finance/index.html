New York City Council

Committee on Finance

Apr 16, 2025

·

10:00 AM

3 hr 22 min

Summary

A hearing on the impact of federal funding cuts on New York City's budget, services, and nonprofit organizations.

Discussions focused on:

  • The potential effects on vulnerable populations
  • The need for contingency planning and increased reserves
  • Financial strain on service providers
  • Coordinated advocacy at federal, state, and local levels

Features testimony from the Independent Budget Office (IBO), Citizens Budget Commission (CBC), various nonprofit organizations, and the public.
