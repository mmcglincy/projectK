PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Tatiana Arguello, Executive Director of Nonprofit Staten Island

2:12:15

·

3 min

Tatiana Arguello, Executive Director of Nonprofit Staten Island, testifies about the underfunding of Staten Island nonprofits and the potential impact of federal funding cuts. She emphasizes the disproportionate allocation of resources to Staten Island compared to its population and the critical services provided by local organizations.

  • Staten Island receives only 2% of the city speaker's fund despite making up 6% of the city's population
  • Nonprofit Staten Island lost 10% of its budget in fiscal year 2025
  • The organization serves residents from all boroughs, not just Staten Island
  • Arguello calls for equitable distribution of city funds and increased investment in Staten Island nonprofits
