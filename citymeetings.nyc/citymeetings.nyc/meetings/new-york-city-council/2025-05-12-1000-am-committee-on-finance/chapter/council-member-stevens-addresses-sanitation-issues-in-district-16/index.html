Council Member Stevens addresses sanitation issues in District 16

1:47:19

·

4 min

Council Member Althea V. Stevens raises concerns about sanitation issues in District 16 of the West Bronx, highlighting the lack of garbage cans, ineffective street cleaning, and illegal dumping. She emphasizes the need for equitable distribution of sanitation services and requests immediate action from the Department of Sanitation to address these problems.

  • Stevens points out the disparity in garbage can distribution between her district and other areas of the city, such as Fifth Avenue.
  • She mentions issues with street cleaning enforcement and garbage left behind after collection.
  • The council member expresses frustration with having to use discretionary funding to replace removed garbage cans and requests a plan to restore them without additional cost to her office.
