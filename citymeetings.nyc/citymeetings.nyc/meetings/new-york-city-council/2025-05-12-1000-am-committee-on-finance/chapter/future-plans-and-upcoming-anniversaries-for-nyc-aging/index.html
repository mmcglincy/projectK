Future plans and upcoming anniversaries for NYC Aging

3:06:38

·

3 min

Commissioner Cortés-Vázquez concludes her testimony by highlighting upcoming anniversaries and future plans for NYC Aging. She emphasizes the department's ongoing commitment to serving older New Yorkers and invites participation in upcoming events.

  • July 14, 2025 marks the 60th anniversary of the Older Americans Act.
  • August 14, 2025 is the 90th anniversary of the Social Security Act.
  • November 4, 2025 will be NYC Aging's 50th anniversary.
  • The department plans to celebrate these milestones and showcase their service to older New Yorkers.
  • An invitation is extended for the National Older Adult Health and Fitness Day on May 28 in Foley Square.
  • The commissioner expresses gratitude for the council's dedication and advocacy for older adults.
