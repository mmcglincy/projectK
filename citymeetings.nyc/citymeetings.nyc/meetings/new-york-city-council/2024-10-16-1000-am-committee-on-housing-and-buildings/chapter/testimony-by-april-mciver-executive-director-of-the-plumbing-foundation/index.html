PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by April McIver, Executive Director of the Plumbing Foundation

1:43:41

·

3 min

April McIver, representing the Plumbing Foundation, testified in support of Intro 429, focusing on revisions to Local Law 152 regarding gas piping inspections. She addressed concerns raised by the Department of Buildings and clarified several points about the proposed changes.

  • McIver emphasized that the bill would streamline the process for building owners to obtain certifications related to gas piping.
  • She explained that the 5-year experience requirement for inspectors aligns with existing DOB rules.
  • McIver clarified that the bill would allow inspections of commercial tenant spaces while excluding residential dwelling spaces.
  • She urged the council to pass the legislation, noting that the next cycle for Local Law 152 begins on January 1st.
