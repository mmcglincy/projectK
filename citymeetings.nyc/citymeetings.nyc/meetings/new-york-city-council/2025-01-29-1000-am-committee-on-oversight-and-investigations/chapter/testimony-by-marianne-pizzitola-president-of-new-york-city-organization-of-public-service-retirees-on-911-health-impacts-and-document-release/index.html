Testimony by Marianne Pizzitola, President of New York City Organization of Public Service Retirees, on 9/11 Health Impacts and Document Release

2:25:55

·

123 sec

Marianne Pizzitola, representing 250,000 municipal retirees, testified about the health impacts on 9/11 survivors and first responders, criticizing the EPA's false claims about air safety and calling for the release of documents from past mayoral administrations. She highlighted the ongoing struggles of civilian 9/11 survivors and criticized attempts to change retirees' healthcare plans.

  • Emphasized the need for transparency regarding what past mayors knew about 9/11 environmental hazards
  • Discussed the challenges faced by civilian 9/11 survivors in accessing health screenings and care
  • Criticized recent attempts to move retirees to a Medicare Advantage Plan, viewing it as compounding the initial betrayal
