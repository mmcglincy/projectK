Relationships with other oversight agencies and community outreach

0:22:48

·

127 sec

Commissioner Strauber describes OIG-NYPD's collaborations with other oversight agencies and its efforts to engage with the community.

  • OIG-NYPD has established working relationships with other police oversight agencies, including the CCRB, Internal Affairs Bureau, and the office of the community liaison
  • The office participates in community forums focused on police accountability
  • Regular meetings are held with community policing advocates to understand community concerns
  • An outreach director is dedicated to connecting with the community through events across New York City
  • These relationships and outreach efforts help inform OIG-NYPD's work and maintain a local resource network in police oversight
