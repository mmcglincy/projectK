QUESTION

Who mandates the examination requirement for civil service positions, DCAS or the state?

0:15:52

·

98 sec

The state constitution governs the city's civil service system, imposing a merit and fitness based system for over 80% of positions.

  • The state constitution mandates a merit and fitness based system for city civil service positions.
  • This system aims to create a fair and competitive framework, evaluating job performance while minimizing bias and discrimination.
  • Certain titles may be designated as noncompetitive or exempt, such as 'agency attorney', which relies on bar admission instead of an exam.
  • The designation of noncompetitive or exempt titles requires demonstrating to the state civil service commission that competitive testing is impractical.
