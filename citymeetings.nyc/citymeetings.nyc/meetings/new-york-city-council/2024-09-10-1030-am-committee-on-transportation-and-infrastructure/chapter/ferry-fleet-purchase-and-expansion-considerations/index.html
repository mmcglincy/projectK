Ferry fleet purchase and expansion considerations

0:34:55

·

3 min

EDC officials discuss the current status of the NYC Ferry fleet and considerations for potential expansion or changes to the fleet.

  • There are no current plans for additional vessel purchases
  • The existing fleet of two vessel sizes (150 and 350 passenger capacity) provides flexibility for different routes and conditions
  • Stronger vessels are used for routes with more open water, while larger vessels are used on high-demand routes
