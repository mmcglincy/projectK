PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Wayne Richards, Member of Public on NYC Ferry and Infrastructure

2:49:16

·

4 min

Wayne Richards presents a design concept for infrastructure development in the Rockaways, emphasizing the need for water taxi service along the peninsula. He argues that the current transportation options are insufficient for the growing population and that ferry service would improve access and economic opportunities in Far Rockaway.

  • Proposes creating a promenade with water taxi stops along the bay side of the Rockaways
  • Highlights the need for additional transportation options due to 10,000 new housing units in Far Rockaway
  • Suggests extending water taxi service from Manhattan to Inwood and down to Rockaway Turnpike Boulevard to improve access and tourism
