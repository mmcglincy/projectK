Brian Ellicott Cook, representing SAGE and SAGE Vets, testified about the challenges faced by LGBTQ+ older adult veterans and requested continued support for their legal services initiative. He highlighted the stagnant budget for the Department of Veterans Services (DVS) and emphasized the importance of supporting LGBTQ+ veterans in the current political climate.

  • SAGE Vets is the only program in NYC specifically supporting LGBTQ+ older adult veterans
  • 56% of LGBTQ+ veterans in NYC are over the age of 50
  • Cook requested a $100,000 renewal for the legal services initiative in the FY26 budget
