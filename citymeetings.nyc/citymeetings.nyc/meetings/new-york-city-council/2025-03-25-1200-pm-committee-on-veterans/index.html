New York City Council

Committee on Veterans

Mar 25, 2025

·

12:00 PM

2 hr 37 min

Summary

A hearing on the Department of Veterans Services (DVS) fiscal year 2026 preliminary budget.

Discussions focused on:

  • The agency's $5.9 million budget (a 16.1% decrease from FY2025)
  • Concerns about inadequate funding
  • Priorities including data collection
  • Support for transitioning service members
  • Housing assistance
  • Claims processing.

The hearing also addressed challenges with federal VA funding cuts, the implementation of the Dwyer Veterans Peer Support Program, and proposed property tax exemptions for Cold War veterans.

Features testimony from the Department of Veterans Services (DVS) and the public.
