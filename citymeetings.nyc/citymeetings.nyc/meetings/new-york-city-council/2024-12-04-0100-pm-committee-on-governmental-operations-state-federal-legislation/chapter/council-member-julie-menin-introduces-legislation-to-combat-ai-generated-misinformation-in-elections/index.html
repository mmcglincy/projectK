Council Member Julie Menin introduces legislation to combat AI-generated misinformation in elections

0:07:43

·

123 sec

Council Member Julie Menin addresses the growing threat of AI-generated misinformation in elections and introduces Intro 293, a bill aimed at prohibiting deceptive AI-generated content in local elections. She emphasizes the need for proactive regulation to protect democratic institutions and ensure election integrity.

  • Menin cites recent examples of AI-generated misinformation in elections, including a robocall impersonating President Biden and an audio clip in a New York State Assembly race.
  • The proposed legislation, Intro 293, would cover primary, special, and general elections for various city offices and impose penalties for non-compliance.
  • Menin stresses the urgency of addressing this issue before a serious incident occurs and calls for safeguards to protect democracy.
