AGENCY TESTIMONY

Initiatives for the 2025 election

0:22:02

·

154 sec

The CFB outlines several initiatives planned for the 2025 election, focusing on voter education and outreach. These initiatives aim to inform New Yorkers about the matching funds program and ranked choice voting (RCV).

  • Comprehensive voter education on the matching funds program, highlighting its 8-to-1 match benefit
  • Collaboration with the Board of Elections and community partners to educate voters on RCV
  • Production and distribution of voter guides, printed and digital educational materials in 13 mandated languages
  • Targeted multimedia advertising campaign to reach all New Yorkers, with a focus on priority communities
