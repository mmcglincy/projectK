Christopher Leon Johnson provides testimony on various city issues, including the Delirista hub, bike lanes, and community land trusts. He expresses strong opinions about Mayor Eric Adams' tenure and calls for investigations into certain community organizations.

  • Advocates for a Delirista hub outside City Hall and increased protections for Deliristas
  • Criticizes the handling of community land trusts in East New York, particularly at 248 Arlington
  • Calls for investigations into East New York CLT and Cypress Hills organization, citing alleged mistreatment of residents
