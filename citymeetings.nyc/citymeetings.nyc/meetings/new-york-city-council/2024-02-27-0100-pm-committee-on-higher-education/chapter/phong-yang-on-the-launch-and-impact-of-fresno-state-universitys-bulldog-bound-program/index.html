Phong Yang on the Launch and Impact of Fresno State University's Bulldog Bound Program

0:07:40

·

5 min

Phong Yang discusses Fresno State University's Bulldog Bound, a program offering direct admissions from high schools, emphasizing its partnership model and early impacts.

  • The Bulldog Bound program facilitates a direct admission pathway for high school students to Fresno State, involving a partnership between the university, school districts, students, and their parents.
  • Begins engaging students as early as 9th grade, providing them with a Fresno State ID, access to university facilities, and participation in college readiness activities.
  • A significant proportion of Fresno State students are first-generation, underscoring the value of the program in educating both students and their parents on college preparation.
  • Early results show increased engagement from rural schools, a 98% admission rate through the program, and shifts in participant demographics.
  • Future plans include enabling dual enrollment and making participants eligible for the new Bulldog scholarship, further enhancing collaboration between Fresno State, school districts, and other community stakeholders.
