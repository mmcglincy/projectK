How are New York City public schools and the City University of New York (CUNY) supporting students with the FAFSA process?

1:05:57

·

4 min

New York City Public Schools and the City University of New York (CUNY) detail their efforts to support students navigating the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) process amidst challenges.

  • Professional development and ongoing training for school counselors and staff focus on navigating the financial aid process.
  • The redesigned FAFSA application presents challenges, such as late availability and technical glitches, requiring diligent work and collaboration with community partners.
  • New York City Public Schools host FAFSA nights and use texting campaigns to update students directly.
  • CUNY admits the FAFSA delay is problematic, prompting various initiatives such as hiring work-study students for citywide financial aid assistance and training admissions staff.
  • Collaboration between New York City Public Schools, CUNY, and donors is underway to offer scaled services throughout the summer.
