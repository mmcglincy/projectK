Testimony by Daniella Mendoza on MSK Pavilion Project's Impact on PS 183 Children

1:56:42

·

3 min

Daniella Mendoza expresses concerns about the Memorial Sloan Kettering (MSK) Pavilion project's impact on children attending PS 183. She emphasizes the need for strict environmental protections and mitigation measures to safeguard the health and well-being of the young students during construction.

  • Highlights the unique vulnerabilities of children to environmental hazards from demolition and construction projects
  • Calls for MSK to provide specific protections such as a canopy for the play space, medical-grade air filtration systems, and deep cleaning services for the school
  • Urges consideration of the project's effects on children's quality of life and learning environment
