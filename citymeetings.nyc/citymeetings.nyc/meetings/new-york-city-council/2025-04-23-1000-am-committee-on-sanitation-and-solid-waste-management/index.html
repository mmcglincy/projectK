New York City Council

Committee on Sanitation and Solid Waste Management

Apr 23, 2025

·

10:00 AM

2 hr 47 min

Summary

A hearing on the implementation of New York City's commercial waste zones (CWZ) program.

Discussions focused on:

  • The slow rollout of the program, with only 1 out of 20 zones implemented so far
  • Safety and enforcement measures
  • Data collection challenges
  • Impacts on businesses
  • Proposed legislation to expand organic waste separation requirements and establish a grease disposal tracking system.

Concerns were raised about the pace of implementation, market consolidation effects, and the need for more resources to speed up the process.

The hearing also addressed the recent decision to pause fines for residential composting violations in smaller buildings.

Features testimony from the Department of Sanitation (DSNY), the Business Integrity Commission (BIC), and the public.
