PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by David Biderman, President of Biderman Consulting, on Commercial Waste Zone Safety

2:41:47

·

169 sec

David Biderman, President of Biderman Consulting, provided an update on safety aspects of the Commercial Waste Zone (CWZ) program. He highlighted his involvement in the CWZ Safety Task Force, safety training for awardees and BIC licensees, and strategic guidance for waste management businesses.

  • Biderman reported progress in the CWZ Safety Task Force, including regular meetings and collaboration between DSNY, BIC, DOT, and industry representatives.
  • He noted improvements in worker safety, with no private sector licensee worker fatalities in recent years, attributing this to increased focus on safety by most companies.
  • Biderman raised concerns about e-bikes as a significant safety threat to waste collection workers, calling for increased enforcement of traffic laws for e-bike riders.
