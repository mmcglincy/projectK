New York City Council

Subcommittee on Zoning and Franchises

Apr 29, 2025

·

10:00 AM

3 hr 35 min

Summary

A hearing on the Western Rail Yards modification proposal in Manhattan.

Discussions focused on Related Companies' plans to modify the 2009 development agreement, including:

  • Increasing housing units
  • Reconfiguring building sites and open space
  • Adding a potential casino option
  • Using a new financing mechanism (PILOT) to fund infrastructure costs

Features testimony from Related Companies, community board representatives, local residents, business owners, union representatives, and the public.
