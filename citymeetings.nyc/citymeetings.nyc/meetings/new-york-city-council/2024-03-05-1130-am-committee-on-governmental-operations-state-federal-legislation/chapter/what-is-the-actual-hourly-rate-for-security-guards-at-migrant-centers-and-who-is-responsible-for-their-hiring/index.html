QUESTION

What is the actual hourly rate for security guards at migrant centers, and who is responsible for their hiring?

0:59:31

·

3 min

Council Member Paladino raises concerns about the reported hourly wages of security guards at migrant centers and queries about the companies providing these services.

  • Council Member Paladino questions the accuracy of reported high hourly wages for security guards at migrant centers.
  • Roman Gofman, from the Department of Citywide Administrative Services (DCAS), clarifies that the contract with Garner Environmental does not include security services.
  • Security services at migrant centers are reportedly contracted separately by other agencies.
  • Concerns are raised regarding the quality of services provided by the hired security guards and the discrepancy in reported hourly wages.
  • DCAS Commissioner Dawn M. Pinnock indicates a willingness to share these concerns with the appropriate agencies.
