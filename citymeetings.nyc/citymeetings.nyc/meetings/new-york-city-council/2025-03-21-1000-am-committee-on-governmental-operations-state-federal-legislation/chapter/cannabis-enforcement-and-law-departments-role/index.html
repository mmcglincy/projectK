Cannabis enforcement and Law Department's role

3:11:17

·

3 min

Council Member Brewer inquires about the Law Department's role in cannabis enforcement, including new positions, funding, and anticipated challenges. The Corporation Counsel and Chief Assistant for Regulatory Law and Policy provide detailed explanations of their involvement.

  • The Law Department has allocated funding for 25 cannabis enforcement positions across multiple divisions.
  • Various divisions are involved, including legal counsel, administrative law, regulatory litigation, tort, special federal litigation, and appeals.
  • The department has been involved in numerous Article 78 proceedings related to cannabis enforcement.
  • Revenue and expenditure information related to enforcement is handled by partner agencies like the sheriff's office and police department.
