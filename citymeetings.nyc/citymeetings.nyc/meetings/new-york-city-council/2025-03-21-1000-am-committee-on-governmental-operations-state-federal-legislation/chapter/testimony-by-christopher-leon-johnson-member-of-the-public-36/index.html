PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Christopher Leon Johnson, Member of the Public

5:22:09

·

3 min

Christopher Leon Johnson provides passionate testimony on several issues related to public facilities and street vendors in New York City. He focuses on bathroom access in DCAS buildings, the rights of street vendors, and the need for funding for workers' and street vendor projects.

  • Advocates for open bathroom access in DCAS buildings, especially for delivery workers and pregnant women
  • Criticizes the disparity in treatment between street vendors and phone company representatives outside government buildings
  • Calls for increased funding (over $1 million) for workers' project and street vendor project
  • Urges the council to investigate and address these issues, particularly by questioning DCAS Commissioner Louis Molina
