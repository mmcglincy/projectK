Council Member Paladino discusses Parks Department budget, project timelines, and Flushing Airport development

1:53:38

·

4 min

Council Member Vickie Paladino addresses the Parks Department, expressing frustration with budget allocation, project timelines, and staffing issues. She proposes breaking down the budget into 51 districts for more efficient fund allocation and discusses concerns about the Flushing Airport development project.

  • Suggests allocating parks budget specifically for each borough to fund projects with clear timelines
  • Expresses concern over long project timelines (7-9 years) and their impact on constituents
  • Requests focus on the Flushing Airport development, citing environmental concerns and the need for Parks Department involvement
