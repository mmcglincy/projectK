Testimony by Roxanne Delgado, Founder of Friends of Pelham Parkway on Parks Department Issues

2:47:33

·

3 min

Roxanne Delgado, founder of Friends of Pelham Parkway, testifies about various issues related to the Parks Department, focusing on problems in the Pelham Parkway Greenway in The Bronx. She highlights concerns about funding, trash management, barbecuing rules, tree maintenance, and retaliation for complaints.

  • Calls for Mayor Adams to fulfill his promise of 1% funding for the Parks Department
  • Criticizes the removal of trash cans and lack of enforcement regarding barbecuing in the Greenway
  • Expresses concern about inadequate tree maintenance and the planting of ornamental trees that don't benefit urban wildlife or address emissions
