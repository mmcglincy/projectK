Council Member Farías questions Randy Mastro about his support for Ninfa Segarra, who held controversial positions on education and LGBTQ+ issues during her time on the Board of Education and as Deputy Mayor for Education under Giuliani. Mastro defends his association with Segarra.

  • Mastro disputes the characterization of Segarra and declines to comment on the personal vilification of potential witnesses
