Impact of the Me Too movement on Bridget Anne Kelly's experience

9:09:15

·

160 sec

Council Member Amanda Farías asks Bridget Anne Kelly if her experience with Randy Mastro and the Bridgegate scandal would have been viewed differently if it had occurred after the Me Too movement. Kelly responds that it likely would have been, as the movement has made people more comfortable speaking out about sexism and discrimination in the workplace.

  • Kelly discusses how the silence of her colleagues at the time was deafening and how the Me Too movement has given women more confidence to come forward.
  • She expresses regret that the incident happened at all, stating it ruined her life.
  • Kelly emphasizes that her personal relationships had nothing to do with the Bridgegate incident, contrary to what Mastro's report suggested.
