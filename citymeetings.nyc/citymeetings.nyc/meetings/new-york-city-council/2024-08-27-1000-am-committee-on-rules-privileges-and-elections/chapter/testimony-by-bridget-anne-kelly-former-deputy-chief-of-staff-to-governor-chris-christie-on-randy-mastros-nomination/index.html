Testimony by Bridget Anne Kelly, Former Deputy Chief of Staff to Governor Chris Christie, on Randy Mastro's Nomination

8:57:01

·

4 min

Bridget Anne Kelly, former Deputy Chief of Staff to New Jersey Governor Chris Christie, testifies against Randy Mastro's nomination as Corporation Counsel for New York City. She criticizes Mastro's role in the Bridgegate scandal, accusing him of sexism, unethical behavior, and damaging her career and reputation through his investigation and report.

  • Kelly describes Mastro as a "cunning and ruthless politician" who will do anything for his principals, including threatening or scapegoating others.
  • She warns that confirming Mastro would send a negative message to women who have been scapegoated to protect powerful men.
  • Kelly urges the committee to oppose Mastro's nomination to help restore trust in government and end divisive political tactics.
