PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Judy Wessler, Retired Former Director & Co-founder of Commission on the Public's Health System (CPHS)

2:24:54

·

140 sec

Judy Wessler, retired director of CPHS, testified about the ongoing issue of hospital closures in medically underserved, low-income, and often communities of color. She emphasized the worsening situation over the past 30 years and presented evidence of the impact of these closures.

  • Wessler shared a report on the impact of Saint Vincent's Hospital closure on Chelsea residents
  • She presented maps illustrating the pattern of hospital closings in underserved communities
  • Wessler urged the City Council to take a more activist position, suggesting the revival of a task force on hospital closings
