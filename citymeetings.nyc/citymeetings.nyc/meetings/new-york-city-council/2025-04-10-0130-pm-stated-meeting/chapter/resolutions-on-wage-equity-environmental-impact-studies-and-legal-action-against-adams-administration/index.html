Resolutions on wage equity, environmental impact studies, and legal action against Adams administration

0:16:42

·

3 min

Speaker Adams introduces several resolutions for voting, covering a range of topics including wage equity, environmental impact studies, and legal action against the Adams administration.

  • Resolution 333-A calls for eliminating sub-minimum wage based on disability or age
  • Resolutions 59-A and 187 call for environmental impact studies on MTA projects
  • Resolution 729 condemns President Trump's targeting of diversity, equity, and inclusion programs
  • Resolution 836 authorizes legal action against the Adams administration for violating sanctuary city laws
