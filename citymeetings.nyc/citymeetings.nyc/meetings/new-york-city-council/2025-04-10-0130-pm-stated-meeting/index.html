New York City Council

Stated Meeting

Apr 10, 2025

·

01:30 PM

1 hr 14 min

Summary

The Stated Meeting happens every 2 weeks (4 weeks in summer), when the council votes on and introduces new legislation.

The council passes several bills enhancing transparency on NYPD surveillance technology use, improving tree maintenance processes, creating a citywide public bathroom network, and waiving civil service exam fees for certain applicants. The council also authorizes legal action against the Adams administration regarding sanctuary city policies.
