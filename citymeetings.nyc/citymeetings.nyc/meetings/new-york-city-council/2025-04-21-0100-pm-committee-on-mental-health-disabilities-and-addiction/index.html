New York City Council

Committee on Mental Health, Disabilities and Addiction

Apr 21, 2025

·

01:00 PM

2 hr 3 min

Summary

A hearing on evaluating access to healthcare for patients with disabilities in New York City.

Discussions focused on:

  • Physical accessibility issues in healthcare facilities
  • Communication barriers
  • Provider training
  • Transportation challenges
  • The need for inclusive decision-making processes

Specific topics included accessible medical equipment, ASL interpretation, challenges for plus-size patients, mental health needs, and culturally competent care for immigrant communities.

Features testimony from the Mayor's Office for People with Disabilities (MOPD), NYC Health + Hospitals, Department of Health and Mental Hygiene (DOHMH), and the public.
