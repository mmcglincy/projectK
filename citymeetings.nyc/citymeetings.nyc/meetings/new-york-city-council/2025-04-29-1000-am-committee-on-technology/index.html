New York City Council

Committee on Technology

Apr 29, 2025

·

10:00 AM

3 hr 19 min

Summary

A hearing on New York City's broadband strategy and digital equity initiatives.

Discussions focused on:

  • The lack of a comprehensive long-term plan after shelving the Internet Master Plan
  • The Big Apple Connect program providing free internet to NYCHA residents
  • Debates over infrastructure investment versus leveraging existing networks
  • Proposed legislation to expand access and affordability

Features testimony from the Office of Technology and Innovation (OTI), Department of Citywide Administrative Services (DCAS), and the public.
