Testimony by Joseph Dumanov, Electronic Communications Engineer on 5G and Health Concerns

3:01:41

·

3 min

Joseph Dumanov, an electronic communications engineer with a background in medical mycology and clinical molecular biology, testified about the potential health impacts of 5G and electromagnetic radiation. He argued that many concerns about these technologies are based on misinterpreted or out-of-context information, and that the situation is not as serious as some claim.

  • Dumanov emphasized his extensive experience in reviewing scientific literature and conducting clinical environmental studies on EMF, EMR, and RF.
  • He stated that he has observed cases of cancer and malformed births related to these technologies, but believes the risks are often exaggerated.
  • The testimony was cut short due to time constraints, with Dumanov unable to fully elaborate on his points.
