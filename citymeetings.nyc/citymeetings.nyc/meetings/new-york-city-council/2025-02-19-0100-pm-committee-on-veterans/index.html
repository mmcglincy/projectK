New York City Council

Committee on Veterans

Feb 19, 2025

·

01:00 PM

2 hr 22 min

Summary

A hearing on addressing the needs of homeless veterans in New York City.

Discussions focused on:

  • Progress in reducing veteran homelessness
  • Challenges in transitioning veterans from shelters to permanent housing
  • Improving conditions at the Borden Avenue Veterans Residence
  • Strategies to better identify and serve veterans in the shelter system

Recommendations included:

  • Implementing RTM therapy for PTSD treatment
  • Establishing veterans committees on community boards
  • Improving job training opportunities
  • Addressing the needs of female homeless veterans

Features testimony from the Department of Veterans' Services (DVS), Department of Homeless Services (DHS), and the public.
