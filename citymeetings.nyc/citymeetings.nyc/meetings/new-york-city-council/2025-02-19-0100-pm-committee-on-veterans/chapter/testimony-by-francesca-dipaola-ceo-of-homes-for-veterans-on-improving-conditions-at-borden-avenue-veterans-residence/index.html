  • Highlighted the need to fix the leaking roof, which has been a problem for 15 years
  • Suggested installing a wind vent at the entrance to address temperature concerns
  • Proposed solutions for ensuring all veterans receive starter kits when moving to their own housing
  • Discussed organizing summer cookouts to engage the community and raise awareness about the shelter
