Testimony by Barbara Manny on Homelessness and Shelter System Challenges

2:47:46

·

7 min

Barbara Manny, a 56-year-old mother of two disabled sons, testifies about her family's struggle with homelessness since 2018 due to issues with their Section 8 voucher and challenges navigating the shelter system. She details a complex journey through multiple cities and countries seeking housing assistance, facing repeated denials and bureaucratic obstacles.

  • Manny's family has been homeless since June 2018, moving between shelters, cities, and even countries.
  • Her sons have faced educational disruptions and health challenges during this period of instability.
  • She reports experiencing issues with Social Security benefits, arrests, and difficulty accessing appropriate services for her disabled children.
