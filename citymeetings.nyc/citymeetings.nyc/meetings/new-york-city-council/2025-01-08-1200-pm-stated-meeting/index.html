New York City Council

Stated Meeting

Jan 08, 2025

·

12:00 PM

37 min

Summary

The Stated Meeting happens every 2 weeks (4 weeks in summer), when the council votes on and introduces new legislation.

Several council members introduced new legislation and resolutions, including bills on pay equity reporting, suicide data collection, camera enforcement of street cleaning rules, and a resolution calling for increased renewable energy targets. The meeting also featured discussions on congestion pricing and immigration policies.
