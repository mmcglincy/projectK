Testimony by Steve Barrison, Land Use Real Estate Attorney, on City of Yes for Housing Opportunity

12:20:18

·

125 sec

Steve Barrison, a land use real estate attorney with decades of experience, strongly opposes the City of Yes for Housing Opportunity proposal. He argues that the proposal is unnecessary and based on false premises, urging council members to vote against it to protect their constituents and the city.

  • Claims 5-6 million new housing units could be built without changing zoning laws
  • Asserts that affordability is the real issue, not zoning restrictions
  • Challenges the reported 81% support for the proposal, stating it's based on misleading information from certain organizations
  • Offers to work with council members to develop alternative solutions for affordable housing
