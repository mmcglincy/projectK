Council Member Robert F. Holden voices strong concerns about the proposal's one-size-fits-all approach and its potential impact on infrastructure in his district. He emphasizes the existing problems with flooding, sewers, and electrical grid in his area.

  • He highlights specific infrastructure issues in his district, including sewer backups during normal thunderstorms and a poor electrical grid
  • Holden suggests that the proposal seems to prioritize developer profits over addressing existing infrastructure problems
