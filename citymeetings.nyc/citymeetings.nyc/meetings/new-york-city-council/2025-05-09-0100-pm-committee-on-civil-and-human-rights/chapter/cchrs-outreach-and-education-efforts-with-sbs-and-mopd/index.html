CCHR's outreach and education efforts with SBS and MOPD

1:01:53

·

94 sec

JoAnn Kamuf Ward, Deputy Commissioner for Policy and External Affairs at CCHR, discusses the commission's outreach and education efforts in collaboration with SBS and MOPD. She emphasizes the strong relationships between these agencies and their joint efforts to inform businesses about changes in human rights law and accessibility requirements.

  • CCHR attends SBS task force meetings and BID meetings to share updates on human rights law changes
  • The agencies collaborate on developing fact sheets and conducting joint training sessions for small businesses
  • CCHR and SBS teams conduct neighborhood outreach to very small businesses, providing information on services and legal requirements
