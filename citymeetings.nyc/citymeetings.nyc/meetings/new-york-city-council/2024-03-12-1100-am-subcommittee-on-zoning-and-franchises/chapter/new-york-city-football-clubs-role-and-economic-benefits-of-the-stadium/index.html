Jennifer O'Sullivan, COO of New York City Football Club (NYCFC), presents the club's background and the economic benefits of the proposed stadium. She highlights the private investment and job creation potential of the project.

  • $800 million privately financed stadium, entirely union-built
  • Creation of 4,000 construction jobs and over $300 million in construction wages, salaries, and benefits
  • Commitment to local procurement and partnering with local businesses
