Affordable housing component and development team background

2:30:40

·

4 min

Frank Monterisi from Related Companies discusses the affordable housing component of the project and the development team's experience. He emphasizes the deep affordability levels and the team's commitment to the redevelopment of Willets Point.

  • All 1,400 units in Phase 2 will be developed under HPD and HDC affordable housing programs
  • Affordability levels range from less than 30% AMI to 110-120% AMI
  • Over 50% of units in Phase 1 reserved for low-income households, with 15% for formerly homeless residents
