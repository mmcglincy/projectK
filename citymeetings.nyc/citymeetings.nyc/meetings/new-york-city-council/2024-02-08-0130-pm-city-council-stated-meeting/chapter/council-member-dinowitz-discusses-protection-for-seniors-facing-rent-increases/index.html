Council Member Dinowitz Discusses Protection for Seniors Facing Rent Increases

0:33:04

·

132 sec

Council Member Dinowitz highlights the challenges facing New Yorkers in securing affordable housing, specifically addressing skyrocketing costs in the Mitchellama program. By introducing Bill intro 25a, Dinowitz proposes leveraging existing application processes to assist older adults through the senior citizen rent increase exemption program, aiming to make it easier for seniors to access rent freeze benefits and age in place with dignity.
