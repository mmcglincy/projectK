New York City Council

Subcommittee on Zoning and Franchises

Feb 24, 2025

·

11:00 AM

1 hr 23 min

Summary

A hearing on two proposals:

  • A mixed-use residential and community facility building in South Jamaica, Queens, including approximately 125 apartments with 31 permanently affordable units
  • A sidewalk cafe application by Le Dive restaurant on Canal Street in the Lower East Side.

For the Queens development, discussions focused on balancing affordable housing with neighborhood character and ensuring local resident priority for new apartments.

For the Le Dive sidewalk application hearing, discussions focused on addressing community concerns about noise, sidewalk blockage, and operating hours violations.

Features testimony from the applicants and the public.
