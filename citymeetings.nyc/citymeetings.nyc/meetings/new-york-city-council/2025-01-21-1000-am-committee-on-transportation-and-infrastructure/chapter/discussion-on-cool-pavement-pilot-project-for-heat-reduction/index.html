Discussion on cool pavement pilot project for heat reduction

1:11:15

·

3 min

Council Member Sandy Nurse and DOT First Deputy Commissioner Margaret Forgione engage in a discussion about the proposed cool pavement pilot project. Nurse seeks clarification on DOT's research and takeaways from conversations with other municipalities regarding heat reduction strategies.

  • Forgione mentions ongoing projects like porous pavement installation but acknowledges the need for further discussion on heat-specific initiatives.
  • DOT expresses support for heat reduction efforts but raises concerns about potential impacts on pavement longevity and neighboring buildings.
  • Nurse clarifies that the treatment can be applied during regular repaving and may extend asphalt life, emphasizing the pilot's purpose to study these effects.
  • DOT agrees to further discussions on implementing the pilot project.
