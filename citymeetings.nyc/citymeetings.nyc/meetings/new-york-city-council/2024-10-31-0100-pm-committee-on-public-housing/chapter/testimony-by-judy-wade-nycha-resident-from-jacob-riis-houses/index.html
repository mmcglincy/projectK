Judy Wade, a resident of Jacob Riis Houses, testifies about her concerns regarding the PACT program and its impact on NYCHA residents. She expresses frustration with the lack of transparency, potential displacement, and health issues related to living conditions in public housing.

  • Wade criticizes the TA support team and board members for not adequately supporting tenants and evading questions
  • She raises concerns about the voting process for the PACT program, calling for more transparency
  • Wade expresses fears about rent increases, potential displacement, and the conversion of public housing into condominiums
