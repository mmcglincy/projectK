AGENCY TESTIMONY

Mold remediation and ventilation improvements

1:14:41

·

94 sec

NYCHA has made progress in mold remediation and ventilation improvements across its properties. The agency has implemented new procedures and invested in infrastructure to reduce mold complaints and improve air quality.

  • NYCHA has maintained a low mold recurrence rate using the 'Mold Busters' standard procedure
  • Mold complaints have decreased from a peak of 1,500 per month in 2021 to around 600-900 per month in 2024
  • The agency invested in new roof fans and comprehensive cleaning of bathroom ventilation systems, impacting over 74,000 apartments
  • Airflow is now measured in each bathroom to ensure proper ventilation
