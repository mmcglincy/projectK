Testimony by Sadie Gilfredrick, NYCHA Resident at Jacob Riis Houses, on Living Conditions and NYCHA Issues

2:39:44

·

4 min

Sadie Gilfredrick, a NYCHA resident at Jacob Riis Houses, provides a detailed account of the challenges and hazards she faces living in public housing. She describes issues ranging from poor water quality and gas leaks to environmental health concerns and frustrations with NYCHA's management and communication.

  • Gilfredrick reports numerous infrastructure problems, including construction debris, water shutoffs, contaminated water, gas leaks, asbestos, lead, and arsenic.
  • She shares personal health issues, including a breast cancer diagnosis, which she believes may be linked to environmental factors in her housing.
  • The testimony highlights concerns about NYCHA's responsiveness to work orders, transparency in community meetings, and overall treatment of residents.
