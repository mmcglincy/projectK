PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Jessie Lee, Managing Director of Renaissance Economic Development

2:40:45

·

119 sec

Jessie Lee, Managing Director of Renaissance Economic Development, testified about their organization's work in supporting small businesses through technical assistance and microloans. She thanked the City Council for their support of various initiatives and urged for continued support, especially in light of potential federal funding cuts.

  • Highlighted the success of the Chamber on the Go initiative, which helped counsel 1,000 small businesses and provided $13 million in microloans
  • Expressed gratitude for the CDFI initiative, which supported low-income solopreneurs
  • Raised concerns about a recent White House executive order threatening the stability of the CDFI fund at the US Treasury
