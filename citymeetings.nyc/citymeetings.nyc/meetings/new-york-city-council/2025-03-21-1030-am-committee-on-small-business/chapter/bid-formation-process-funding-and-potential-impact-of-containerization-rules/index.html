BID formation process, funding, and potential impact of containerization rules

1:03:44

·

3 min

Commissioner Gross explains the BID formation process, funding sources, and addresses concerns about the potential impact of new containerization rules on BID formation.

  • BID formation requires a driven group of stakeholders in the community to lead the process
  • At least 51% of property owners in the district must support the formation of a BID
  • SBS provides bid formation grants to support staffing, planning projects, research, and implementation
  • The application for FY26 bid formation grants closes on March 30
  • New containerization rules are not currently seen as a deterrent to BID formation
  • Communities planning new BIDs can factor in containerization costs during their planning stages
