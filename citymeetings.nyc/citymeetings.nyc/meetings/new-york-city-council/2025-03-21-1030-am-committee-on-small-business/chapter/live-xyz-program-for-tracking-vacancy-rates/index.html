Live XYZ program for tracking vacancy rates

1:27:28

·

3 min

Commissioner Gross provides details on the Live XYZ program, which SBS uses to track storefront occupancy rates in New York City.

  • Live XYZ conducts quarterly reviews and walks of every street in NYC to assess commercial space occupancy.
  • The data is reflected in a technology tool accessible by over 80 city agencies.
  • Commissioner Gross expresses confidence in the accuracy of the Live XYZ data, noting increased trust from various stakeholders over time.
  • Pre-pandemic storefront vacancy citywide was approaching 11%, based on Live XYZ data from the first quarter of FY 2020.
