  • Application refinements are made to credit sweeper activities only within regulated times.
  • Weekly reviews are conducted to assess service statistics and make necessary route adjustments.
  • Additional resources, such as extra equipment, are deployed to address service gaps.
  • Planned application tweaks aim to improve schedule adherence and increase transparency for residents.
  • Operations are adjusted for efficiency, with sweepers servicing open blocks outside regulated times for expedited route completion.
