0:45:01

Council Member Keith Powers provides an overview of the Civilian Complaint Review Board (CCRB), its composition, and responsibilities. He then introduces Dr. Muhammad Khalid as the nominee from Staten Island for appointment to the CCRB.

  • The CCRB is described as an independent body that investigates complaints of police misconduct.
  • The board's composition, jurisdiction, and relationship with the police commissioner are explained.
  • Dr. Khalid is introduced as a previous CCRB member during Mayor Bloomberg's term, nominated for a term expiring July 4, 2025.
