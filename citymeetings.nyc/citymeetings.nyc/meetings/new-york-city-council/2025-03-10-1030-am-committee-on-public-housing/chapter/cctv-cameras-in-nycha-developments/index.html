CCTV cameras in NYCHA developments

2:01:33

·

158 sec

Council Member Chris Banks inquires about the installation, location, and access of CCTV cameras in NYCHA developments, particularly in senior buildings. NYCHA officials provide information on the camera program and its implementation.

  • NYCHA has over 20,000 cameras across its portfolio, including lobbies, public spaces, and grounds
  • There are ongoing CCTV projects funded by various sources, including mayoral, city council, and state assembly member funds
  • When determining camera locations, NYCHA works with property management, resident leadership, and the Office of Security and Safety
  • Resident input is taken into account, and resident leaders sign off on location plans
  • The CCTV unit has remote access to review footage from the cameras
