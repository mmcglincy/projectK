PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Daren Sealey, Associate Director of the Housing Resource Center at Red Hook Community Justice Center

2:41:37

·

103 sec

Daren Sealey, from the Red Hook Community Justice Center, testified about the challenges faced by NYCHA residents and the services provided by their Housing Resource Centers to address these issues. He emphasized the importance of safe and affordable housing and described their initiatives to support NYCHA residents.

  • The Housing Resource Centers in Harlem and Red Hook supported over 2,600 NYCHA residents last year with court navigation, tenant assistance, and connections to legal and financial resources.
  • A citywide Housing Navigator Initiative was launched to embed navigators in various NYCHA developments to assist with rental arrears, habitability concerns, and eviction prevention.
  • The Virtual Court Access Network (VCAN) helps tenants respond to eviction notices, file for repairs, and connect with court services remotely, addressing barriers to in-person court access.
