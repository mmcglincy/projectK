0:01:26

Council Member Rafael Salamanca Jr., Chair of the Committee on Land Use, opens the meeting and outlines the various items to be voted on during the session.

These items include rezonings, special permits, affordable housing projects, and landmark designations across different districts in New York City.

  • The committee will vote on modifications to land use items related to rezoning and development projects in Manhattan and Queens.
  • Several pre-considered items from the Landmark subcommittee will be voted on, including affordable housing preservation projects in Brooklyn.
  • Two Landmarks Preservation Commission designations will be considered: the Brooklyn Edison Building and 1 Wall Street Banking Room.
