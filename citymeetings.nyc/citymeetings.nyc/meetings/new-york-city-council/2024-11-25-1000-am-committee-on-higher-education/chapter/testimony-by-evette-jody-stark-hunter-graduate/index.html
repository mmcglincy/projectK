Evette Jody Stark, a Hunter graduate, testified about the need for accountability and remedies to address issues at CUNY, particularly concerning antisemitism and campus protests. She emphasized the importance of clear rules and consequences for those causing disruptions.

  • Stark advocated for removing masks on campus to improve safety and aid in identifying offenders.
  • She stressed the need for involving the criminal justice system in cases of property destruction and disruptive behavior.
  • Stark expressed concern about "brainwashed chanting" and made a controversial statement denying the existence of Palestine.
