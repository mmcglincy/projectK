New York City Council

Committee on Contracts

Mar 24, 2025

·

01:00 PM

2 hr 26 min

Summary

A hearing on the FY 2026 preliminary budget for the Mayor's Office of Contract Services (MOCS), focusing on contracting processes, payment delays to nonprofits, and procurement system improvements.

Discussions centered on MOCS' efforts to streamline procedures, implement new technology, and increase transparency, alongside persistent challenges faced by nonprofit providers in contract registration and timely payments.

The hearing also covered staffing shortages, challenges with the new Passport e-procurement system, and concerns about leadership continuity following the announced departure of MOCS Director Lisa Flores.

Features testimony from the Mayor's Office of Contract Services (MOCS) and the public, including various nonprofit organizations.
