PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Tara Klein, Deputy Director of Policy and Advocacy at United Neighborhood Houses

2:02:58

·

171 sec

Tara Klein from United Neighborhood Houses testified about the urgent need to address contracting delays and late payments for nonprofit service providers. She emphasized the severe financial strain on nonprofits due to payment delays and called for immediate action to improve the situation.

  • Klein highlighted that 13 member organizations are collectively owed $81,600,000, with some individual organizations owed up to $15 million.
  • She mentioned several proposed bills to address the issues, including ones related to speeding up payments, creating a database, setting timelines, and reimbursing interest costs.
  • While appreciating the recent COLA agreement, Klein stressed that deeper investments are needed to address long-term disinvestment in the human services sector.
