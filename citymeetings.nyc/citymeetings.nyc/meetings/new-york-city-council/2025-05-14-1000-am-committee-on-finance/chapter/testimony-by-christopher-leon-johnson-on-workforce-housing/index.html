5:09:03

·

127 sec

Christopher Leon Johnson testifies in support of workforce housing, arguing that it's a better alternative to regular affordable housing and supportive housing. He urges the committee to push for more workforce housing initiatives and funding in the budget.

  • Suggests allocating $5 million for research on workforce housing
  • Proposes including street vendors and "liberistas" in workforce housing initiatives
  • Recommends introducing a bill with Senator Kevin Parker to promote workforce housing
