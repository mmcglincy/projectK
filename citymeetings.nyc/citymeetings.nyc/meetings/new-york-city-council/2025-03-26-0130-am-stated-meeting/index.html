New York City Council

Stated Meeting

Mar 26, 2025

·

01:30 PM

1 hr 16 min

Summary

The Stated Meeting happens every 2 weeks (4 weeks in summer), when the council votes on and introduces new legislation.

Notably at this meeting, the council passes a package of bills reforming sidewalk shed regulations, aiming to improve design, lighting, and enforcement of facade repairs.

The council also approves several land use items for affordable housing developments, expands HIV/AIDS services eligibility, and establishes a sleep apnea screening pilot program. Additionally, multiple resolutions are adopted, including calls for protecting homeowners from foreclosures and improving the parole board process.
