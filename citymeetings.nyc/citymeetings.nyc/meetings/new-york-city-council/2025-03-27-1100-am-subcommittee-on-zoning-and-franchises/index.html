New York City Council

Subcommittee on Zoning and Franchises

Mar 27, 2025

·

11:00 AM

2 hr 24 min

Summary

A hearing on the Atlantic Avenue Mixed Use Plan (AAMUP), a proposed rezoning initiative for the Atlantic Avenue corridor in Brooklyn.

The plan aims to create 4,600 new homes (including 1,400 affordable units) and space for 2,800 new jobs.

Discussions focused on:

  • Increasing deeply affordable housing
  • Better utilization of public sites
  • Concerns about displacement
  • Lack of zoning incentives for light industrial and arts spaces
  • Improving safety and infrastructure on Atlantic Avenue

Council members emphasized that their support is contingent on addressing community concerns about affordability, job creation, and infrastructure improvements.

Features testimony from the Department of City Planning (DCP), Department of Housing Preservation and Development (HPD), Department of Transportation (DOT), and the public.
