Nadia Chait from CASES testified in support of the Crisis to Care investments, specifically $22 million for Intensive Mobile Treatment (IMT) teams. She shared success stories of clients who have benefited from IMT services, highlighting improvements in housing, education, and mental health treatment engagement.
