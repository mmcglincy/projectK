PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Maryam Mohammed-Miller, Director of Government Relations and Policy at Planned Parenthood of Greater New York

4:57:30

·

145 sec

Maryam Mohammed-Miller from Planned Parenthood of Greater New York (PPGNY) testifies about the organization's services and funding needs. She highlights the challenges faced by reproductive healthcare providers and requests increased funding for PPGNY's programs and services.

  • PPGNY serves thousands of New Yorkers through clinical services and over 20,000 through education and community engagement programs.
  • The organization is asking for increases to the reproductive and sexual health initiative and support for their patient navigation program.
  • Failure to provide funding could potentially impact over 7,000 New Yorkers who depend on PPGNY's services.
