New York City Council

Committee on Veterans

Jan 22, 2025

·

01:00 PM

2 hr 37 min

Summary

A hearing on the New York City Veterans Advisory Board (VAB), examining its effectiveness and exploring potential improvements.

Discussions focused on:

  • The VAB's lack of budget and resources
  • Challenges with member appointments and attendance
  • Need for better communication with veterans
  • Desire for more VAB input on appointments and policy
  • Concerns about transparency and independence from the Department of Veterans Services (DVS)
  • Recommendations to codify VAB roles
  • Veteran employment programs
  • Issues with veteran homelessness
  • The need for increased funding for veteran services in NYC overall.

Features testimony from the Department of Veterans Services (DVS), Veterans Advisory Board (VAB) members, and the public.
