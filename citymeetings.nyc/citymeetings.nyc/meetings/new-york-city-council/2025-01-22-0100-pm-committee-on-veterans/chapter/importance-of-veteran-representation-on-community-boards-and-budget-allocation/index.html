Importance of veteran representation on community boards and budget allocation

1:54:19

·

134 sec

Ryan Graham, Vice Chair of the New York City Veterans Advisory Board, discusses the importance of having veteran representation on community boards and the current budget allocation for veterans' issues in NYC. He emphasizes the need for increased veteran presence on these boards to pressure council members into taking action on veterans' issues.

  • Graham shares his experience serving on Community Board 10
  • He highlights that veterans' issues are near the bottom of the city's budget priorities
  • The potential impact of having veterans on each community board is discussed
  • Graham suggests exploring private sector alternatives, such as partnerships with the New York Mets
