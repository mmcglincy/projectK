PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Joe Bello, Former VAB Chair, on NYC Veterans Advisory Board Challenges and Recommendations

2:06:03

·

8 min

Joe Bello, former VAB Chair, testified on the challenges facing the NYC Veterans Advisory Board (VAB) and proposed recommendations for improvement. He highlighted issues with the VAB's effectiveness, transparency, and independence from the Department of Veterans Services (DVS).

  • Bello expressed concerns about the potential merger between VAB and the VA's Community Veterans Engagement Board, citing lack of communication and legal complications.
  • He proposed legislative actions including term limits for board members, codifying officer positions, designating a DVS liaison, and improving outreach efforts.
  • Bello emphasized the need for regular oversight hearings to ensure the VAB is fulfilling its mandate and serving the veterans community effectively.
