Addressing concerns of seniors refusing to sign Section 8 leases in RAD/PACT conversions

2:16:53

·

4 min

Council Member Chris Banks raises concerns about seniors refusing to sign Section 8 leases in RAD/PACT conversions and the impact on their participation in OAC programs. Representatives from NYCHA and the Department for the Aging discuss available support and the need for better awareness of this issue.

  • Legal aid is available to support residents needing clarification on lease signing
  • OAC staff can assist seniors by making proper referrals, including to legal services
  • Concern raised about seniors avoiding OACs due to fear of losing their apartments
  • Agreement on the need to pay attention to this issue and its impact on seniors' access to meals and programs
