New York City Council

Committee on Aging

Jan 28, 2025

·

01:00 PM

2 hr 53 min

Summary

A hearing on older adult centers (OACs) in NYCHA facilities, focusing on their management, maintenance, and impact on senior residents.

Discussions covered:

  • The partnership between NYC Aging and NYCHA in operating 106 OACs
  • Challenges with aging infrastructure and funding
  • Concerns about repair delays and accessibility
  • The impact of RAD/PACT conversions
  • Plans for service expansion and improvement
  • Capital investments

Council members called for better inter-agency coordination, proactive planning for the aging population, and increased investment in OACs.

Features testimony from NYC Department for the Aging, NYC Housing Authority (NYCHA), and the public.
