Testimony: Jessica Walker from Manhattan Chamber of Commerce on Supporting Intro 23 and Identifying Gaps in MWBE Professional Services Contracts

3:38:47

·

3 min

Jessica Walker, President of the Manhattan Chamber of Commerce, testifies in support of Intro 23, highlighting structural barriers faced by Minority and Women-Owned Business Enterprises (MWBEs). Walker emphasizes the transformative potential of the bill to improve MWBE access to city and state government contracts. She urges a focus on auditing agency activities and identifying gaps, particularly in professional services where MWBEs are underrepresented, advocating for continued funding of vital programs aiding MWBE certification and project success.
