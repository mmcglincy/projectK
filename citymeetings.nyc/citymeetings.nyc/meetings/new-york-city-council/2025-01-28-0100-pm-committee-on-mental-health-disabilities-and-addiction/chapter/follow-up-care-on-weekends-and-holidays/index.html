Follow-up care on weekends and holidays

1:01:55

·

143 sec

Council Member Narcisse raises concerns about follow-up care for patients on weekends and holidays when clinics are typically closed. Dr. Schatz acknowledges the importance of this issue and outlines several strategies H+H is implementing to address it.

  • Many H+H clinics offer extended hours to provide coverage outside normal business hours
  • The Virtual Express Care program offers 24/7 coverage for mental health and buprenorphine access
  • Emergency departments are encouraged to administer and prescribe medications for opioid use disorder, including on weekends and holidays
  • Settlement funds are being used to ensure there are no barriers to patients receiving necessary medications
