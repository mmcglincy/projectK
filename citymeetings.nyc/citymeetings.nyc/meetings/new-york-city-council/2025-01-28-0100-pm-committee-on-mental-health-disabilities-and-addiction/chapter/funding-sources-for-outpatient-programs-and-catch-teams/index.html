Funding sources for outpatient programs and CATCH teams

1:12:02

·

179 sec

Council Member Narcisse inquires about the funding sources for H+H's outpatient programs and Consult for Addiction Treatment and Care in Hospitals (CATCH) teams. Dr. Schatz provides information on the funding structures and staffing arrangements for these programs.

  • Outpatient programs and CATCH teams are primarily funded through revenue-generating activities and city tax levy dollars, not opioid settlement funds
  • CATCH teams are fully staffed by H+H employees
  • Most non-physician staff on CATCH teams are 100% dedicated to the program
  • Some physicians split their time between CATCH and other departments (e.g., emergency medicine)
  • Addiction Response Teams, an alternative to CATCH on the inpatient side, are fully supported by opioid settlement funds
