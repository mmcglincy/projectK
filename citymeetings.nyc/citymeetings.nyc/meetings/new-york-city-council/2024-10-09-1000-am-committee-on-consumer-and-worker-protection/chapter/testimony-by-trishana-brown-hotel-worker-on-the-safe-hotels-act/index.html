Testimony by Trishana Brown, Hotel Worker on the Safe Hotels Act

3:03:09

·

159 sec

Trishana Brown, a hotel worker, testifies in support of the Safe Hotels Act, sharing her experiences with subcontract agencies and the challenges she faced as a hotel worker. She describes issues with payment transparency, discrimination during pregnancy, and lack of support after a workplace injury.

  • Brown worked for subcontract agencies like Star Staffing and Tropical Stars, experiencing problems with payment methods and lack of documentation.
  • She faced a reduction in work hours after becoming pregnant, suggesting potential discrimination.
  • Brown recounts an incident where she was injured by a drug needle while cleaning a room, leading to medical expenses that the agency initially refused to cover.
