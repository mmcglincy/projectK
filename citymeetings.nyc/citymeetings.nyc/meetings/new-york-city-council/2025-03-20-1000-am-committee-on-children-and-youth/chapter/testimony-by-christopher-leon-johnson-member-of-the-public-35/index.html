PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Christopher Leon Johnson, Member of the Public

7:05:36

·

138 sec

Christopher Leon Johnson testifies calling for the defunding of Cure Violence nonprofits in New York City, specifically mentioning LifeCamp and Man Up Inc. He demands investigations into certain funding allocations and proposes restrictions on former public advocate employees working in the nonprofit sector.

  • Calls for defunding all Cure Violence nonprofits, including LifeCamp and Man Up Inc.
  • Demands investigation into a $1,600,000 contract given to the Flossy organization via Man Up Inc.
  • Suggests allocating money to laboristas and the street vendor project for youth OSHA trainings and job trainings.
  • Proposes a bill to prevent anyone who worked for the public advocate's office from working in the nonprofit sector for at least ten years.
