Council Members Alexa Avilés and Althea V. Stevens engage in a Q&A session with representatives from the Department of Youth and Community Development (DYCD) regarding the agency's racial equity plan and program funding. The discussion covers the DYCD's efforts to address racial inequity and the status of their preliminary racial equity plan.

  • DYCD confirms they will provide a copy of their preliminary racial equity plan once it's finalized with the mayor's office.
