PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Rebecca, Member of Lower East Side Girls Club

5:38:09

·

3 min

Rebecca, a 15-year-old member of the Lower East Side Girls Club, shares her personal experience with youth-serving nonprofits and advocates for increased funding. She highlights how these organizations have positively impacted her life, providing support, opportunities, and personal growth.

  • Rebecca emphasizes the critical role of nonprofits in supporting youth development and mental health.
  • She describes her transformation from an isolated, unmotivated student to a confident public speaker through her involvement with the Lower East Side Girls Club and Star Citywide Girls Initiative.
  • Rebecca urges the committee to increase funding for youth-serving nonprofits, stressing their importance in shaping young leaders and providing essential support for youth well-being.
