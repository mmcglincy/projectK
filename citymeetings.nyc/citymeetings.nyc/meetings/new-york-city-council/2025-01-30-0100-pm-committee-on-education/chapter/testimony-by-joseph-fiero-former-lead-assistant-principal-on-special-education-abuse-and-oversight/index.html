Testimony by Joseph Fiero, Former Lead Assistant Principal, on Special Education Abuse and Oversight

6:23:03

·

9 min

Joseph Fiero, a former lead assistant principal with extensive experience in special education, provided testimony on widespread abuse and negligence in special education programs across multiple school districts in New York City. He detailed personal accounts of physical, verbal, and sexual abuse of students with disabilities, as well as retaliation against whistleblowers like himself.

  • Fiero reported various forms of abuse, including verbal threats, physical violence, and sexual molestation of disabled students.
  • He criticized multiple city administrations for failing to address these issues despite his repeated reports.
  • Fiero called for a comprehensive city, state, and federal investigation to bring those responsible to justice.
