Special education teacher shortage and recruitment efforts

2:42:18

·

4 min

Council Member Rita Joseph inquired about the ongoing special education teacher shortage and recruitment efforts. DOE representatives provided the following information:

  • The Office of Teacher Recruitment and Quality supports teacher recruitment
  • They offer various programs, including alternative certification programs and New York City Teaching Fellows
  • Recruitment efforts include social media advertising, LinkedIn, hiring events, and university partnerships
  • The application process timeline varies depending on the candidate's certification status
  • Incentives include scholarships, tuition reimbursements, loan forgiveness, and placement assistance
  • The DOE is considering revisiting housing stipends, depending on budget constraints

Rita Joseph expressed concern about the effectiveness of current recruitment efforts and emphasized the need for more aggressive strategies to ensure no child is left without a teacher. The DOE representatives acknowledged the challenges and stated they are continually working to improve their recruitment and retention efforts.
