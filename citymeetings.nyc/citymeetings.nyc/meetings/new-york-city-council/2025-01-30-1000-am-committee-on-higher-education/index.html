New York City Council

Committee on Higher Education

Jan 30, 2025

·

10:00 AM

1 hr 54 min

Summary

A hearing on the newly independent CUNY School of Medicine, focusing on:

  • Its transition from City College to the CUNY Graduate Center
  • Its holistic admissions process
  • Efforts to address healthcare disparities and increase diversity in medicine

Discussions included:

  • Space constraints limiting enrollment
  • High rates of graduates serving in health professional shortage areas
  • Recent NIH grant acquisition
  • Student debt challenges
  • Potential expansion of pipeline programs with NYC high schools and other CUNY colleges

Features testimony from the CUNY School of Medicine and the public.
