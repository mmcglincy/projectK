Development and vendor involvement in MyCity's childcare component

1:35:17

·

4 min

Council Member Gutiérrez inquires about the development of the childcare portion of the MyCity portal, particularly focusing on vendor involvement and specific costs. Commissioner Fraser provides details on the approach and challenges in quantifying specific costs for this component.

  • The childcare portion was developed using both in-house and external vendors
  • Fraser explains that many components developed for childcare are shared across other business functions in MyCity
  • Specific spending on childcare services in fiscal year 25 is reported as $5,480
  • Fraser emphasizes the difficulty in isolating costs for the childcare component due to the integrated nature of the development process
  • He commits to providing a more detailed breakdown of hours and costs allocated specifically to the childcare component as a follow-up
