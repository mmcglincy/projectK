Testimony by Sharon Brown on Religious Freedom in Schools and Civic Engagement

1:45:29

·

3 min

Sharon Brown testified about the importance of incorporating religious freedom and practices in schools as part of civic engagement. She argued that the removal of prayer and religious elements from schools has led to increased problems such as violence and inappropriate content.

  • Brown emphasized the need for prayer, Bible study, and religious displays in schools
  • She connected the absence of religious practices in schools to issues like school shootings and the presence of "pornographic materials"
  • Brown advocated for people to engage civically by standing up for freedom of religion in educational settings
