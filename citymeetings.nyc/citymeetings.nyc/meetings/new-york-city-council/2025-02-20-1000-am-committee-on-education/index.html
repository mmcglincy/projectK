New York City Council

Committee on Education

Feb 20, 2025

·

10:00 AM

3 hr 9 min

Summary

A hearing on early childhood care center closures in New York City, focusing on the Department of Education's decision to initially close five centers and subsequent one-year extensions.

Discussions covered:

  • Transparency issues
  • Enrollment data discrepancies
  • Lease negotiations
  • Payment delays
  • The overall stability of the early childhood education system

Features testimony from the Department of Education (DOE), School Construction Authority (SCA), affected providers, and the public.
