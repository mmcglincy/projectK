PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Raymond Ponce de Leon, Veteran and Street Vendor, on Challenges Faced by Veteran Vendors and Lack of Information on Veterans' Programs

2:22:40

·

4 min

Raymond Ponce de Leon, a Vietnam veteran and street vendor, testifies about the challenges faced by veteran vendors in New York City, including difficulties with licensing, inspections, and harassment. He also expresses frustration about the lack of information on veterans' programs, particularly in East New York.

  • Ponce de Leon highlights the expensive and time-consuming process of obtaining and maintaining a food vendor license
  • He criticizes the lack of awareness about arts programs for veterans in certain neighborhoods
  • The testimony reveals issues with enforcement, including tickets given to veteran vendors and problems with non-veteran vendors leaving food carts overnight
