PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Karen Loew, Chair of Coast Guard Art Program (COGAP) Committee at Salmagundi Club

1:16:02

·

3 min

Karen Loew presented testimony about the Coast Guard Art Program (COGAP) and its role in honoring Coast Guard service members through art. She highlighted the program's collaboration with the Salmagundi Club and its efforts to increase public awareness of military service through art exhibitions.

  • COGAP, co-sponsored by Salmagundi Club and the U.S. Coast Guard since 1981, creates art to showcase Coast Guard missions and training.
  • The program organizes exhibitions at various venues, including Salmagundi Club, Federal Hall National Memorial, and Coast Guard Headquarters.
  • Loew also mentioned Salmagundi Club's support for military veterans' art exhibitions, emphasizing the importance of giving veterans a platform to be seen and heard.
