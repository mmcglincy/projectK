PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Ruben Diaz Junior, Senior Vice President of Strategic Initiatives at Montefiore Medical Center

2:58:45

·

4 min

Ruben Diaz Junior, representing Montefiore Medical Center, expresses strong support for the Bronx Metro North Area Study, particularly emphasizing the potential for creating a "wellness village" and biotech hub in Morris Park. He outlines Montefiore's vision for expanding healthcare services, research capabilities, and economic opportunities in the area.

  • Highlights the importance of upzoning near the new Morris Park Metro North Station to spur mixed-use development
  • Discusses plans for a new high-acuity hospital pavilion and the need for zoning adjustments to accommodate large-scale facility expansion
  • Emphasizes the potential for creating educational and career pipelines for Bronx residents, including a STEAM center in partnership with the Department of Education
