Testimony by Special Education Teacher on Bronx Metro North Rezoning

3:54:00

·

146 sec

A special education teacher from the Bronx provided testimony on the Bronx Metro North rezoning proposal, focusing on educational and community needs. The teacher emphasized the importance of new schools, accessibility, and support for people with disabilities in the redevelopment plans.

  • Requested 3-4 new schools in rezoned areas, including a District 75 school for students with severe disabilities
  • Advocated for mental health centers, accessible housing for people with disabilities, and allocation of housing for teachers
  • Suggested the development of STEM centers to provide youth with access to science and technology education
