New York City Council

Committee on Land Use

Feb 12, 2025

·

12:00 PM

20 min

Summary

A vote. The committee passes several land use items:

  • The Arrow Linen rezoning in Brooklyn with increased affordability and reduced height
  • The SPARC Kips Bay project for a new academic, healthcare, and life sciences campus
  • Innovation East at 455 First Avenue for a life science building
  • Landmark designation for the Jacob Day residence
  • A mixed-use building rezoning in Councilman Paladino's district
