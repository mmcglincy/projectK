1:36:20

Council Member Kristy Marmorato questions Dr. Michelle Morse about the H5N1 flu outbreak's impact on live poultry markets in her district and the suspected Ebola exposure case. Dr. Morse provides information on DOHMH's role in supporting affected workers and addresses concerns about patient privacy breaches.

  • DOHMH focuses on health concerns for workers in affected markets, while other agencies handle business-related issues.
  • Information for workers and the public about H5N1 is available on the DOHMH website.
  • Dr. Morse shares concerns about patient privacy violations in the Ebola case but clarifies that investigating the leak is outside DOHMH's jurisdiction.
