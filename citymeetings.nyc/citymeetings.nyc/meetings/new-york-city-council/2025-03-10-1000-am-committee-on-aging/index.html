New York City Council

Committee on Aging

Mar 10, 2025

·

10:00 AM

3 hr 49 min

Summary

A hearing on the Department for the Aging (DFTA)'s preliminary budget for fiscal year 2026.

Discussions focused on the proposed $124 million budget cut, its potential impact on services for older adults, and the need for increased funding to meet growing demands.

Key issues included funding for older adult centers, home-delivered meals, case management, transportation services, and capital improvements for aging facilities.

Concerns were raised about potential center closures, reduced services, and the need to prioritize aging services given demographic trends.

Features testimony from the Department for the Aging (DFTA) and the public.
