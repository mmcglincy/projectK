PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Kavita Shah, Program and Budget Director of India Home

3:36:15

·

3 min

Kavita Shah, Program and Budget Director at India Home, testified about the challenges faced by their organization in serving South Asian and Indo-Caribbean seniors. She highlighted the importance of older adult programs, the impact of budget cuts, and the unique services provided by India Home.

  • India Home offers culturally competent services including halal and vegetarian meals, case management, mental health programs, and recreational activities.
  • Challenges include expensive halal meals, housing issues for seniors, and space constraints in their programs.
  • Shah emphasized the need to view funding for seniors as an investment, given their lifetime of experience and contributions.
