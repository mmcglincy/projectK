Testimony by Sharon Brown

3:31:02

Sharon Brown testifies on various issues affecting seniors, including housing, financial support, and food security. She emphasizes the need for independent living options, increased funding, and programs to support older adults' well-being and financial stability.

  • Advocates for allocating housing for seniors without specific "senior housing" labels
  • Suggests funding senior businesses, home ownership, and investment opportunities
  • Calls for supplemental funding for rent, repairs, transportation, and food assistance
  • Proposes programs for seniors to grow their own food and increase food security
