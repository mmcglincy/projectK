New York City Council

Subcommittee on Landmarks, Public Sitings and Dispositions

Feb 05, 2025

·

11:00 AM

47 min

Summary

A hearing on two affordable housing projects: The Beacon in East Harlem and 581 Grant Avenue in East New York, Brooklyn.

The Beacon includes 281 affordable units with 37% family-sized apartments and rehabilitation of a multi-service center.

The 581 Grant Avenue project features 73 affordable units, including the first-ever 4-bedroom units in NYC affordable housing, and 8,700 sq ft of community facility space.

Discussions focused on unit mix, affordability levels, local hiring commitments, and potential for increasing housing units under new zoning regulations.

Features presentations from the Department of Housing Preservation and Development (HPD), The Community Builders, Thorobird Companies, and Bangladeshi American Community Development and Youth Services.
