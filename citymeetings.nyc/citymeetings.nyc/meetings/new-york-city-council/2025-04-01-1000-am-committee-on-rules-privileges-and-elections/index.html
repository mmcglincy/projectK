New York City Council

Committee on Rules, Privileges and Elections

Apr 01, 2025

·

10:00 AM

1 hr 34 min

Summary

A hearing on nominations for the NYC Board of Corrections, NYC Health and Hospitals Corporation board, and City Planning Commission.

Discussions focused on improving mental health services in jails, addressing homelessness and public health challenges, and housing affordability issues.

The hearing explored strategies for enhancing care in correctional facilities, expanding affordable housing options, and balancing development with community needs.

Features testimony from the nominees Dr. Lauren Stossel, Dr. Patricia Marthone, and Leah Goodridge.
