Testimony by Andrew Sokolof Diaz, Co-founder of 89th Street Tenants Unidos

0:49:54

·

125 sec

Andrew Sokolof Diaz, co-founder of 89th Street Tenants Unidos, strongly opposes the Queens Futures ULURP for a casino and hotel complex in Flushing Meadows Corona Park. He argues that the project would have negative impacts on the community, environment, and local economy while benefiting billionaire developers.

  • Warns of potential displacement, gambling addiction, and strain on infrastructure
  • Criticizes the environmental review process for ignoring socioeconomic effects
  • Questions the quality and sustainability of jobs promised by the casino project
  • Calls for modifications based on the Phoenix Meadows vision plan if the project moves forward
