PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Melissa Zavala, Member of the Public

2:32:06

·

130 sec

Melissa Zavala, a resident of Jackson Heights, Queens, expressed strong opposition to the Queen's Future/Metropolitan Park proposal. She raised concerns about environmental issues, skepticism about the project's promises, and advocated for alternative community-driven plans.

  • Highlighted the risk of sinking due to the area being a wetland
  • Expressed skepticism about the numerous promises made by the developers
  • Suggested using city funds to build a resilience-enhancing park without a casino or luxury amenities
  • Called for support of public land in Flushing Meadows Park and urged council members to vote against the proposal
