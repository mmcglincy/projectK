Testimony by Vincent Laucella, Member of the Public, on Queen's Future/Metropolitan Park proposal

2:46:48

·

93 sec

Vincent Laucella, a long-time resident of Queens, strongly supports the Queen's Future/Metropolitan Park proposal, emphasizing its potential to bring much-needed development and opportunities to the underserved area. He argues that the project will provide jobs, entertainment venues, and put New York City on the forefront of hosting major events.

  • Laucella highlights the project's ability to create construction jobs and provide opportunities for families to improve their lives.
  • He mentions that the project addresses community needs and has incorporated feedback from various stakeholders.
  • Laucella expresses gratitude to Steve Cohen and elected officials for their efforts in bringing this development to Queens.
