New York City Council

Subcommittee on Zoning and Franchises

Mar 06, 2025

·

10:00 AM

3 hr 11 min

Summary

A hearing on the Queen's Future/Metropolitan Park proposal to develop a casino complex near Citi Field in Queens.

Discussions focused on rezoning and demapping parkland to allow for a casino, hotel, entertainment venues, and new public park space.

Key issues included potential economic benefits like job creation and improved infrastructure, as well as concerns about gambling addiction, environmental impacts, and loss of public parkland.

Features testimony from Queen's Future LLC (the applicant), various community organizations, labor unions, local business owners, and members of the public.
