PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by MD Rahman, Street Vendor from Street Vendor Project

4:42:10

·

3 min

MD Rahman, a street vendor since 2010, testifies about his experiences and challenges as a food vendor in New York City. He discusses the difficulties faced by vendors, including weather conditions, security concerns, and issues with law enforcement. Rahman emphasizes the need for easier access to vending licenses and permits.

  • Rahman worked on the Brooklyn Bridge as a licensed vendor for 10 years before being removed in 2021
  • He highlights the long waiting times for obtaining vending licenses and permits
  • Rahman calls for the city to take action and provide licenses and permits to vendors who need to support their families
