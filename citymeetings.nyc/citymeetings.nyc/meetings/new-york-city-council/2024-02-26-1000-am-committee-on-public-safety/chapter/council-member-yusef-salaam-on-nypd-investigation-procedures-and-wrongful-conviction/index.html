Council Member Yusef Salaam on NYPD investigation procedures and wrongful conviction

0:01:02

·

3 min

In this chapter, Council Member Yusef Salam, chair of the committee on public safety, addresses a oversight hearing on NYPD's investigation procedures and the issue of wrongful convictions.

The remarks emphasize the importance of examining current practices, the need for transparency, accountability, and adherence to evidence-based practices within law enforcement to mitigate the risks of wrongful convictions.

The council member shares his personal connection to the issue, highlights the potential for injustices to erode public trust in the criminal justice system, and calls for meaningful reform. Salaam invites representatives from the NYPD and stakeholders to engage in the discussion and share insights on improving investigative practices and collaboration efforts.
