PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Michelle D. Winfield, District Leader of 74th Assembly District

3:04:02

·

3 min

Michelle D. Winfield, a District Leader in the 74th Assembly District, testifies in support of the proposed bill and shares insights from a young woman's experiences in the foster care system. She highlights both positive aspects of ACS work and areas for improvement based on the young woman's documented experiences.

  • Winfield references a book, "5 Feet of Water" by Amiri Molloy Anderson, which details the author's experiences in foster care from age 11 to 26.
  • The testimony covers issues such as sexual abuse, bullying, and inadequate living conditions in foster homes.
  • Winfield makes suggestions for improvement, including taking allegations seriously, providing paid tutoring, and addressing bullying by ACS staff.
