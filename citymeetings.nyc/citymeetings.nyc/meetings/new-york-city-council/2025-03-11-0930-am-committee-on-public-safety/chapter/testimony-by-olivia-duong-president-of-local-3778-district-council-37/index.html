PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Olivia Duong, President of Local 3778, District Council 37

8:21:49

·

143 sec

Olivia Duong, representing NYPD civilian professionals, urgently requests a new facility for the NYPD Police Laboratory. She emphasizes the outdated conditions of the current building and the need for a state-of-the-art facility to safely handle hazardous evidence and increased caseloads.

  • The current facility, built in 1930, is in constant disrepair and unsuitable for modern forensic work.
  • Workers need ventilated spaces to safely handle dangerous substances like fentanyl and test firearms, including ghost guns.
  • Despite facility challenges, forensic workers strive to maintain high-quality work for New York City's public safety.
