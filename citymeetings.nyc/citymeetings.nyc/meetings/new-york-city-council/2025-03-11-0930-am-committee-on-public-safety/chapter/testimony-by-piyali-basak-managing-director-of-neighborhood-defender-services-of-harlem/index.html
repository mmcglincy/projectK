PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Piyali Basak, Managing Director of Neighborhood Defender Services of Harlem

8:31:00

·

155 sec

Piyali Basak from Neighborhood Defender Services of Harlem presented testimony on the need for increased funding for public defender organizations. She highlighted the holistic and interdisciplinary approach of their services and emphasized the importance of competitive salaries to maintain quality representation.

  • Requested a 25% increase in personnel budgets to raise existing salaries
  • Asked for additional funding to support rising health insurance and occupancy costs
  • Emphasized the need for more funding to support homicide contracts
