Queens DA discusses jail population statistics and diversion program efforts

6:20:39

·

95 sec

Queens District Attorney Melinda Katz provides insights on jail population and diversion efforts:

  • Queens represents 27% of NYC's population but only 13% of the Rikers Island population
  • Emphasis on diversion programs, including pre-arrangement diversions and specialized courts (mental health, human trafficking, drug rehabilitation, veterans)
  • 32% of Queens remands are for homicide charges, 15% for firearms and dangerous weapons
  • Careful consideration of bail requests, including defendant's ability to pay
  • Focus on violent crimes for bail or remand requests
