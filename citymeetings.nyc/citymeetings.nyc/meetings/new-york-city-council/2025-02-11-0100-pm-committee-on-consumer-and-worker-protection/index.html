New York City Council

Committee on Consumer and Worker Protection

Feb 11, 2025

·

01:00 PM

56 min

Summary

A hearing on consumer financial experiences in the retail industry, focusing on three proposed bills:

  • Intro 374 to prohibit credit card minimum purchase requirements over $10
  • Intro 1049 to require gas stations to disclose pre-authorization holds
  • Intro 1097 to mandate acceptance of flexible benefit cards by certain retailers

Discussions covered enforcement of consumer protection laws, inspection strategies, complaint handling, and the potential impact of these bills on businesses and consumers, particularly low-income residents and seniors.

Features testimony from the Department of Consumer and Worker Protection (DCWP) and the public.
