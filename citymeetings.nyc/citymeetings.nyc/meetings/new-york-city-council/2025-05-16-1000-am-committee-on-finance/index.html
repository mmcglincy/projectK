New York City Council

Committee on Finance

May 16, 2025

·

10:00 AM

5 hr 5 min

Summary

A hearing on the Department of Social Services (DSS) fiscal year 2026 executive budget, covering both the Human Resources Administration (HRA) and Department of Homeless Services (DHS).

Discussions focused on:

  • Potential federal funding cuts to critical programs like SNAP and Medicaid
  • Historically under-budgeted entitlement programs
  • Implementation of prevailing wages for shelter security officers
  • Proposed changes to the CityFHEPS housing voucher program
  • Funding for food assistance programs
  • Immigrant legal services
  • Right to Counsel for tenants
  • Shelter conditions and policies
  • Support for domestic violence survivors.

Features testimony from DSS Commissioner Molly Wasow Park, HRA and DHS officials, and the public.
