Discussion on the Enhanced Client Placement Support program

1:11:01

·

4 min

Council Member Diana I. Ayala inquires about the Enhanced Client Placement Support program announced by DHS, expressing concerns about potential increased street homelessness. Commissioner Molly Wasow Park and Administrator Jocelyn Carter respond, explaining the program's intent, implementation plans, and accountability measures.

  • The program aims to build a culture of accountability without increasing unsheltered homelessness
  • It focuses on supporting clients, providers, and DHS to achieve permanent housing solutions
  • Implementation includes training, provider support, and a multi-step process before any discontinuance of shelter services
