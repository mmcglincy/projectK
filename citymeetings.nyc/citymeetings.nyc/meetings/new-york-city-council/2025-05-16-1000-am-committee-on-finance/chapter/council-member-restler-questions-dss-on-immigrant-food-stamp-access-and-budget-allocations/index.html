Council Member Restler questions DSS on immigrant food stamp access and budget allocations

1:40:47

·

179 sec

Council Member Lincoln Restler engages in a Q&A session with DSS Commissioner Molly Wasow Park and HRA Administrator Scott French. The discussion covers concerns about legal immigrants' access to food stamps, potential impacts of federal cuts on specific districts, child care voucher utilization, and City FHEPS funding allocation in the budget.

  • Restler expresses concern about legal immigrants potentially losing access to food stamps due to proposed federal legislation.
  • The commissioner is asked to provide data on the impact of potential cuts to food stamps and Medicaid in specific congressional districts.
  • Restler requests monthly updates on child care voucher utilization and advocates for including previously negotiated FHEPS funding in the adopted budget.
