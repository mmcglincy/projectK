Council Member Ari Kagan introduces legislation and addresses public safety concerns

1:29:14

·

113 sec

Council Member Ari Kagan introduces new legislation requiring the Department of Sanitation to provide adequate water supply for street sweepers. He also addresses recent tragic incidents in the city, expressing concerns about beach safety and public safety in general.

  • Introduced Introduction 1149 to ensure street sweepers operate with water to reduce dust
  • Expressed condolences for a 15-year-old boy who drowned at Coney Island beach, calling for more lifeguards
  • Mentioned another tragedy involving a 15-year-old boy killed in broad daylight, emphasizing the need for increased police presence
