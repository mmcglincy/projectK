New York City Council

Temporary Task Force on Tax Liens

Apr 30, 2025

·

10:00 AM

34 min

Summary

A hearing of the temporary task force on tax liens, focusing on reforms to NYC's enforcement of delinquent property taxes and water bills following the passage of Local Law 82 of 2024.

Discussions centered on:

  • Improving transparency, reporting, and resolution processes for properties stuck in the 1998-2002 trust
  • Addressing concerns about the negative impacts of tax lien sales on vulnerable homeowners, particularly in minority communities
  • Exploring alternatives to the current trust model

Recommendations included improving accessibility of tax exemption programs, providing more support for struggling homeowners, and addressing challenges faced by small property owners in maintaining affordable housing units.

Features testimony from the public.
