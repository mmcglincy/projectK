Challenges and improvements in accessing city incentive programs

0:22:14

·

79 sec

The discussion focused on difficulties industrial businesses face in accessing city incentive programs and steps taken to address these issues. Key points include:

  • SBS provides incentive consultations, focusing on energy cost savings programs
  • In the current fiscal year, over 143 business incentive consultations have been provided
  • SBS offers assistance with applications and information about eligibility for various incentives

  • The exact percentage of eligible businesses currently accessing SBS incentives was not immediately available

  • The responses indicate efforts to assist businesses in navigating incentive programs, but specific data on program utilization and effectiveness was limited.
