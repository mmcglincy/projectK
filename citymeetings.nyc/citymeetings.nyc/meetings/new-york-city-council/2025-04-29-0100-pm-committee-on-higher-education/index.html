New York City Council

Committee on Higher Education

Apr 29, 2025

·

01:00 PM

1 hr 54 min

Summary

A hearing on removing small financial barriers for CUNY students, focusing on three main issues: application fees, unpaid balances preventing re-enrollment, and transportation costs.

Discussions included:

  • The success of the CUNY Reconnect program for adult learners
  • The impact of unpaid balances on student enrollment
  • Proposals for free MetroCards/OmniCards for all CUNY students

The hearing emphasized the potential economic benefits of investing in these initiatives to improve student access and success.

Features testimony from CUNY officials, the Center for an Urban Future, student representatives, and the public.
