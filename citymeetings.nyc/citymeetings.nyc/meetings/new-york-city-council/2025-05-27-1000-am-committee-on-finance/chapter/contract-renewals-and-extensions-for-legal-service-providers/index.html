2:52:56

·

3 min

Council Member Avilés inquires about the status of contract renewals and extensions for legal service providers under MOIA's new model. MOIA officials provide information on the process and outcomes.

  • All current and new vendors starting July 1 have been notified
  • Two current providers did not submit proposals for extension
  • There will be up to 25 new MOIA centers and 13 extended contracts, totaling 38 centers
  • Some overlap exists between extended contracts and new providers
  • Full list of providers and locations will be published and shared with council members
