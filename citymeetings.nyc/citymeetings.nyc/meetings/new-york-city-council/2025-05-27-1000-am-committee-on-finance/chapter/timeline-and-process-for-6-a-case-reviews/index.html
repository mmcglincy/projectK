Council Member Sandy Nurse inquires about the timeline and process for reviewing 6-A cases. Deputy Commissioner Nell McCarty provides details on the review process and staffing allocation.

  • Initial review of a case can take a day or a few days
  • Full folder review can take up to two weeks
  • Review includes checking charges, open cases, and any judicial orders prohibiting early release
  • One staff member spends multiple hours per day on case reviews
