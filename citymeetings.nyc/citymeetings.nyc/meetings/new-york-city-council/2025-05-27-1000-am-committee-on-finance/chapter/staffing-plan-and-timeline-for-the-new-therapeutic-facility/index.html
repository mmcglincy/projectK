Staffing plan and timeline for the new therapeutic facility

1:02:41

·

178 sec

Council Member Sandy Nurse inquires about the staffing plan and timeline for the new therapeutic facility. Assistant Commissioner Alex Maldonado provides details on the current status and next steps.

  • DOC submitted a draft staffing plan to SCOC earlier in the year, proposing approximately 280 staff members
  • SCOC's post analysis suggested 99 posts, but this doesn't account for local regulations and collective bargaining agreements
  • DOC is working to reconcile these differences and determine the final staffing number, which is still expected to be over 200
  • DOC anticipates submitting a revised plan to SCOC within a couple of months
  • The next scheduled meeting with SCOC is expected to be the following month
