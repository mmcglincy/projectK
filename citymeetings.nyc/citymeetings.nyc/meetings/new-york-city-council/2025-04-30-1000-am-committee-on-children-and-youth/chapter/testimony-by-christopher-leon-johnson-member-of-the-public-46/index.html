Testimony by Christopher Leon Johnson, Member of the Public

4:38:10

·

123 sec

Christopher Leon Johnson provided a controversial testimony criticizing the Speaker of the New York City Council, Adrienne Adams, accusing her of weaponizing the budget and contract process for political gain. He urged nonprofits to stand against her and report her actions to the FBI.

  • Johnson claimed that late payments to nonprofits were due to Adams' attempts to secure support for her mayoral campaign.
  • He accused Adams of blackmailing nonprofits and vendors by withholding payments.
  • Johnson encouraged support for Andrew Cuomo as the next mayor instead of Adams.
