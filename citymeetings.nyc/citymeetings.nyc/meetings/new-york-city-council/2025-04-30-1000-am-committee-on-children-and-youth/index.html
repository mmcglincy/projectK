New York City Council

Committee on Children and Youth

Apr 30, 2025

·

10:00 AM

4 hr 48 min

Summary

A hearing on the city's persistent challenges in paying human service providers on time, causing severe financial strain for many nonprofits.

Discussions focused on:

  • Delays in contract registration
  • Slow invoice processing
  • Backlogs in budget modifications
  • The impact on nonprofit operations and services

The council considered several bills aimed at addressing these issues, including requiring quicker disbursement of funds after contract registration and creating a new Department of Contract Services.

Nonprofit leaders testified about the severe impacts of payment delays, including taking out high-interest loans, struggling to make payroll, and having to cut programs and staff.

Features testimony from the Mayor's Office of Contract Services (MOCS), Mayor's Office of Nonprofit Services (MONS), Department of Youth and Community Development (DYCD), Department of Social Services (DSS), Department for the Aging (DFTA), and the public.
