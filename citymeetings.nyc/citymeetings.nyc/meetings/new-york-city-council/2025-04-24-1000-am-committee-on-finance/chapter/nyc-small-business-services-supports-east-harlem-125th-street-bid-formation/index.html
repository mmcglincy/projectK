AGENCY TESTIMONY

NYC Small Business Services supports East Harlem 125th Street BID formation

0:03:34

·

4 min

Calvin Brown, Deputy Commissioner for Neighborhood Development at NYC Small Business Services, testifies in support of the East Harlem 125th Street Business Improvement District formation. He outlines SBS's commitment to neighborhood development, the BID formation process, and the specific support for this BID effort.

  • SBS has awarded over $1,800,000 in grants to incentivize new BIDs and nearly $11,000,000 to support small BIDs during the Adams administration.
  • The East Harlem 125th Street BID formation effort has achieved broad-based support, with 74% of the area's total assessed value, 50% of total properties, and 34% of commercial tenants backing the proposal.
  • SBS believes this BID will provide much-needed services and support to businesses within the proposed district.
