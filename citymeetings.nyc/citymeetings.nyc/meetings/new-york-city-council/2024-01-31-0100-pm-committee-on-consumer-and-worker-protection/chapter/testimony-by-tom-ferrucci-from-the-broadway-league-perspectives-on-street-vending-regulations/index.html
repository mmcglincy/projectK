Tom Ferrucci, representing The Broadway League, delivers a testimony on proposed street vending regulations. While expressing no position on Intros 0065 through 0072, The Broadway League opposes Intros 0074 and 0075 due to concerns about increased congestion and enforcement challenges in Midtown Manhattan, especially around the Times Square area.
