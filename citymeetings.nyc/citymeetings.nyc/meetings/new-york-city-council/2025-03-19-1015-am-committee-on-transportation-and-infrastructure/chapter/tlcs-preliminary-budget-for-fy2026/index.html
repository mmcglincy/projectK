AGENCY TESTIMONY

TLC's preliminary budget for FY2026

4:12:03

·

114 sec

Commissioner Do presents an overview of TLC's preliminary budget for fiscal year 2026, highlighting key figures and changes from the previous year.

  • The projected budget is approximately $58 million, with $45 million for personnel services and $13 million for other than personal services.
  • This represents a decrease of nearly $2.3 million or 3.8% from the previous year.
  • The decrease is due to the program to eliminate the gap savings initiative, resulting from less than planned contractual spending and vacancy reductions.
  • TLC's authorized headcount for FY2026 is 546, a decrease of 9 from FY2025 due to reduction of unassigned heads.
  • The agency is working with OMB and Mayor Adams to meet staffing needs by backfilling critical positions.
