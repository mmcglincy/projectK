Testimony by Sharon Brown, Member of the Public

6:16:13

·

121 sec

Sharon Brown, a member of the public, testified about the need for comprehensive infrastructure surveys and repairs across New York City's five boroughs. She emphasized the importance of proactive maintenance and safety measures for roads, bridges, tunnels, trains, sidewalks, and streets.

  • Advocated for regular surveys of all infrastructure to identify and fix issues before complaints arise
  • Shared a personal story about her sister's death in a pedestrian accident to highlight the importance of street safety
  • Expressed support for the continuation of safe streets initiatives
  • Briefly mentioned the need to close Rikers Island, calling it a "death trap"
