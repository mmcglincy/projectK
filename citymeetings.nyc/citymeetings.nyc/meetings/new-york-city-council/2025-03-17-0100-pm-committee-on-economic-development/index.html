New York City Council

Committee on Economic Development

Mar 17, 2025

·

01:00 PM

2 hr 28 min

Summary

A hearing on the New York City Economic Development Corporation (EDC)'s fiscal year 2026 preliminary budget.

Discussions focused on EDC's initiatives in tech, life sciences, green economy, workforce development, and waterfront projects across the city

Council members inquired about specific developments like shore power for cruise ships, the Brooklyn Marine Terminal redevelopment, and efforts to revitalize areas such as Downtown Brooklyn.

The hearing also covered EDC's capital budget, tax incentive programs, and contributions to the city's general fund.

Features testimony from the Economic Development Corporation (EDC) and the public.
