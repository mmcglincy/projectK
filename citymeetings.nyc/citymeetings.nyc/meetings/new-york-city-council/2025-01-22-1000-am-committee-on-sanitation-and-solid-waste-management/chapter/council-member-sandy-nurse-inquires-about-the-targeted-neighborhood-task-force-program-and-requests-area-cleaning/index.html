Council Member Sandy Nurse inquires about the Targeted Neighborhood Task Force program and requests area cleaning

0:44:38

·

139 sec

Council Member Sandy Nurse asks Acting Commissioner Javier Lojan about the status of a program addressing "no man's land" areas with green spaces. Lojan confirms the program, known as the Targeted Neighborhood Task Force (TNT), is still active and successful. Nurse then requests to add a specific area in her district to the program's list for regular cleaning.

  • The program involves taking photos and receiving daily and weekly updates on cleaning operations.
  • Nurse specifically mentions the area where Atlantic Avenue and North Conduit diverge as a chronic problem area.
  • Lojan agrees to look into adding the area to their permanent list for cleaning, acknowledging the challenges of maintaining cleanliness in such locations.
