Council Member Susan Zhuang raises concerns about religious accommodations for snow removal and garbage collection, particularly for communities observing Shabbat. DSNY officials explain their policies on enforcing snow removal and trash collection rules, emphasizing the need for fair application while acknowledging the challenges faced by certain religious communities.

  • DSNY states they would issue summons for uncleared snow even on religious holidays due to pedestrian safety concerns.
  • Officials discuss the possibility of changing collection days in neighborhoods with high populations of Shabbat-observant residents.
  • DSNY emphasizes that property owners are responsible for complying with rules, regardless of individual religious observances.
