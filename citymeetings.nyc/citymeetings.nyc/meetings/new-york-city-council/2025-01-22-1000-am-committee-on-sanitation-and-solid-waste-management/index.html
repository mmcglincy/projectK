New York City Council

Committee on Sanitation and Solid Waste Management

Jan 22, 2025

·

10:00 AM

52 min

Summary

A hearing on the Department of Sanitation (DSNY)'s snow preparedness for the 2025 winter season.

Discussions focused on:

  • Recent snow removal efforts
  • New technologies like Blade Runner 2.0 tracking system
  • Specialized bike lane clearing vehicles
  • The concept of "snow equity" for citywide service
  • Salt and brine inventory management
  • Sidewalk clearing enforcement
  • Collaboration with other agencies.

Council members expressed general satisfaction with recent efforts while raising concerns about specific issues like enforcement and assistance for seniors.

Features testimony from the Department of Sanitation (DSNY).
