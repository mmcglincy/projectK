PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Lisa Rivera, President and CEO of New York Legal Assistance Group

4:35:33

·

147 sec

Lisa Rivera, President and CEO of the New York Legal Assistance Group (NYLAG), testified about the growing need for legal assistance services and the challenges faced by their clients in accessing benefits and navigating city agencies. She highlighted persistent issues with the Department of Social Services (DSS) and Human Resources Administration (HRA), including system failures, processing delays, and wrongful denials.

  • Rivera emphasized the interconnected nature of their clients' legal needs, spanning areas such as shelter, benefits, healthcare, and housing access.
  • She called for increased funding for various legal service initiatives, including support for no-income New Yorkers, veterans, immigrants, and low-wage workers.
  • NYLAG's testimony underscored the urgent need for improvements in staffing and training at HRA and DSS to better serve vulnerable New Yorkers.
