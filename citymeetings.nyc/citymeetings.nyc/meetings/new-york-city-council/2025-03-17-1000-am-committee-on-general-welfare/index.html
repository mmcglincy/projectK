New York City Council

Committee on General Welfare

Mar 17, 2025

·

10:00 AM

5 hr 51 min

Summary

A hearing on the fiscal year 2026 preliminary budget for the Department of Social Services (DSS), which includes the Human Resources Administration (HRA) and Department of Homeless Services (DHS).

Discussions focused on:

  • Potential federal funding cuts to social safety net programs
  • Delayed contract payments to nonprofit providers
  • Funding for rental assistance and food security programs
  • Expansion of the Fair Fares transit discount program
  • Strategies to address street homelessness
  • Improving benefit application processing times

Features testimony from DSS officials, including Commissioner Molly Wasow Park, and the public.
