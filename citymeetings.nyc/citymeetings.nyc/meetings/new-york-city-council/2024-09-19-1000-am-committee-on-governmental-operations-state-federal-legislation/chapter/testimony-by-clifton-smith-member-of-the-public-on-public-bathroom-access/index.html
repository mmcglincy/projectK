PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Clifton Smith, Member of the Public, on Public Bathroom Access

2:08:06

·

3 min

Clifton Smith testified about the urgent need for more public bathrooms in New York City, sharing personal experiences of himself and others struggling to find restrooms. He highlighted the challenges faced by those with medical conditions or disabilities, and the impact on daily life and dignity.

  • Smith described incidents where he and his housemate had accidents due to lack of bathroom access, emphasizing the embarrassment and inconvenience caused.
  • He mentioned the limited availability of public restrooms, especially at night, forcing him to hold his bladder during long commutes.
  • Smith also noted that he was advised to avoid coffee and soda to reduce bathroom needs, illustrating how lack of facilities affects personal choices.
