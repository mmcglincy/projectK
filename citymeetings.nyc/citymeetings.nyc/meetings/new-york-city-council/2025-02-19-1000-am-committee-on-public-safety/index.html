New York City Council

Committee on Public Safety

Feb 19, 2025

·

10:00 AM

4 hr 12 min

Summary

A hearing on the NYPD's implementation of the Public Oversight of Surveillance Technology (POST) Act.

Discussions focused on:

  • Concerns about transparency
  • Incomplete disclosures of surveillance technologies
  • Data sharing practices
  • Potential discriminatory impacts

The hearing also covered proposed bills to enhance oversight, including requirements for more detailed impact and use policies and mandated audits of facial recognition technology.

Debates arose over the NYPD's use of facial recognition, predictive policing, and data sharing with federal agencies.

Features testimony from the Department of Investigation, NYPD officials, civil liberties organizations, and the public.
