New York City Council

Subcommittee on Zoning and Franchises

May 20, 2025

·

11:00 AM

2 hr 28 min

Summary

A hearing on the One45 for Harlem development proposal, a mixed-use project in Central Harlem requiring rezoning approvals.

Discussions focused on affordable housing levels, community benefits, building height, environmental impacts, and local hiring commitments.

The proposal includes 968 apartments (291 affordable), retail space, a community center, and a rooftop banquet hall.

Key issues included increasing affordable units, ensuring affordability matches local incomes, adding more family-sized units, and addressing gentrification concerns.

Features testimony from the developer, Bruce Teitelbaum, and the public, with diverse opinions on the project's potential impact on the community.
