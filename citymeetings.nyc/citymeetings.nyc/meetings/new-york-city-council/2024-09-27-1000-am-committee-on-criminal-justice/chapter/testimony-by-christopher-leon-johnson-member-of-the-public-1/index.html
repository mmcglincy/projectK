Testimony by Christopher Leon Johnson, Member of the Public

3:45:50

·

170 sec

Christopher Leon Johnson testifies on Intro 412, expressing concerns about the bill's effectiveness and the Department of Correction's (DOC) practices. He criticizes the DOC and COBA (Correction Officers' Benevolent Association) for potentially opposing the bill and not properly reporting incidents.

  • Johnson argues that Intro 412 is a "weak bill" and suggests that DOC and COBA will oppose it due to their alleged failure to perform their duties.
  • He expresses concern about suicide reporting and transparency in the correctional system.
  • The testimony veers into political discussions about future elections and potential candidates, which the council members attempt to redirect back to policy matters.
