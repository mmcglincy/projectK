QUESTION

What are the reasons for underutilization of the NYC Department of Probation’s programs and what steps are being taken to improve engagement?

0:34:48

·

173 sec

The NYC Department of Probation identifies lack of engagement and compliance as major issues in program utilization, introducing virtual reality as a solution.

  • Participants' low compliance and engagement levels are identified as key reasons for program underutilization.
  • To improve engagement, the department plans to introduce a virtual reality program called 'Options', which is engaging and customizable.
  • The 'Options' program aims to stimulate participant decision-making through scenario-based interactions.
  • Despite being down seven credible messengers, the department assures that these roles are not being replaced but rather integrated with the new technology.
  • The virtual reality systems are expected to be operational within the next 30 to 60 days, funded by existing resources.
