Lynelle Maginley-Liddie, Commissioner of the New York City Department of Correction on Comprehensive Plans and Budget for Fiscal Year 2025

2:01:55

·

15 min

Commissioner Lynelle Maginley-Liddie presents the New York City Department of Correction's preliminary budget for fiscal year 2025, emphasizing reform and a humane approach.

  • Discusses agency's vision and leadership team's role in future planning.
  • Highlights challenges such as outdated facilities, a rising inmate population, high workforce attrition, and the need for enhanced inmate and staff support.
  • Outlines strategies for addressing these challenges, including a focus on basic needs, conflict resolution, enhanced staff training, and expanded inmate programming.
  • Details fiscal strategies to reduce the Department's budget through savings on personnel, overtime reductions, and collective bargaining adjustments.
  • Stresses the importance of partnerships with city council, federal monitors, and community organizations to implement reforms and improve conditions.
