Robert Press criticizes City Planning for failing to secure community board approval for its housing and rezoning proposals in NYC.

  • Press points out that City Planning did not obtain majority approval from community boards and did not seek further feedback after making revisions to its proposals.
  • Press mentions City Planning Chair Dan Geronik and Bronx City Planning representative Paul Phillips, highlighting their premature declarations of success on housing and rezoning plans in the Bronx.
  • Press urges the City Council to postpone decisions until City Planning obtains community board consensus on the revised proposals, emphasizing the importance of a proper approval process.
  • The testimony stresses the risk of proceeding without adequate community input, with Press advocating for getting it right the first time.
