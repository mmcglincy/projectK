QUESTION

How much does NYC Aging spend on campaigns aimed at immigrant older adults?

1:54:28

·

121 sec

The New York City Department for the Aging invests $2,000,000 in marketing and outreach for immigrant older adults, ensuring multi-language campaigns and provider engagement.

  • The Department adds $2,000,000 to providers’ budgets for marketing and outreach aimed at immigrant older adults.
  • A requirement for providers to conduct outreach is part of the Department's strategy, with program officers assessing these efforts.
  • Multilingual campaigns, including the "Join Us" initiative, are conducted in English, Spanish, Cantonese, Mandarin, and Russian across various media platforms.
  • On-the-ground outreach efforts are also carried out by providers to directly engage with immigrant communities.
