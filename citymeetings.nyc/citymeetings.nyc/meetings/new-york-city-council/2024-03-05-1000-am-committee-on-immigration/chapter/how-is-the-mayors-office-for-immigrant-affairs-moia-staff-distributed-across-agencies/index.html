QUESTION

How is the Mayor's Office for Immigrant Affairs (MOIA) staff distributed across agencies?

0:29:46

·

5 min

The Mayor's Office for Immigrant Affairs (MOIA) staff is distributed across three agencies, amounting to a total of 61 positions.

  • MOIA's budget encompasses positions within the mayor's office, the Department of Social Services (DSS), the Department of Citywide Administrative Services (DCAS), and a partnership with RF Quni.
  • The breakdown includes 6 positions under the mayor's office, 11 under Department of Social Services (DSS), 43 under Department of Citywide Administrative Services (DCAS), and 1 under Research Foundation CUNY (RF CUNY).
  • Council Member Avilés inquires about the specific roles of the 6 MOIA staff under the mayor's office's direct purview.
  • The positions are disclosed, including the commissioner, chief of staff, deputy commissioner of External Affairs, and director of communications among others.
