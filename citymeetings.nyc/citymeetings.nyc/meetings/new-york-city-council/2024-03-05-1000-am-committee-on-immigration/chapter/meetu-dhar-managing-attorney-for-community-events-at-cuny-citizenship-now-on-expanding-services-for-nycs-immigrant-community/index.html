Meetu Dhar, Managing Attorney for Community Events at CUNY Citizenship Now on Expanding Services for NYC's Immigrant Community

3:26:39

·

137 sec

Meetu Dhar, highlights CUNY Citizenship Now's efforts to expand legal services for immigrants in New York City in response to changing policies and increased demand.

  • CUNY Citizenship Now provides assistance with citizenship applications, lawful permanent resident status adjustments, family petitions, Temporary Protective Status (TPS), and Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals (DACA) renewals.
  • The organization aims to educate the immigrant community about immigration services and policy changes through various platforms and events, offering an alternative to unethical legal service providers.
  • Dhar requests a budget increase to extend services to recent migrants, including asylum screenings, employment authorization applications, and orientations on immigration and court procedures.
  • Citing upcoming immigration filing fee increases and higher demand during an election year, the need for expanded services is urgent.
  • Expanded funding will ensure continuous access to free, high-quality, and confidential legal services for all New York City immigrants.
