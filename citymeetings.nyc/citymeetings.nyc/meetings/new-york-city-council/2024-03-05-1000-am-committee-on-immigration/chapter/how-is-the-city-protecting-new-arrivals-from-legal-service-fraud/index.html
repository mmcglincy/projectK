QUESTION

How is the city protecting new arrivals from legal service fraud?

3:18:26

·

3 min

The city is actively combating legal service fraud targeting new arrivals by collaborating with the Department of Consumer Worker Protections (DCWP) and establishing a hotline for outreach and information.

  • A primary concern highlighted by the Ecuadorian community is the misinformation spread as discussions about Temporary Protected Status (TPS) emerge.
  • The Mayor's Office for Immigrant Affairs (MOIA) partners with DCWP to oversee programs that fight against immigration and legal services fraud.
  • MOIA utilizes outreach staff to educate communities and has set up a hotline for those who cannot access information through other means.
  • Commissioner Manuel Castro indicates that combating fraud is crucial due to the vulnerability of these communities to exploitation by fraudulent actors.
