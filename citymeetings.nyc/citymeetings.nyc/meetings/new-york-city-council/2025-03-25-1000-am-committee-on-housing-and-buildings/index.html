New York City Council

Committee on Housing and Buildings

Mar 25, 2025

·

10:00 AM

6 hr 25 min

Summary

A hearing on the preliminary budget for the Department of Housing Preservation and Development (HPD) and the Department of Buildings (DOB).

Discussions focused on housing preservation, enforcement actions against negligent landlords, construction safety, implementation of Local Law 97 on emissions reductions, and efforts to address quality of life issues like sidewalk sheds.

Council members raised concerns about increasing housing violations, lack of specific preservation goals, delays in discretionary contracts, and staffing challenges.

Features testimony from HPD, DOB, and the public.
