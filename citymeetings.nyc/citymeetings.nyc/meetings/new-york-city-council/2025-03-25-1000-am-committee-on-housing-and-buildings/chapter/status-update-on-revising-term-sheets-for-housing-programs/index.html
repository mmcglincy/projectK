Status update on revising term sheets for housing programs

1:32:38

·

3 min

Council Member Sanchez inquires about the progress of updating term sheets for new construction and preservation programs, some of which haven't been updated since 2018. Acting Commissioner Tigani explains the challenges and progress in this area.

  • Tigani cites changing funding availability, construction and lending trends as factors affecting updates
  • HPD is working to incorporate new policy ideas and sustainability requirements into term sheets
  • Roundtable discussions with various stakeholders have been conducted to ensure effectiveness
  • Tigani commits to releasing updated term sheets by the end of the fiscal year
  • Emphasis on the importance of term sheets for predictability and consistency in the industry
