New York City Council

Subcommittee on Zoning and Franchises

Apr 08, 2025

·

11:00 AM

22 min

Summary

A hearing on two zoning proposals:

  • Dispositions of city-owned land related to the Atlantic Avenue Mixed Use Plan (AAMUP) in Brooklyn
  • The 2510 Coney Island Avenue rezoning proposal in Sheepshead Bay, Brooklyn

Discussions focused on affordable housing development for AAMUP and revisions to the Coney Island Avenue proposal, including reduced building height, increased parking, and addressing community concerns.

Features testimony from the Housing Preservation and Development (HPD) and the applicant team.
