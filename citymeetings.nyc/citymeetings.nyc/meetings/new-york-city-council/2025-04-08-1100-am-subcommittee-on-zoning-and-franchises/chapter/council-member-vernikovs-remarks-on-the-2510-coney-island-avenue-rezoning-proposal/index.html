Council Member Vernikov's remarks on the 2510 Coney Island Avenue rezoning proposal

0:08:56

·

87 sec

Council Member Inna Vernikov addresses the subcommittee regarding the 2510 Coney Island Avenue rezoning proposal in her district. She outlines the initial concerns about the project, including its height and parking issues, and explains how negotiations have led to significant changes that address these concerns.

  • The proposal has been revised from its original form to a four-story building
  • A restrictive declaration will be implemented
  • One-to-one parking will be provided, addressing traffic and parking concerns
  • Vernikov acknowledges various individuals and groups who contributed to the negotiation process
