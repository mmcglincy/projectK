CORE's community engagement and organizing efforts

2:16:08

·

4 min

CORE's organizing and engagement team focuses on ensuring community needs and campaigns for change are heard and supported. The team has conducted extensive outreach and plans to expand its efforts in the coming fiscal year.

  • Reached over 4,000 people and 40 organizations across New York City
  • Planning to relaunch hyper-local community conversations on equity priorities
  • Will expand engagement work to include truth, healing, reconciliation, and reparations initiatives
  • Developing curriculum and workshops to increase community participation in racial equity campaigns
