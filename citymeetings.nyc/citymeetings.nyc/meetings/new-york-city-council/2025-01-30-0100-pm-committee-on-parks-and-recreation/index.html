New York City Council

Committee on Parks and Recreation

Jan 30, 2025

·

01:00 PM

2 hr 27 min

Summary

A hearing on preventing brush fires in NYC parks, addressing the increasing threat due to recent drought conditions and climate change impacts.

Discussions focused on the challenges in fighting these fires, including difficult terrain and lack of water access in some areas.

The hearing explored proposed legislation for a wildfire mitigation plan and tree maintenance prioritization criteria. Debates centered on the need for increased funding, staffing, specialized equipment, and training to better prevent and respond to brush fires.

Features testimony from the Fire Department of New York (FDNY), NYC Parks Department, environmental groups, park advocates, and the public.
