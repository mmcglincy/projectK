PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Daniel Abram, Director of Policy at New Yorkers for Parks, on Preventing Brush Fires in NYC Parks

1:43:14

·

140 sec

Daniel Abram from New Yorkers for Parks testified on the urgent need to prevent and mitigate brush fires in NYC parks. He highlighted the underfunding of the Parks Department's Natural Resources Group and its impact on fire prevention and response capabilities.

  • Advocated for $5 million in funding to rehire 76 critical staff for the Natural Resources Group
  • Urged investment of $20.5 million to hire 250 additional PEP officers and baseline 50 urban park rangers
  • Emphasized the need for increased enforcement and interagency collaboration to prevent fires and improve emergency response
