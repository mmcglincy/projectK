PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Testimony by Marshall Lee Weimer, City Forester from Manhattan Borough Forestry and NYC Parks

2:02:52

·

153 sec

Marshall Lee Weimer, a city forester with Manhattan Borough Forestry and NYC Parks, testified about the need for more funding and staff to mitigate wildfire concerns in NYC parks. He shared his experience responding to the Inwood Hills fire and emphasized the importance of addressing tree hazards and improving risk assessment practices.

  • Weimer stressed the need for higher pay and incentives to attract and retain skilled tree care professionals.
  • He supported the in-house training program for firefighters but noted that higher pay is necessary to encourage parks workers to join.
  • Weimer emphasized that without adequate staffing, even with proper risk assessment criteria, extreme risks cannot be effectively addressed.
