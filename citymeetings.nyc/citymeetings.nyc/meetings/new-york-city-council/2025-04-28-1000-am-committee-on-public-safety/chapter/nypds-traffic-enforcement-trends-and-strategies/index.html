0:26:53

·

3 min

Council Member Yusef Salaam questions the apparent decrease in traffic enforcement since the COVID-19 pandemic. NYPD representatives, including Chief Obe and Josh Levin, provide data and explanations to counter this perception, emphasizing that they are doing 'more with less.'

  • NYPD reports no decrease in enforcement, citing increased efficiency and targeted approaches
  • Traffic agents are writing more summonses per tour despite having fewer agents
  • Specific types of violations, such as those related to Vision Zero and e-bike enforcement, have seen significant increases
