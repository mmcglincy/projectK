Council member questions DOT on parking permit system and abuse prevention

1:44:27

·

3 min

Council Member Selvena N. Brooks-Powers inquires about DOT's parking permit system, focusing on measures to prevent abuse and coordination with NYPD. Deputy Commissioner Joshua Benson provides detailed responses about the department's permit issuance and monitoring practices.

  • DOT issues 30,000-40,000 active permits for citizens with disabilities and additional permits for agencies and nonprofits.
  • The department employs strict vetting processes, uses holographic seals and serial numbers on placards, and shares permit data with NYPD monthly.
  • DOT tracks misuse and can revoke permits under a three-strikes policy within an 18-month period.
  • NYPD traffic enforcement agents have real-time access to a database of valid DOT permits for verification during enforcement.
