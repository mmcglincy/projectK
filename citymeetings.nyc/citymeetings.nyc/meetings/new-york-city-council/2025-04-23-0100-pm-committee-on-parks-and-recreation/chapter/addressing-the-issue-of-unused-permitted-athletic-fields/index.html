Addressing the issue of unused permitted athletic fields

1:06:42

·

162 sec

Council Member Krishnan inquires about the frequency of unused permitted fields and the process to address this issue. The Parks Department representative explains their inspection program and the steps taken to reduce unused permitted time.

  • In 2023, out of 6,500 inspections, 1,500 times fields were found empty and unused
  • Parks Department implemented an inspection system to hold permit holders accountable
  • When fields are found unused, permit holders are notified and may face consequences if the pattern continues
  • The process of reclaiming unused time is gradual, with impacts typically seen in the following season
  • The inspection system has led to permit holders voluntarily giving back unused time
