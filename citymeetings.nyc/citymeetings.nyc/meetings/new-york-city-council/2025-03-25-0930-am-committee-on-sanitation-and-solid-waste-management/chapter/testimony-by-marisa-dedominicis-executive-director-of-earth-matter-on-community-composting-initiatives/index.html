Marisa DeDominicis, Executive Director of Earth Matter, expresses gratitude to the city council for supporting and expanding community composting initiatives, emphasizing their impact on youth engagement and environmental education.

  • Highlights the importance of giving youth a voice in environmental matters
  • Acknowledges the council's role in preserving composting infrastructure and growing partner networks
  • Emphasizes the broader impact of community composting on building owners, families, and the entire city
