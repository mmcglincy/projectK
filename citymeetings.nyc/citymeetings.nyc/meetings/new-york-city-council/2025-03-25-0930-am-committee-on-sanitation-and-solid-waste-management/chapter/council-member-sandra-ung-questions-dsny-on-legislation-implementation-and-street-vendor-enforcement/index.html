Council Member Sandra Ung questions DSNY on legislation implementation and street vendor enforcement

1:00:29

·

3 min

Council Member Sandra Ung engages in a Q&A session with DSNY officials, focusing on the implementation of Intro 97 (Local Law 67) regarding increased fines for stoop line violations and inquiring about street vendor enforcement resources. The DSNY representatives provide updates on the law's implementation and discuss the current state of street vendor enforcement in New York City.

  • DSNY confirms that Intro 97 has been implemented, including both education and fines, but will provide more specific data on violations and fines later.
  • The department reveals there are 40 uniform employees citywide dedicated to street vendor enforcement, which they currently deem sufficient.
  • DSNY agrees to provide more detailed information on enforcement activities in Queens and specifically in Council District 20.
