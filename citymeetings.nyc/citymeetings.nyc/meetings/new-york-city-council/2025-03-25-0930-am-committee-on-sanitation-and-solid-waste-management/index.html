New York City Council

Committee on Sanitation and Solid Waste Management

Mar 25, 2025

·

09:30 AM

4 hr 1 min

Summary

A hearing on the Department of Sanitation's (DSNY) fiscal year 2026 preliminary budget and solid waste management initiatives.

Discussions focused on:

  • Containerization efforts
  • Rat mitigation
  • Citywide composting programs
  • Commercial waste zones implementation
  • Concerns about the financial impact of new waste management rules on Business Improvement Districts and community organizations

The hearing also addressed staffing levels, overtime spending, and funding for community composting programs.

Features testimony from the Department of Sanitation (DSNY) and the public.
