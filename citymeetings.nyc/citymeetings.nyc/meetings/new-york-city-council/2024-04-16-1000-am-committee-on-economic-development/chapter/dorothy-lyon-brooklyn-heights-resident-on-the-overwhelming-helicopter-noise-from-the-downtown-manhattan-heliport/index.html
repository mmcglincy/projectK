Dorothy Lyon, Brooklyn Heights Resident on the Overwhelming Helicopter Noise from the Downtown Manhattan Heliport

2:56:49

·

111 sec

Dorothy Lyon, a Brooklyn Heights resident, testifies about the severe noise pollution from increased helicopter traffic at the downtown Manhattan Heliport.

  • The number of helicopters landing and taking off has dramatically increased over the past 2 years.
  • The loud helicopter noise makes it impossible for Lyon to be productive in her living room.
  • The reverberating helicopter noise is especially bad in Brooklyn Bridge Park.
  • Lyon has witnessed helicopters landing and taking off from the heliport every 4-5 minutes in the afternoon.
  • The helicopter traffic peaks from 2pm to 7pm daily and starts earlier on weekends.
  • Lyon believes most flights are commuter routes to and from airports like JFK and Newark.
  • She supports bills limiting the downtown heliport to emergency use only to reduce commuter helicopter noise.
