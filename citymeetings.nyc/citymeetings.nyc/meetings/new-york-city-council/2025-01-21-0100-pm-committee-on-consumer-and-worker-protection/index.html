New York City Council

Committee on Consumer and Worker Protection

Jan 21, 2025

·

01:00 PM

44 min

Summary

A hearing on two worker protection bills:

  • Intro 1081, which would require the Department of Consumer and Worker Protection (DCWP) to confirm receipt of Fair Workweek complaints within 30 days
  • Intro 780, which would expand paid sick leave to cover more caregiving needs

Discussions focused on:

  • Complaint resolution times
  • Notification practices for workers and employers
  • The expansion of worker protections

The hearing also covered DCWP's budget and resources, enforcement statistics, and potential modifications to the proposed legislation.

Features testimony from the Department of Consumer and Worker Protection (DCWP), worker advocacy organizations, industry representatives, and the public.
