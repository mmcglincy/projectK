Council Member Gutierrez Raises Concerns About Midwife and Doula Contract Renewals

0:57:55

·

3 min

Council Member Jennifer Gutierrez questions the commitment to midwives and doulas in light of contract renewal issues and program inconsistencies within hospitals. Gutierrez stresses the contradiction in claiming to value women's health while struggling to support those who facilitate it. Dr. Michelle Morse, representing the Department of Health, acknowledges the concern and discusses collaboration with the state for better reimbursement rates and initiatives to improve birth outcomes and equity. Morse also highlights ongoing dialogues and initiatives aimed at bolstering midwifery across the city.
