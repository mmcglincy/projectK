Testimony: Lauren Schuster, Vice President of Government Affairs at Urban Resource Institute on Domestic Violence

1:29:57

·

3 min

Lauren Schuster, Vice President of Government Affairs at Urban Resource Institute, testifies about the severe impact of domestic violence, particularly on women, in NYC. Highlighting the disproportionate effects on Black and Hispanic communities, Shuster presents data on domestic violence homicides and felony assaults. A recent tragic case is shared to exemplify these impacts. The testimony concludes with a call to action for community-led solutions, centered around equity and survivor voices, to address and prevent domestic violence.
