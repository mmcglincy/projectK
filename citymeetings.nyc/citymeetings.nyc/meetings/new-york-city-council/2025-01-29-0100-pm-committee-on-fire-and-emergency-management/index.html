New York City Council

Committee on Fire and Emergency Management

Jan 29, 2025

·

01:00 PM

45 min

Summary

A hearing on flash flooding preparedness and response in New York City, focusing on the increasing challenges posed by flash flooding events and their impact on city infrastructure and resident safety.

Discussions included:

  • The city's vulnerabilities to flash flooding
  • Emergency communication strategies
  • Post-flood recovery operations
  • The proposed legislation (Intro 807) to establish emergency shelters for flash flooding events

Council members expressed concerns about the lack of pre-designated emergency shelters and called for better inter-agency collaboration and proactive measures.

Features testimony from NYC Emergency Management (NYCEM) and the public.
