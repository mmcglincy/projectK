Andrea Bender, Vice President of External Affairs, NYC School Construction Authority (SCA) on Proposal for a New Primary School in Riverdale, Bronx

0:03:20

·

3 min

Andrea Bender, Vice President of External Affairs at the NYC School Construction Authority (SCA), presents a proposal for a new primary school in the Riverdale neighborhood of the Bronx.

  • Bender announces the site selection for the new 547-seat primary school, per the New York State Public Authorities Law.
  • The proposed school, expected to open in 2028, will feature 23 classrooms, special education rooms, District 75 classrooms, and an 11,000 square foot play area.
  • Public hearings were held by Community Education Council and Bronx Community Board 8, with all comments considered during the site selection process.
  • Bender acknowledges council member Dinowitz' support and seeks the subcommittee's favorable review of the plan.
