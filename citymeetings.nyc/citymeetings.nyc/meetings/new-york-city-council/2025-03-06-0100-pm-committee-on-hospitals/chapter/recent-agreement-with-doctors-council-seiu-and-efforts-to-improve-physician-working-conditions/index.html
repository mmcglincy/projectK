Recent agreement with Doctors Council SEIU and efforts to improve physician working conditions

1:49:37

·

7 min

Dr. Mitch Katz discusses the recent agreement with Doctors Council SEIU and ongoing efforts to improve working conditions for physicians at NYC Health + Hospitals facilities.

  • The agreement includes salary increases, bonuses, restoration of sick leave hours, and benefit contributions
  • The full out-year cost of the agreement is estimated at $180 million for the affiliated portion of the contract
  • H+H is implementing various initiatives to improve physician well-being, including wellness rooms, anonymous mental health counseling, and dedicated social workers for clinical staff
  • There's a shift in organizational culture towards recognizing the stress of medical work and supporting staff mental health beyond just offering perks like yoga classes
