Overview and statistics of the SHOW (Street Health Outreach and Wellness) program

0:22:18

·

4 min

Dr. Mitch Katz provides an overview of the SHOW program, its operations, and key statistics on its impact and reach across New York City.

  • Since 2021, SHOW has conducted 269,000 street engagements and 32,000 medical consults.
  • The program has distributed 82,000 hygiene kits, 5,000 Narcan kits, and 3,000 fentanyl test strips.
  • SHOW units are deployed in specific zip codes across the Bronx, Brooklyn, Manhattan, and Queens, targeting areas with high concentrations of homeless individuals.
  • More than half of the people visited have come for repeat engagements, indicating the program's effectiveness in building trust and providing consistent care.
