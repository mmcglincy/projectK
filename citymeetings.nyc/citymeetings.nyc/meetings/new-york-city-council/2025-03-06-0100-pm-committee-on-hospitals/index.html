New York City Council

Committee on Hospitals

Mar 06, 2025

·

01:00 PM

2 hr 41 min

Summary

A hearing on the NYC Health + Hospitals (H+H) fiscal year 2026 preliminary budget.

Discussions focused on:

  • H+H's financial performance
  • New initiatives like the Bridge to Home program for homeless individuals with mental health issues
  • Capital projects including the Rockaway Trauma Center
  • Concerns about potential federal Medicaid cuts

The hearing also addressed staffing challenges, physician working conditions, and specific hospital needs across the city.

Features testimony from NYC Health + Hospitals leadership, including CEO Dr. Mitch Katz, and the public.
