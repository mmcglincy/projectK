Testimony: Adam Armstrong Advocates for Immediate Shore Power Implementation

2:17:27

·

3 min

Adam Armstrong delivers a compelling testimony on the urgent need for immediate implementation of shore power at the Brooklyn cruise terminal. Detailing a personal timeline of the lengthy struggle against pollution from cruise ships in Red Hook, Armstrong highlights the failures and delays of the NYC Economic Development Corporation (EDC) in fulfilling shore power promises. He emphasizes the negative health and environmental impacts of the prolonged inaction and calls for New York City to mandate shore power usage for visiting ships, matching global port city standards.
