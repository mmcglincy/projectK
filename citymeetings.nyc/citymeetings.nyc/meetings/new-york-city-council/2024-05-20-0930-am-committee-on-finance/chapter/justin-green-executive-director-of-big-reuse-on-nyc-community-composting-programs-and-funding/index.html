Justin Green, Executive Director of Big Reuse, on NYC Community Composting Programs and Funding

7:48:13

·

116 sec

Justin Green, the Executive Director of Big Reuse, testifies on the importance of restoring funding for community composting programs in NYC and allowing Big Reuse to remain at their Queensbridge composting site.

  • Big Reuse previously collected food scraps from 70 drop-offs across 3 boroughs and composted 2.2 million pounds at 3 sites
  • The elimination of community composting funding led to layoffs of 16 Big Reuse staff and 115 green jobs across 7 organizations
  • Green argues for expanding, not cutting, community composting programs to empower communities in responding to climate change
  • He requests the restoration of composting funds and for the Parks Department to allow Big Reuse's Queensbridge site to remain
