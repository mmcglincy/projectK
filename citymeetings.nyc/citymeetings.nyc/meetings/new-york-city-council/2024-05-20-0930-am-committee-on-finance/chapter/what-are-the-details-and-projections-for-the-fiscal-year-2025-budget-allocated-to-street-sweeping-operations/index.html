What are the details and projections for the fiscal year 2025 budget allocated to street sweeping operations?

1:43:50

·

89 sec

The New York City Department of Sanitation plans to allocate $30.8 million for purchasing 76 new mechanical street sweepers as capital expenditure in fiscal year 2025.

  • An estimated $22.2 million is budgeted for personnel salaries, with around 280 uniformed staff assigned to street sweeping duties.
  • Maintenance and cleaning supply expenses are ordered as needed with no specific budgeted amount for fiscal 2025.
  • The street sweeping budget for fiscal 2025 is largely consistent with previous fiscal years, with fluctuations only in the number of new mechanical sweepers purchased annually based on equipment lifecycle.
