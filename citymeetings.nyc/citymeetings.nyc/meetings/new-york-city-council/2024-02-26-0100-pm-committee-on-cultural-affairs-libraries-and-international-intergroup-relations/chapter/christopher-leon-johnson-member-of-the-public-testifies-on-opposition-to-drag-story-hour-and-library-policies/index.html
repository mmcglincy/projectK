178 sec

Christopher Leon Johnson, presenting as a member of the public, voices strong opposition to Drag Story Hour, criticizing city council and library leadership for endorsing what he perceives as perverse readings to children. He argues this is funded by taxpayer dollars and accuses the council and library directors of promoting a 'woke agenda.' Johnson calls for defunding Drag Story Hour in the FY 2025 budget and expresses disapproval of council members he deems supportive of these initiatives.
