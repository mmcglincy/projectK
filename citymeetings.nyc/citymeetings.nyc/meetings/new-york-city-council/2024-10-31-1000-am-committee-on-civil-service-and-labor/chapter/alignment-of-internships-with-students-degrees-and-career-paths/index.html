Alignment of internships with students' degrees and career paths

0:51:50

·

81 sec

Council Member Eric Dinowitz discusses the importance of aligning internships with students' degrees and career paths. Lauren Andersen from CUNY provides information on their efforts to ensure proper alignment.

  • CUNY is working to ensure internships are aligned with students' career aspirations and degrees
  • The 311 internship program was an exception, not the norm, in terms of misalignment
  • CUNY has generally done extensive work with agencies to identify internships and role titles with clear progression into degree-aligned roles
  • Council Member Dinowitz agrees that while 311 work is valuable, it's important for college internships to align with students' specific purposes and goals
  • CUNY's overall strategy focuses on creating internship opportunities that serve as launch pads for careers related to students' fields of study
