Testimony by Youth Leader from Make the Road New York

6:02:16

·

158 sec

A senior at Brooklyn School for Math and Research and youth leader at Make the Road New York Student Success Center, testifies about the importance of Student Success Centers (SSCs) in supporting underfunded schools and providing crucial resources for students. He urges the city council to ensure continued and expanded funding for SSCs to support students' futures.

  • He highlights the disparity in funding between schools in New York City, emphasizing how SSCs help bridge the gap in underserved areas like Bushwick campus.
  • He shares personal experiences of how working at the SSC has helped him develop skills and provide peer support for college applications and financial aid processes.
  • The testimony underscores the role of SSCs in offering academic support, professional development, and wellness services to diverse student populations.
